# Illustration Prompt Guidelines for Kids Bible Project

## Overview
This document provides guidelines for creating AI illustration prompts that will generate consistent, child-friendly biblical illustrations for ages 5-12.

## Illustration Style Guidelines

### Core Style Principles
- **Children's Book Illustration**: Warm, friendly, approachable style
- **Color Palette**: Bright, cheerful colors with good contrast
- **Character Design**: Simple, expressive, diverse characters
- **Emotional Tone**: Generally positive, reassuring, and hopeful
- **Age Appropriateness**: Suitable for ages 5-12, no scary or violent imagery

### Visual Style Reference
- **Art Style**: Modern children's book illustration
- **Line Quality**: Clean, soft edges, no harsh lines
- **Texture**: Soft, warm textures, nothing sharp or aggressive
- **Composition**: Clear focal points, uncluttered scenes
- **Lighting**: Warm, natural lighting, no dramatic shadows

## AI Prompt Structure

### Standard Prompt Template
```markdown
<!-- Illustration Prompt -->
**Scene**: [Brief, clear description of the scene]
**Style**: Children's book illustration, colorful, friendly, warm lighting
**Characters**: [List of characters with descriptions, clothing, expressions]
**Setting**: [Time period, location, atmosphere, background elements]
**Action**: [What's happening in the scene, movement, interactions]
**Emotional Tone**: [Happy, peaceful, wonder, courage, etc.]
**Age Appropriateness**: Suitable for ages 5-12, gentle and reassuring
**Composition**: [Framing, perspective, focal points]
**Color Palette**: [Specific color suggestions if needed]
**Details**: [Important details to include, things to avoid]
<!-- End Illustration Prompt -->
```

### Detailed Prompt Examples

```markdown
<!-- Illustration Prompt -->
**Scene**: Noah's ark floating on peaceful waters with animals visible
**Style**: Children's book illustration, colorful, friendly, warm lighting

```

#### Example 2: David and Goliath
```markdown
<!-- Illustration Prompt -->
**Scene**: Young David facing giant Goliath with courage
**Style**: Children's book illustration, colorful, friendly, warm lighting
**Characters**: David (small but brave, with sling), Goliath (tall but not scary)

```

## Character Design Guidelines

### Biblical Characters
- **Noah**: Kind elderly man with white beard, simple robes
- **Abraham**: Wise middle-aged man, warm smile, traveler's clothes
- **Sarah**: Gentle woman, caring expression, modest dress
- **Moses**: Leadership presence, shepherd's staff, humble but confident
- **David**: Young shepherd boy, curly hair, brave expression
- **Jesus**: Warm, gentle presence, approachable, loving expression
- **Mary**: Kind, loving mother expression, modest dress

### Children Characters
- **Proportions**: Larger heads, smaller bodies (child-like proportions)
- **Expressions**: Clear emotions, exaggerated slightly for clarity
- **Clothing**: Simple, modest, time-appropriate but child-friendly
- **Diversity**: Include various ethnicities and appearances

### Animal Characters
- **Style**: Friendly, not realistic/scary
- **Expressions**: Human-like emotions for storytelling
- **Behavior**: Calm, peaceful, cooperative
- **Details**: Soft features, rounded edges, no aggressive poses

## Scene Composition Guidelines

### Focal Points
- **Main Character**: Should be clearly visible and recognizable
- **Action**: The key moment of the story should be central
- **Emotion**: Facial expressions should convey the story's emotion
- **Context**: Background should support but not overwhelm

### Framing Guidelines
- **Close-ups**: For emotional moments and character expressions
- **Medium shots**: For interactions between characters
- **Wide shots**: For establishing scenes and large-scale events
- **Eye Level**: Generally at child's eye level for relatability

### Background Elements
- **Simplicity**: Uncluttered, supports main action
- **Context**: Provides setting without distraction
- **Color**: Complementary to main subjects
- **Details**: Enough to be interesting but not overwhelming

## Age-Specific Considerations

### Ages 5-7
- **Simple Scenes**: One main action per illustration
- **Bright Colors**: High contrast, cheerful palette
- **Clear Characters**: Large, expressive faces
- **Familiar Elements**: Objects children recognize
- **Positive Focus**: Happy, reassuring imagery

### Ages 8-10
- **More Detail**: Additional background elements
- **Complex Scenes**: Multiple interactions possible
- **Story Context**: More narrative elements
- **Emotional Range**: Wider emotional expressions
- **Action Sequences**: More dynamic compositions

### Ages 11-12
- **Realistic Style**: More detailed illustrations
- **Complex Narratives**: Multiple story elements
- **Cultural Context**: Historical and cultural details
- **Abstract Concepts**: Symbolic elements possible
- **Mature Themes**: Handled sensitively but appropriately

## Content-Specific Guidelines

### Creation Stories
- **Wonder and Awe**: Magical, beautiful creation scenes
- **God's Presence**: Represented by light, not human form
- **Nature**: Vibrant, diverse, beautiful
- **Order**: Show progression from chaos to order

### Flood Stories
- **Safety Focus**: Ark as place of safety
- **Animal Care**: Noah caring for animals
- **Hope**: Rainbow and new beginnings
- **Family**: Noah's family together and safe

### Battle Stories
- **Courage Focus**: Bravery and faith over violence
- **Protection**: God's protection emphasized
- **Victory**: Positive outcomes, not graphic details
- **Peace**: Resolution and restoration

### Miracle Stories
- **Wonder**: Magical, awe-inspiring elements
- **Jesus' Compassion**: Focus on healing and helping
- **Transformation**: Before and after elements
- **Joy**: Happy reactions and celebrations

## Technical Specifications

### Image Format
- **Resolution**: High resolution for print and digital
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 for web, 4:3 for print
- **File Format**: PNG for transparency, JPG for photos
- **Color Space**: RGB for digital, CMYK for print

### AI Generation Parameters
- **Style Weight**: Strong emphasis on children's book style
- **Content Filter**: Strict family-friendly filters
- **Consistency**: Use same parameters for series consistency
- **Quality**: High-quality generation settings

## Quality Control

### Review Checklist
- [ ] Is the style consistent with other illustrations?
- [ ] Are characters age-appropriate and friendly?
- [ ] Is the emotional tone suitable for children?
- [ ] Does it accurately represent the biblical story?
- [ ] Are there any scary or inappropriate elements?
- [ ] Is the composition clear and engaging?
- [ ] Are colors bright and cheerful?

### Testing Process
1. **Child Review**: Show to children in target age group
2. **Parent Review**: Get parent feedback on appropriateness
3. **Expert Review**: Biblical and educational expert review
4. **Consistency Check**: Ensure style matches other illustrations

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### Visual Issues
- **Scary Imagery**: Avoid frightening characters or scenes
- **Violence**: No graphic or violent content
- **Cultural Insensitivity**: Respect historical and cultural context
- **Inconsistent Style**: Maintain visual consistency across stories

### Content Issues
- **Theological Errors**: Ensure biblical accuracy
- **Age Inappropriateness**: Content suitable for target age
- **Cultural Misrepresentation**: Respect diversity and accuracy
- **Overly Complex**: Keep scenes clear and understandable
