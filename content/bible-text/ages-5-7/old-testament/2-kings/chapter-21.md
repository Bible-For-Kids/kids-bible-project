# 2 Kings 21

## Book
2 Kings

## Chapter
21

## Verses

### 2 Kings 21:1
Manasseh was twelve years old when he began to rule, and he ruled fifty-five years in Jerusalem. His mother's name was Hephzibah.

### 2 Kings 21:2
Manasseh did what was evil in the Lord's sight, following the detestable practices of the nations the Lord had driven out before the people of Israel.

### 2 Kings 21:3
He rebuilt the worship places on hills that Hezekiah his father had destroyed. He set up altars for Baal and made an Asherah idol, as Ahab king of Israel had done. He worshiped and served the sun, moon, and stars of the sky.

### 2 Kings 21:4
He built altars in the Lord's house, of which the Lord said, "I will put my name in Jerusalem."

### 2 Kings 21:5
He built altars for the sun, moon, and stars of the sky in the two courts of the Lord's house.

### 2 Kings 21:6
He burned his son as an offering, practiced forbidden magic, used forbidden signs, and dealt with people who tried to speak with spirits and with magic workers. He did much evil in the Lord's sight, making the Lord angry.

### 2 Kings 21:7
He set the carved idol of Asherah idol that he had made in the house of which the Lord said to David and to Solomon his son, "In this house, and in Jerusalem, which I have chosen out of all the tribes of Israel, I will put my name forever;

### 2 Kings 21:8
I will not cause the feet of Israel to wander any more out of the land which I gave their fathers, if only they will observe to do according to all that I have commanded them, and according to all the law that my servant Moses commanded them."

### 2 Kings 21:9
But they didn't listen, and Manasseh seduced them to do what is evil more than the nations did whom the Lord destroyed before the people of Israel.

### 2 Kings 21:10
The Lord spoke by his servants the prophets, saying,

### 2 Kings 21:11
"Because Manasseh king of Judah has done these detestable sins, and has done wickedly above all that the Amorites did, who were before him, and has also made Judah to sin with his idols;

### 2 Kings 21:12
therefore the Lord, the God of Israel, says, "Look, I am bringing disaster on Jerusalem and Judah. Everyone who hears about it will have tingling ears.

### 2 Kings 21:13
I will stretch over Jerusalem the line of Samaria, and the plummet of Ahab's house; and I will wipe Jerusalem as a man wipes a dish, wiping it and turning it upside down.

### 2 Kings 21:14
I will reject the small group left from my people, and deliver them into the hands of their enemies. They will become a prey and a plunder to all their enemies,

### 2 Kings 21:15
because they have done what is evil in my sight, and have provoked me to anger, since the day their fathers came out of Egypt, even to this day.' "

### 2 Kings 21:16
Manasseh also shed much innocent blood, until he had filled Jerusalem from one end to the other, besides the sin he made Judah commit by doing what was evil in the Lord's sight.

### 2 Kings 21:17
Now the rest of the acts of Manasseh, and all that he did, and his sin that he sinned, they are written in the history book of the kings of Judah.

### 2 Kings 21:18
Manasseh died and was buried with his fathers, and was buried in the garden of his own house, in the garden of Uzza; and Amon his son ruled in his place.

### 2 Kings 21:19
Amon was twenty-two years old when he began to rule; and he ruled two years in Jerusalem. His mother's name was Meshullemeth the daughter of Haruz of Jotbah.

### 2 Kings 21:20
He did what was evil in the Lord's sight, as Manasseh his father did.

### 2 Kings 21:21
He walked in all the ways that his father walked in, and served the idols that his father served, and worshiped them;

### 2 Kings 21:22
and he abandoned the Lord, the God of his fathers, and didn't walk in the way of the Lord.

### 2 Kings 21:23
The servants of Amon made a secret plan against him, and put the king to death in his own house.

### 2 Kings 21:24
But the people of the land killed all those who had made a secret plan against king Amon; and the people of the land made Josiah his son king in his place.

### 2 Kings 21:25
The rest of what Amon did is written in the history book of the kings of Judah.

### 2 Kings 21:26
He was buried in his tomb in the garden of Uzza, and Josiah his son ruled in his place.
