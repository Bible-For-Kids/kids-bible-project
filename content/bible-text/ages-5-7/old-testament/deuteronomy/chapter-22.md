# Deuteronomy 22

## Book
Deuteronomy

## Chapter
22

## Verses

### Deuteronomy 22:1
You must not see your brother's ox or his sheep go astray and hide yourself from them. You must surely bring them again to your brother.

### Deuteronomy 22:2
If your brother isn't near to you, or if you don't know him, then you must bring it home to your house, and it will be with you until your brother comes looking for it, and you must restore it to him.

### Deuteronomy 22:3
So you must do with his donkey. So you must do with his clothing. So you must do with every lost thing of your brother's, which he has lost and you have found. You may not hide yourself.

### Deuteronomy 22:4
You must not see your brother's donkey or his ox fallen down by the way, and hide yourself from them. You must surely help him to lift them up again.

### Deuteronomy 22:5
A woman must not wear men's clothing, neither must a man put on women's clothing; for whoever does these things is a detestable thing to the Lord your God.

### Deuteronomy 22:6
If you come across a bird's nest on the way, in any tree or on the ground, with young ones or eggs, and the hen sitting on the young, or on the eggs, you must not take the hen with the young.

### Deuteronomy 22:7
You must surely let the hen go, but the young you may take for yourself, so things may go well with you, and that you may make your days long.

### Deuteronomy 22:8
When you build a new house, then you must make a railing around your roof, so that you don't bring blood on your house if anyone falls from there.

### Deuteronomy 22:9
You must not sow your vineyard with two kinds of seed, or else all the fruit be made unclean, the seed which you have sown, and the increase of the vineyard.

### Deuteronomy 22:10
You must not plow with an ox and a donkey together.

### Deuteronomy 22:11
You must not wear clothes of wool and linen woven together.

### Deuteronomy 22:12
You must make yourselves fringes on the four corners of your cloak with which you cover yourself.

### Deuteronomy 22:13
If any man takes a wife, and has sexual relations with her, hates her,

### Deuteronomy 22:14
accuses her of shameful things, gives her a bad name, and says, "I took this woman, and when I came near to her, I didn't find in her the proof that she had not had sexual relations before marriage;"

### Deuteronomy 22:15
then the young woman's father and mother must take and bring the proof that the young woman had not had sexual relations before marriage to the elders at the city gate.

### Deuteronomy 22:16
The young woman's father must tell the elders, "I gave my daughter to this man as his wife, and he hates her.

### Deuteronomy 22:17
he has accused her of shameful things, saying, 'I didn't find in your daughter the proof that she had not had sexual relations before marriage;' and yet this is the proof that my daughter had not had sexual relations before marriage." They must spread the cloth before the elders of the city.

### Deuteronomy 22:18
The elders of that city must take the man and discipline him.

### Deuteronomy 22:19
They must fine him one hundred silver pieces, and give them to the father of the young woman, because he has given a bad name to a young woman of Israel. She will be his wife. He may not divorce her all his days.

### Deuteronomy 22:20
But if this thing is true, that the proof was not found in the young woman,

### Deuteronomy 22:21
then they must bring out the young woman to the door of her father's house, and the men of her city must throw stones at her until she dies, because she has done a disgraceful sin in Israel, to live in sexual sin in her father's house. So you must remove the evil from among you.

### Deuteronomy 22:22
If a man is found having sexual relations with a woman married to a husband, then they must both die, the man who had sexual relations with the woman and the woman. So you must remove the evil from Israel.

### Deuteronomy 22:23
If there is a young woman who has not had sexual relations and is promised to be married to a husband, and a man finds her in the city, and has sexual relations with her,

### Deuteronomy 22:24
then you must bring them both out to the gate of that city, and you must throw stones at them until they die; the young woman, because she did not cry out for help in the city; and the man, because he violated his neighbor's wife. So you must remove the evil from among you.

### Deuteronomy 22:25
But if the man finds the young woman who is promised to be married in the field, and the man overpowers her and has sexual relations with her, then only the man who violated her must die;

### Deuteronomy 22:26
but to the young woman you must do nothing. There is in the young woman no sin worthy of death; for as when a man rises against his neighbor and kills him, even so is this matter;

### Deuteronomy 22:27
for he found her in the field, the young woman promised to be married cried, and there was no one to save her.

### Deuteronomy 22:28
If a man finds a young woman who has not had sexual relations, who is not promised to be married, takes hold of her and has sexual relations with her, and they are found,

### Deuteronomy 22:29
then the man must give the young woman's father fifty silver pieces. She will be his wife, because he has violated her. He may not divorce her all his days.

### Deuteronomy 22:30
A man must not take his father's wife or dishonor his father's marriage bed.
