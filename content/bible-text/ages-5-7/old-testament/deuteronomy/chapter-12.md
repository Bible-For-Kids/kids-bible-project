# Deuteronomy 12

## Book
Deuteronomy

## Chapter
12

## Verses

### Deuteronomy 12:1
These are the rules and the commands that you will be careful to do in the land that the Lord, the God of your ancestors, has given you to take all the days that you live on the earth.

### Deuteronomy 12:2
You must completely destroy all the places where the nations you will drive out served their gods: on the high mountains, on the hills, and under every green tree.

### Deuteronomy 12:3
Break down their altars, smash their pillars, and burn their Asherah poles with fire. Cut down the carved idols of their gods, and remove their names from that place.

### Deuteronomy 12:4
You must not do so to the Lord your God.

### Deuteronomy 12:5
Go to the place the Lord your God will choose from all your tribes as the place for his name. Seek him there, and come there.

### Deuteronomy 12:6
Bring there your burned offerings, sacrifices, tithes, special gifts, special promises, gifts people choose to bring, and the firstborn from your herd and flock.

### Deuteronomy 12:7
There you and your households must eat before the Lord your God and rejoice in all your work, because the Lord your God has blessed you.

### Deuteronomy 12:8
You must not worship the way we are doing here today, with each person doing whatever seems right to him.

### Deuteronomy 12:9
because you have not yet come to the rest and inheritance the Lord your God gives you.

### Deuteronomy 12:10
But when you go over the Jordan and live in the land that the Lord your God causes you to receive as an inheritance, and he gives you rest from all your enemies around you, so that you live in safety,

### Deuteronomy 12:11
then bring everything I command you to the place the Lord your God will choose for his name: your burned offerings, sacrifices, tithes, special gifts, and all the special promises you choose to make to the Lord.

### Deuteronomy 12:12
Rejoice before the Lord your God: you, your sons and daughters, your male and female servants, and the Levite in your towns, because he has no share of land with you.

### Deuteronomy 12:13
Be careful that you don't offer your burned offerings in every place that you see;

### Deuteronomy 12:14
but in the place that the Lord chooses in one of your tribes, there you must offer your burned offerings, and there you must do all that I command you.

### Deuteronomy 12:15
You may kill and eat meat in all your towns, as much as you desire, according to the blessing the Lord your God has given you. People not clean for worship and people who are clean may eat it, as they eat gazelle or deer.

### Deuteronomy 12:16
Only you must not eat the blood. You must pour it out on the earth like water.

### Deuteronomy 12:17
But in your towns you may not eat the tithe of your grain, new wine, oil, the firstborn of your herd or flock, any special promises you make, gifts people choose to bring, or your special gifts.

### Deuteronomy 12:18
Eat them before the Lord your God in the place the Lord your God will choose: you, your son, your daughter, your male servant, your female servant, and the Levite in your towns. Rejoice before the Lord your God in all your work.

### Deuteronomy 12:19
Be careful that you don't forsake the Levite as long as you live in your land.

### Deuteronomy 12:20
When the Lord your God enlarges your border, as he has promised you, and you say, "I want to eat meat," because you want to eat meat, you may eat meat, as much as you desire.

### Deuteronomy 12:21
If the place the Lord your God will choose for his name is too far from you, then you may kill animals from the herd and flock the Lord has given you, as I commanded you, and eat in your towns as much as you desire.

### Deuteronomy 12:22
Eat it as gazelle or deer is eaten. People not clean for worship and people who are clean may eat it alike.

### Deuteronomy 12:23
Only be sure that you don't eat the blood; for the blood is the life. You must not eat the life with the meat.

### Deuteronomy 12:24
You must not eat it. You must pour it out on the earth like water.

### Deuteronomy 12:25
You must not eat it, so things may go well with you and with your children after you, when you do what is right in the Lord's eyes.

### Deuteronomy 12:26
Take your holy gifts and your special promises, and go to the place the Lord will choose.

### Deuteronomy 12:27
You must offer your burned offerings, the meat and the blood, on the Lord your God's altar. The blood of your sacrifices will be poured out on the Lord your God's altar, and you must eat the meat.

### Deuteronomy 12:28
Listen to and keep all these words I command you, so things may go well with you and your children after you forever, when you do what is good and right in the Lord your God's eyes.

### Deuteronomy 12:29
When the Lord your God cuts off the nations from before you where you go in to drive them out, and you drive them out and live in their land,

### Deuteronomy 12:30
be careful that you are not trapped into following them after they are destroyed before you. Do not ask about their gods, saying, "How did these nations serve their gods? I will do the same."

### Deuteronomy 12:31
You must not worship the Lord your God in their way. They have done every hateful thing the Lord hates for their gods. They even burn their sons and daughters in the fire for their gods.

### Deuteronomy 12:32
Everything I command you, be careful to do. Do not add to it or take away from it.
