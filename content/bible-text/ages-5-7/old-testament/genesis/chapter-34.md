# Genesis 34

## Book
Genesis

## Chapter
34

## Verses

### Genesis 34:1
Dinah, the daughter Leah had for Jacob, went out to visit the young women of the land.

### Genesis 34:2
Shechem, the son of Hamor the Hivite and a prince of the land, saw Dinah. He took her and treated her in a shameful and hurtful way.

### Genesis 34:3
Shechem's heart stayed close to Dinah, Jacob's daughter. He loved the young woman and spoke kindly to her.

### Genesis 34:4
Shechem spoke to his father Hamor and said, "Get this young woman for me as my wife."

### Genesis 34:5
Jacob heard that Shechem had done this shameful thing to Dinah his daughter. Jacob's sons were with his animals in the field, so Jacob stayed quiet until they came back.

### Genesis 34:6
Hamor, Shechem's father, went out to talk with Jacob.

### Genesis 34:7
Jacob's sons came in from the field when they heard what had happened. The men were hurt and very angry, because Shechem had done a foolish and wrong thing in Israel by harming Jacob's daughter. Such a thing should not be done.

### Genesis 34:8
Hamor spoke with them and said, "My son Shechem longs for your daughter. Please give her to him as his wife.

### Genesis 34:9
"Let our families marry each other. Give your daughters to us, and take our daughters for yourselves.

### Genesis 34:10
"Live with us. The land will be open to you. Live here, trade here, and own land here."

### Genesis 34:11
Shechem said to Dinah's father and brothers, "Please show me kindness. Whatever you ask of me, I will give.

### Genesis 34:12
"Ask me for as large a bride gift as you want, and I will give what you say. Only give me the young woman as my wife."

### Genesis 34:13
Jacob's sons answered Shechem and his father Hamor with deceit, because Shechem had harmed Dinah their sister.

### Genesis 34:14
They said to them, "We cannot do this. We cannot give our sister to a man who has not received the body sign called circumcision, because that would bring shame on us.

### Genesis 34:15
"We will agree only if you become like us, and every male among you receives this sign.

### Genesis 34:16
"Then we will give our daughters to you, and we will take your daughters for ourselves. We will live with you and become one people.

### Genesis 34:17
"But if you will not listen and receive this sign, then we will take our daughter and go."

### Genesis 34:18
Their words pleased Hamor and his son Shechem.

### Genesis 34:19
The young man did not wait to do what they asked, because he delighted in Jacob's daughter. He was the most honored person in his father's house.

### Genesis 34:20
Hamor and his son Shechem went to the gate of their city and spoke to the men of the city.

### Genesis 34:21
They said, "These men are peaceful with us. Let them live in the land and trade here, because the land is large enough for them. We can take their daughters as wives, and we can give them our daughters.

### Genesis 34:22
"The men will agree to live with us and become one people only if every male among us receives the same sign they have.

### Genesis 34:23
"Will not their animals, their goods, and everything they own become ours? Let us agree with them, and they will live with us."

### Genesis 34:24
All the men who went out of the city gate listened to Hamor and Shechem. Every male in the city received the sign of circumcision.

### Genesis 34:25
On the third day, while the men were still sore, two of Jacob's sons, Simeon and Levi, Dinah's brothers, took their swords. They came into the city boldly and killed all the males.

### Genesis 34:26
They killed Hamor and his son Shechem with the sword. Then they took Dinah out of Shechem's house and left.

### Genesis 34:27
Jacob's sons came upon the dead men and took things from the city, because their sister had been harmed.

### Genesis 34:28
They took the sheep, cattle, donkeys, and everything in the city and in the field.

### Genesis 34:29
They also took all the wealth, all the children, and the wives. They carried away everything that was in the houses.

### Genesis 34:30
Jacob said to Simeon and Levi, "You have brought trouble on me. You have made the people of this land, the Canaanites and the Perizzites, hate me. We are few in number. If they gather against me and attack me, I and my house will be destroyed."

### Genesis 34:31
But they said, "Should he have treated our sister like someone with no honor?"
