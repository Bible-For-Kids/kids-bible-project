# Genesis 1

## Book
Genesis

## Chapter
1

## Verses

### Genesis 1:1
At the very beginning, God made everything above us and the earth beneath us.

### Genesis 1:2
The earth God made was not shaped yet. It was empty and covered in deep darkness. Deep waters were there, and God's Spirit moved over the waters.

### Genesis 1:3
God spoke into the deep darkness that covered everything. He said, "Let there be light!" And bright light began to shine over all the darkness.

### Genesis 1:4
God looked at the light. He saw that it was good. Then God set the light apart from the darkness.

### Genesis 1:5
God named the light "Day." He named the darkness "Night." Evening came, then morning came. That was the first day.

### Genesis 1:6
Then God spoke again. He said, "Let there be a wide space between the waters. Let it separate water above from water below."

### Genesis 1:7
God made the wide space. He put some waters below it and some waters above it. And it happened just as God said.

### Genesis 1:8
God named the wide space "Sky." Evening came, then morning came. That was the second day.

### Genesis 1:9
Then God spoke to the waters under the sky. He said, "Gather together, so dry ground can appear." The waters moved together, and land came into view.

### Genesis 1:10
God named the dry ground "Land." He named the gathered waters "Seas." God looked at it all and saw that it was good.

### Genesis 1:11
Then God spoke to the land. He said, "Let the land bring out plants with seeds and fruit trees with seeds inside." And it happened just as God said.

### Genesis 1:12
The land brought out plants by God's command: seed plants and fruit trees with seeds inside. Each one was made in its own kind. God saw that it was good.

### Genesis 1:13
Evening came, then morning came. That was the third day.

### Genesis 1:14
Then God said, "Let lights shine in the sky. Let them separate day from night. Let them help mark days, seasons, and years."

### Genesis 1:15
God said, "Let those lights shine from the sky onto the earth." And that is exactly what happened.

### Genesis 1:16
God made two great lights. He made the bigger light to shine in the day. He made the smaller light to shine at night. He also made the stars.

### Genesis 1:17
God placed these lights in the sky so they would shine on the earth.

### Genesis 1:18
They would shine over the day and over the night. They would keep light and darkness apart. God saw that it was good.

### Genesis 1:19
Evening came, then morning came. That was the fourth day.

### Genesis 1:20
Then God spoke to the waters. He said, "Be full of living creatures." And he said, "Let birds fly across the sky."

### Genesis 1:21
So God created the great sea creatures. He made every living thing that swims and moves in the water. He made every kind of bird with wings. God saw that it was good.

### Genesis 1:22
God blessed them. He said, "Have many little ones. Fill the seas. Let birds grow many on the earth."

### Genesis 1:23
Evening came, then morning came. That was the fifth day.

### Genesis 1:24
Then God spoke to the land. He said, "Let the land bring out living animals. Let there be tame animals, crawling creatures, and wild animals, each in its own kind." And it happened.

### Genesis 1:25
God made the wild animals. He made the tame animals. He made all the creatures that move along the ground. Each one was made in its own kind. God saw that it was good.

### Genesis 1:26
Then God said, "Let us make people in our image, to be like us in a special way. Let them lead and care for the fish, the birds, the animals, and every creature that moves on the ground."

### Genesis 1:27
So God created people in his own image. He made them to show what he is like. He created them male and female.

### Genesis 1:28
God blessed the people. He said, "Have children and grow into many families. Fill the earth and care for it. Lead the fish, the birds, and every living creature that moves on the earth."

### Genesis 1:29
Then God said, "Look, I have given you every seed plant on the whole earth. I have given you every fruit tree with seeds inside its fruit. They will be your food."

### Genesis 1:30
God said, "I have also given green plants for food to every animal, every bird, and every creature that breathes and moves on the ground." And it was so.

### Genesis 1:31
God looked at everything he had made. It was not just good. It was very good. Evening came, then morning came. That was the sixth day.
