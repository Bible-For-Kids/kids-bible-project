# 2 Samuel 23

## Book
2 Samuel

## Chapter
23

## Verses

### 2 Samuel 23:1
Now these are the last words of David. David the son of Jesse says, the man who was raised on high says, the anointed of the God of Jacob, the sweet psalmist of Israel:

### 2 Samuel 23:2
"The Lord's Spirit spoke by me. His word was on my tongue.

### 2 Samuel 23:3
The God of Israel said, the Rock of Israel spoke to me, 'One who rules over men righteously, who rules in the fear of God,

### 2 Samuel 23:4
must be as the light of the morning, when the sun rises, a morning without clouds, when the tender grass springs out of the earth, through clear shining after rain.

### 2 Samuel 23:5
Is not my house so with God? He has made an everlasting covenant with me, ordered in all things and sure. Will he not make all my salvation and desire grow?

### 2 Samuel 23:6
But all the ungodly will be as thorns to be thrust away, because they can't be taken with the hand,

### 2 Samuel 23:7
But the man who touches them must be armed with iron and the staff of a spear. They will be utterly burned with fire in their place."

### 2 Samuel 23:8
These are the names of the brave men whom David had: Josheb Basshebeth a Tahchemonite, chief of the commanders; he was called Adino the Eznite, who killed eight hundred at one time.

### 2 Samuel 23:9
After him was Eleazar the son of Dodai the son of an Ahohite, one of the three brave men with David, when they defied the Philistines who were there gathered together to battle, and the men of Israel had gone away.

### 2 Samuel 23:10
He got up and struck the Philistines until his hand was weary, and his hand froze to the sword; and the Lord worked a great victory that day; and the people returned after him only to take plunder.

### 2 Samuel 23:11
After him was Shammah the son of Agee a Hararite. The Philistines had gathered together into a troop, where there was a plot of ground full of lentils; and the people ran away from the Philistines.

### 2 Samuel 23:12
But he stood in the middle of the plot and defended it, and killed the Philistines; and the Lord worked a great victory.

### 2 Samuel 23:13
Three of the thirty chief men went down, and came to David in the harvest time to the cave of Adullam; and the troop of the Philistines was camped in the valley of Rephaim.

### 2 Samuel 23:14
David was then in the stronghold; and the military camp of the Philistines was then in Bethlehem.

### 2 Samuel 23:15
David longed, and said, "Oh that someone would give me water to drink from the well of Bethlehem, that is by the gate!"

### 2 Samuel 23:16
The three brave men broke through the army of the Philistines, and drew water out of the well of Bethlehem that was by the gate, and took it and brought it to David; but he would not drink of it, but poured it out to the Lord.

### 2 Samuel 23:17
He said, "Be it far from me, the Lord, that I should do this! Isn't this the blood of the men who risked their lives to go?" Therefore he would not drink it. The three brave men did these things.

### 2 Samuel 23:18
Abishai, the brother of Joab, the son of Zeruiah, was chief of the three. He lifted up his spear against three hundred and killed them, and had a name among the three.

### 2 Samuel 23:19
Wasn't he most honorable of the three? Therefore he was made their commander. However he wasn't included as one of the three.

### 2 Samuel 23:20
Benaiah the son of Jehoiada, the son of a valiant man of Kabzeel, who had done mighty deeds, killed the two sons of Ariel of Moab. He also went down and killed a lion in the middle of a pit in a time of snow.

### 2 Samuel 23:21
He killed a huge Egyptian, and the Egyptian had a spear in his hand; but he went down to him with a staff, and plucked the spear out of the Egyptian's hand, and killed him with his own spear.

### 2 Samuel 23:22
Benaiah the son of Jehoiada did these things, and had a name among the three brave men.

### 2 Samuel 23:23
Benaiah was honored more than the thirty, but he did not belong to the three. David appointed him over his guard.

### 2 Samuel 23:24
Asahel the brother of Joab was one of the thirty: Elhanan the son of Dodo of Bethlehem,

### 2 Samuel 23:25
Shammah the Harodite, Elika the Harodite,

### 2 Samuel 23:26
Helez the Paltite, Ira the son of Ikkesh the Tekoite,

### 2 Samuel 23:27
Abiezer the Anathothite, Mebunnai the Hushathite,

### 2 Samuel 23:28
Zalmon the Ahohite, Maharai the Netophathite,

### 2 Samuel 23:29
Heleb the son of Baanah the Netophathite, Ittai the son of Ribai of Gibeah of the people of Benjamin,

### 2 Samuel 23:30
Benaiah a Pirathonite, Hiddai of the brooks of Gaash.

### 2 Samuel 23:31
Abialbon the Arbathite, Azmaveth the Barhumite,

### 2 Samuel 23:32
Eliahba the Shaalbonite, the sons of Jashen, Jonathan,

### 2 Samuel 23:33
Shammah the Hararite, Ahiam the son of Sharar the Ararite,

### 2 Samuel 23:34
Eliphelet the son of Ahasbai, the son of the Maacathite, Eliam the son of Ahithophel the Gilonite,

### 2 Samuel 23:35
Hezro the Carmelite, Paarai the Arbite,

### 2 Samuel 23:36
Igal the son of Nathan of Zobah, Bani the Gadite,

### 2 Samuel 23:37
Zelek the Ammonite, and Naharai the Beerothite, who carried armor for Joab son of Zeruiah,

### 2 Samuel 23:38
Ira the Ithrite, Gareb the Ithrite,

### 2 Samuel 23:39
and Uriah the Hittite: thirty-seven in all.
