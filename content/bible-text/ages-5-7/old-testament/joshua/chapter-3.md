# Joshua 3

## Book
Joshua

## Chapter
3

## Verses

### Joshua 3:1
Early in the morning, Joshua and all the people of Israel left Shittim and came to the Jordan River. They camped there before crossing over.

### Joshua 3:2
After three days, the officers went through the camp.

### Joshua 3:3
They commanded the people, "When you see the Lord's covenant chest and the priests from Levi's tribe carrying it, leave your place and follow it.

### Joshua 3:4
Keep a wide space between you and the covenant chest. Do not come close to it. Then you will know the way to go, because you have never gone this way before."

### Joshua 3:5
Joshua said to the people, "Prepare yourselves before the Lord, because tomorrow the Lord will do wonders among you."

### Joshua 3:6
Joshua told the priests, "Pick up the covenant chest and cross over ahead of the people." So they picked up the covenant chest and went before the people.

### Joshua 3:7
The Lord said to Joshua, "Today I will begin to make you great in the sight of all Israel. They will know that I will be with you just as I was with Moses.

### Joshua 3:8
Command the priests who carry the covenant chest, 'When you reach the edge of the Jordan River, stand still in the river.'"

### Joshua 3:9
Joshua said to the people of Israel, "Come here, and hear the words of the Lord your God."

### Joshua 3:10
Joshua said, "By this you will know that the living God is among you. He will surely drive out the Canaanites, Hittites, Hivites, Perizzites, Girgashites, Amorites, and Jebusites before you.

### Joshua 3:11
Look, the covenant chest of the Lord of all the earth is crossing the Jordan River ahead of you.

### Joshua 3:12
Now choose twelve men from the tribes of Israel, one man from each tribe.

### Joshua 3:13
As soon as the priests carrying the Lord's covenant chest set their feet in the Jordan River, the water flowing down from upstream will stop and stand in a heap."

### Joshua 3:14
The people left their tents to cross the Jordan River, and the priests carrying the covenant chest went ahead of them.

### Joshua 3:15
It was harvest time, and the Jordan River was overflowing its banks. The priests carrying the covenant chest came to the river, and their feet touched the edge of the water.

### Joshua 3:16
Then the water flowing from upstream stopped. It stood up in a heap far away near the city of Adam, beside Zarethan. The water flowing down toward the Salt Sea was cut off, and the people crossed near Jericho.

### Joshua 3:17
The priests carrying the Lord's covenant chest stood firmly on dry ground in the middle of the Jordan River. All Israel crossed over on dry ground until the whole nation had finished crossing.
