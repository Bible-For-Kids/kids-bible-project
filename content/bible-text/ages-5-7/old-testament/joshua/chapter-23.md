# Joshua 23

## Book
Joshua

## Chapter
23

## Verses

### Joshua 23:1
After many days, the Lord had given Israel rest from all their enemies around them. Joshua was now very old.

### Joshua 23:2
Joshua called all Israel, including their elders, heads, judges, and officers. He said to them, "I am very old.

### Joshua 23:3
You have seen all that the Lord your God has done to all these nations because of you; for it is the Lord your God who has fought for you.

### Joshua 23:4
Look, I have divided the land for your tribes, including the nations that remain and all the nations I have cut off, from the Jordan River to the Great Sea where the sun goes down.

### Joshua 23:5
The Lord your God will push those nations out before you and drive them away from your sight. You will receive their land, just as the Lord your God promised you.

### Joshua 23:6
Be very courageous. Keep and do everything written in the Book of the Law of Moses. Do not turn away from it to the right or to the left.

### Joshua 23:7
Do not mix yourselves with the nations that remain among you. Do not call on the names of their gods, swear by them, serve them, or bow down to them.

### Joshua 23:8
but hold fast to the Lord your God, as you have done to this day.

### Joshua 23:9
The Lord has driven out great and strong nations before you. No one has been able to stand against you to this day.

### Joshua 23:10
One man from you can chase a thousand, because the Lord your God fights for you, just as he promised.

### Joshua 23:11
So guard your hearts carefully, and love the Lord your God.

### Joshua 23:12
But if you turn back, hold tight to the nations that still remain among you, marry among them, and join yourselves to them,

### Joshua 23:13
Then know for certain that the Lord your God will no longer drive these nations out before you. They will become a trap and a danger to you, like whips on your sides and thorns in your eyes, until you are removed from this good land the Lord your God has given you.

### Joshua 23:14
Joshua said, "Now I am going the way of all the earth. You know with all your heart and all your soul that not one good thing has failed from everything the Lord your God promised you. Every word has come to pass. Not one word has failed.

### Joshua 23:15
But just as every good word the Lord your God promised has come to pass for you, the Lord can also bring every warning on you until he removes you from this good land he has given you.

### Joshua 23:16
If you break the covenant of the Lord your God, serve other gods, and bow down to them, the Lord's anger will burn against you, and you will quickly be removed from the good land he has given you."
