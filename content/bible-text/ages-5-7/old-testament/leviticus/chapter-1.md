# Leviticus 1

## Book
Leviticus

## Chapter
1

## Verses

### Leviticus 1:1
The Lord called to Moses, and spoke to him from the Tent of Meeting, saying,

### Leviticus 1:2
"Speak to the people of Israel, and tell them, 'When any one of you offers an offering to the Lord, you must offer your offering of the animals, from the herd and from the flock.

### Leviticus 1:3
"If his offering is a burned offering from the herd, he must offer a male with nothing wrong with it. He must offer it at the door of the Tent of Meeting, that he may be accepted before the Lord.

### Leviticus 1:4
He must place his hand on the head of the burned offering, and it will be accepted for him to make atonement for him, to help make things right with God.

### Leviticus 1:5
He must kill the bull before the Lord. Aaron's sons, the priests, must present the blood and sprinkle the blood around on the altar that is at the door of the Tent of Meeting.

### Leviticus 1:6
He must skin the burned offering, and cut it into pieces.

### Leviticus 1:7
The sons of Aaron the priest must put fire on the altar, and lay wood in order on the fire;

### Leviticus 1:8
and Aaron's sons, the priests, must lay the pieces, the head, and the fat in order on the wood that is on the fire which is on the altar;

### Leviticus 1:9
but he must wash its inner parts and its legs with water. The priest must burn all of it on the altar, for a burned offering, an offering burned on the altar, of a pleasing smell to the Lord.

### Leviticus 1:10
"If his offering is from the flock, from the sheep or from the goats, for a burned offering, he must offer a male with nothing wrong with it.

### Leviticus 1:11
He must kill it on the north side of the altar before the Lord. Aaron's sons, the priests, must sprinkle its blood around on the altar.

### Leviticus 1:12
He must cut it into its pieces, with its head and its fat. The priest must lay them in order on the wood that is on the fire which is on the altar,

### Leviticus 1:13
but the inner parts and the legs he must wash with water. The priest must offer the whole, and burn it on the altar. It is a burned offering, an offering burned on the altar, of a pleasing smell to the Lord.

### Leviticus 1:14
"If his offering to the Lord is a burned offering of birds, then he must offer his offering from doves or of young pigeons.

### Leviticus 1:15
The priest must bring it to the altar, and wring off its head, and burn it on the altar; and its blood must be drained out on the side of the altar;

### Leviticus 1:16
and he must take away its crop and its feathers, and put it beside the altar on the east part, in the place of the ashes.

### Leviticus 1:17
He must tear it by its wings, but must not divide it apart. The priest must burn it on the altar, on the wood that is on the fire. It is a burned offering, an offering burned on the altar, of a pleasing smell to the Lord.
