# Leviticus 1

## Book
Leviticus

## Chapter
1

## Verses

### Leviticus 1:1
The Lord called to Moses from the Tent of Meeting. He spoke to him and said,

### Leviticus 1:2
"Speak to the people of Israel. Tell them, 'When one of you brings an offering to the Lord, bring it from your farm animals, from cattle or from sheep and goats.

### Leviticus 1:3
"If his offering is a burned offering from the cattle, he must bring a male animal with nothing wrong with it. He must bring it to the entrance of the Tent of Meeting, so he may be accepted before the Lord.

### Leviticus 1:4
He must place his hand on the head of the burned offering. The Lord will accept the offering for him, to cover his sin and make a way for him to be right with God.

### Leviticus 1:5
He must kill the bull before the Lord. Aaron's sons, the priests, must bring the blood and sprinkle it all around the altar at the entrance of the Tent of Meeting.

### Leviticus 1:6
He must remove the animal's skin and cut the burned offering into pieces.

### Leviticus 1:7
The sons of Aaron the priest must put fire on the altar and arrange wood on the fire.

### Leviticus 1:8
Then Aaron's sons, the priests, must arrange the pieces, the head, and the fat on the wood burning on the altar.

### Leviticus 1:9
But he must wash its inner parts and its legs with water. The priest must burn all of it on the altar. It is a burned offering, an offering burned on the altar, with a pleasing smell to the Lord.

### Leviticus 1:10
"If his offering is a burned offering from the flock, from the sheep or goats, he must bring a male animal with nothing wrong with it.

### Leviticus 1:11
He must kill it on the north side of the altar before the Lord. Aaron's sons, the priests, must sprinkle its blood all around the altar.

### Leviticus 1:12
He must cut it into pieces, with its head and its fat. The priest must arrange them on the wood burning on the altar.

### Leviticus 1:13
He must wash the inner parts and the legs with water. The priest must offer the whole animal and burn it on the altar. It is a burned offering, an offering burned on the altar, with a pleasing smell to the Lord.

### Leviticus 1:14
"If his offering to the Lord is a burned offering of birds, he must bring a dove or a young pigeon.

### Leviticus 1:15
The priest must bring the bird to the altar, take off its head, and burn it on the altar. Its blood must be drained out on the side of the altar.

### Leviticus 1:16
He must take away its food pouch and feathers and put them beside the altar on the east side, in the place for ashes.

### Leviticus 1:17
He must pull it open by its wings, but he must not tear it completely apart. The priest must burn it on the altar, on the wood in the fire. It is a burned offering, an offering burned on the altar, with a pleasing smell to the Lord.
