# 1 Kings 6

## Book
1 Kings

## Chapter
6

## Verses

### 1 Kings 6:1
In the four hundred eightieth year after the people of Israel came out of Egypt, in Solomon's fourth year as king, Solomon began to build the Lord's house.

### 1 Kings 6:2
The house King Solomon built for the Lord was sixty arm-lengths long, twenty arm-lengths wide, and thirty arm-lengths high.

### 1 Kings 6:3
The entry room at the front of the temple stretched across the whole width of the house. It was twenty arm-lengths long and ten arm-lengths deep.

### 1 Kings 6:4
Solomon made narrow windows with fixed lattice work for the house.

### 1 Kings 6:5
He built side rooms all around the walls of the house, around both the main holy room and the inner holy room.

### 1 Kings 6:6
The lowest side rooms were five arm-lengths wide, the middle rooms six, and the third-floor rooms seven. The outer wall had ledges all around so the beams would rest without cutting into the temple walls.

### 1 Kings 6:7
The stones were cut and prepared at the quarry before they were brought to the temple. So while the house was being built, no hammer, ax, or iron tool was heard there.

### 1 Kings 6:8
The door to the middle side rooms was on the right side of the house. Winding stairs went up to the middle floor and then up to the third floor.

### 1 Kings 6:9
So Solomon built the house and finished it. He covered it with cedar beams and cedar planks.

### 1 Kings 6:10
He built the side rooms along the whole house, each floor five arm-lengths high, and they rested on cedar beams.

### 1 Kings 6:11
The Lord's word came to Solomon, saying,

### 1 Kings 6:12
"About this house you are building: if you walk in my laws, do my rules, and keep all my commands, then I will establish the word I spoke to David your father.

### 1 Kings 6:13
I will live among the people of Israel, and I will not forsake my people Israel."

### 1 Kings 6:14
So Solomon built the house, and finished it.

### 1 Kings 6:15
Inside the Lord's house, Solomon covered the stone walls with cedar boards from the floor to the ceiling. He also covered the floor with cypress boards.

### 1 Kings 6:16
At the back of the house, he built an inner holy room with cedar boards from floor to ceiling. This room was the Most Holy Place.

### 1 Kings 6:17
The main holy room in front of it was forty arm-lengths long.

### 1 Kings 6:18
The cedar inside the house was carved with buds and open flowers. Everything inside was cedar, so no stone could be seen.

### 1 Kings 6:19
Solomon prepared the inner holy room inside the house so the ark of the Lord's covenant could be placed there.

### 1 Kings 6:20
The inner holy room was twenty arm-lengths long, twenty arm-lengths wide, and twenty arm-lengths high. Solomon covered it with pure gold, and he covered the altar with cedar.

### 1 Kings 6:21
Solomon covered the inside of the house with pure gold. He stretched gold chains across in front of the inner holy room and covered that room with gold.

### 1 Kings 6:22
He covered the whole house with gold until all the work was finished. He also covered the whole altar that belonged to the inner holy room with gold.

### 1 Kings 6:23
In the inner holy room, Solomon made two cherubim, mighty heavenly creatures, from olive wood. Each one was ten arm-lengths high.

### 1 Kings 6:24
Each cherub had two wings. One wing was five arm-lengths long, and the other wing was five arm-lengths long, ten arm-lengths from wingtip to wingtip.

### 1 Kings 6:25
The second cherub was the same size and shape as the first.

### 1 Kings 6:26
Each cherub was ten arm-lengths high.

### 1 Kings 6:27
He set the cherubim, mighty heavenly creatures inside the inner room. Their wings stretched out so one wing touched one wall, the other wing touched the other wall, and their wings touched each other in the middle.

### 1 Kings 6:28
He covered the cherubim, mighty heavenly creatures with gold.

### 1 Kings 6:29
All around the walls, inside and outside the inner room, he carved cherubim, mighty heavenly creatures, palm tree carvings, and open flowers.

### 1 Kings 6:30
He covered the floor of the house with gold, both inside the main room and inside the inner room.

### 1 Kings 6:31
For the entrance to the inner holy room, he made doors of olive wood with strong posts and a top beam.

### 1 Kings 6:32
On the two olive-wood doors, he carved cherubim, mighty heavenly creatures, palm tree carvings, and open flowers. Then he covered the doors and carvings with gold.

### 1 Kings 6:33
He also made olive-wood posts for the entrance to the main holy room.

### 1 Kings 6:34
He made two folding doors of cypress wood. Each door had two folding panels.

### 1 Kings 6:35
He carved cherubim, mighty heavenly creatures, palm tree carvings, and open flowers on them, and covered the carved work with fitted gold.

### 1 Kings 6:36
He built the inner court with three courses of cut stone and a course of cedar beams.

### 1 Kings 6:37
The foundation of the Lord's house was laid in the fourth year, in the month Ziv.

### 1 Kings 6:38
In Solomon's eleventh year, in the eighth month, the house was finished in all its parts and according to all its plans. Solomon spent seven years building it.
