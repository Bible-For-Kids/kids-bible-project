# Exodus 31

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
31

## Verses

### Exodus 31:1
The Lord spoke to Moses.

### Exodus 31:2
He said, "I have chosen Bezalel by name. He is the son of Uri, the son of Hur, from the tribe of Judah."

### Exodus 31:3
"I have filled him with the Spirit of God, giving him wisdom, understanding, knowledge, and skill for every kind of work."

### Exodus 31:4
"He can plan beautiful designs and work with gold, silver, and bronze."

### Exodus 31:5
"He can cut stones and set them in place. He can carve wood and do all kinds of careful work."

### Exodus 31:6
"I have also given him Aholiab, son of Ahisamach, from the tribe of Dan. I have placed wisdom in the hearts of all the skilled workers, so they can make everything I commanded you."

### Exodus 31:7
"They will make the meeting tent, the ark of the testimony, the cover of the ark on it, and all the things used in the holy tent."

### Exodus 31:8
"They will make the table and its things, the pure lampstand and all its things, and the altar for sweet-smelling incense."

### Exodus 31:9
"They will make the altar for burned offerings and all its things, and the washbasin with its stand."

### Exodus 31:10
"They will make the special woven clothes, the holy clothes for Aaron the priest, and the clothes for his sons when they serve as priests."

### Exodus 31:11
"They will make the special setting-apart oil and the sweet-smelling incense for the holy place. They must do everything just as I commanded you."

### Exodus 31:12
The Lord spoke to Moses again.

### Exodus 31:13
He said, "Speak to the people of Israel and tell them, 'You must keep my Sabbaths. This will be a sign between me and you for all your generations, so you may know that I am the Lord who makes you holy.'"

### Exodus 31:14
"'Keep the Sabbath, because it is holy for you. Anyone who treats it as ordinary must be put to death. Whoever works on that day must be cut off from his people.'"

### Exodus 31:15
"'Work may be done for six days, but the seventh day is a Sabbath of complete rest, holy to the Lord. Whoever works on the Sabbath day must be put to death.'"

### Exodus 31:16
"'The people of Israel must keep the Sabbath and honor it through all their generations as a lasting covenant.'"

### Exodus 31:17
"'It is a sign forever between me and the people of Israel, because in six days the Lord made heaven and earth, and on the seventh day he rested and was refreshed.'"

### Exodus 31:18
When the Lord finished speaking with Moses on Mount Sinai, he gave him two tablets of testimony. They were tablets of stone, written by the finger of God.
