# Exodus 37

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
37

## Verses

### Exodus 37:1
Bezalel made the ark from acacia wood. It was two and a half arm-lengths long, one and a half arm-lengths wide, and one and a half arm-lengths high.

### Exodus 37:2
He covered it inside and outside with pure gold, and he made a gold border around it.

### Exodus 37:3
He make four gold rings for its four feet, two rings on one side and two rings on the other side.

### Exodus 37:4
He made poles from acacia wood and covered them with gold.

### Exodus 37:5
He put the poles through the rings on the sides of the ark so the ark could be carried.

### Exodus 37:6
He made the cover of the ark from pure gold. It was two and a half arm-lengths long and one and a half arm-lengths wide.

### Exodus 37:7
He made two mighty heavenly guardians of hammered gold at the two ends of the cover of the ark.

### Exodus 37:8
One mighty heavenly guardian was on one end, and the other mighty heavenly guardian was on the other end. The mighty heavenly guardians were made as one piece with the cover of the ark.

### Exodus 37:9
The mighty heavenly guardians spread their wings upward, covering the cover of the ark with their wings. Their faces looked toward each other and down toward the cover of the ark.

### Exodus 37:10
He made the table from acacia wood. It was two arm-lengths long, one arm-length wide, and one and a half arm-lengths high.

### Exodus 37:11
He covered it with pure gold and made a gold border around it.

### Exodus 37:12
He made a rim a palm-width wide around the table, with a gold border around the rim.

### Exodus 37:13
He make four gold rings and put them on the four corners by the table's four legs.

### Exodus 37:14
The rings were close to the rim, and they held the poles used to carry the table.

### Exodus 37:15
He made the poles from acacia wood and covered them with gold, so the table could be carried.

### Exodus 37:16
He made the table's dishes, spoons, bowls, and jars for pouring offerings from pure gold.

### Exodus 37:17
He made the lampstand from pure hammered gold. Its base, stem, cups, buds, and flowers were all made from one piece.

### Exodus 37:18
Six branches came out from its sides, three branches from one side and three from the other.

### Exodus 37:19
On one branch were three cups shaped like almond flowers, with a bud and a flower. The same design was on each of the six branches.

### Exodus 37:20
On the lampstand itself were four cups shaped like almond flowers, with buds and flowers.

### Exodus 37:21
There was one bud under each pair of branches, making three buds for the six branches coming out of the lampstand.

### Exodus 37:22
The buds and branches were all one piece with the lampstand, hammered from pure gold.

### Exodus 37:23
He made its seven lamps, its tongs, and its trays from pure gold.

### Exodus 37:24
He used one large weight measure of pure gold to make the lampstand and all its tools.

### Exodus 37:25
He made the sweet-smelling incense altar from acacia wood. It was one arm-length long, one arm-length wide, square in shape, and two arm-lengths high. Its horns were made as one piece with it.

### Exodus 37:26
He covered the top, sides, and horns with pure gold, and he made a gold border around it.

### Exodus 37:27
He made two gold rings under the border on two opposite sides. These rings held the poles for carrying the altar.

### Exodus 37:28
He made the poles from acacia wood and covered them with gold.

### Exodus 37:29
He also made the holy special setting-apart oil and the pure, sweet-smelling incense, mixed like the work of a skilled perfume maker.
