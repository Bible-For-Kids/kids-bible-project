# Exodus 6

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
6

## Verses

### Exodus 6:1
Then the Lord said to Moses, "Now you will see what I will do to Pharaoh. By a strong hand he will let my people go, and by a strong hand he will drive them out of his land."

### Exodus 6:2
God spoke to Moses and said, "I am the Lord."

### Exodus 6:3
"I showed myself to Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob as God Almighty, but I did not make my name, the Lord, known to them in the way I am showing it now."

### Exodus 6:4
"I also made my covenant, my serious promise, with them. I promised to give them the land of Canaan, the land where they lived as strangers."

### Exodus 6:5
"I have heard the sad groaning of the children of Israel, whom the Egyptians keep as slaves, and I have remembered my covenant promise."

### Exodus 6:6
"So tell the children of Israel, 'I am the Lord. I will bring you out from under Egypt's heavy loads, free you from slavery, and redeem you with my outstretched arm and with great judgments.'"

### Exodus 6:7
"'I will take you as my people, and I will be your God. You will know that I am the Lord your God, who brings you out from under Egypt's burdens.'"

### Exodus 6:8
"'I will bring you into the land I promised to give to Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob. I will give it to you as your own home. I am the Lord.'"

### Exodus 6:9
Moses told the children of Israel these words, but their spirits were so crushed by hard slavery that they did not listen to him.

### Exodus 6:10
The Lord spoke to Moses again.

### Exodus 6:11
He said, "Go and speak to Pharaoh king of Egypt. Tell him to let the children of Israel leave his land."

### Exodus 6:12
Moses said to the Lord, "The children of Israel have not listened to me. How will Pharaoh listen to me, since I do not speak well?"

### Exodus 6:13
The Lord spoke to Moses and Aaron and gave them a command for the children of Israel and for Pharaoh king of Egypt: they were to bring the children of Israel out of Egypt.

### Exodus 6:14
These were the leaders of their fathers' families: Reuben, Israel's firstborn, had sons named Hanoch, Pallu, Hezron, and Carmi. These were the families of Reuben.

### Exodus 6:15
Simeon's sons were Jemuel, Jamin, Ohad, Jachin, Zohar, and Shaul, whose mother was a Canaanite woman. These were the families of Simeon.

### Exodus 6:16
These were the names of Levi's sons by their generations: Gershon, Kohath, and Merari. Levi lived 137 years.

### Exodus 6:17
Gershon's sons were Libni and Shimi, each with their families.

### Exodus 6:18
Kohath's sons were Amram, Izhar, Hebron, and Uzziel. Kohath lived 133 years.

### Exodus 6:19
Merari's sons were Mahali and Mushi. These were the families of Levi by their generations.

### Exodus 6:20
Amram married Jochebed, his father's sister, and she gave birth to Aaron and Moses. Amram lived 137 years.

### Exodus 6:21
Izhar's sons were Korah, Nepheg, and Zichri.

### Exodus 6:22
Uzziel's sons were Mishael, Elzaphan, and Zithri.

### Exodus 6:23
Aaron married Elisheba, daughter of Amminadab and sister of Naashon. She gave birth to Nadab, Abihu, Eleazar, and Ithamar.

### Exodus 6:24
Korah's sons were Assir, Elkanah, and Abiasaph. These were the families of the Korahites.

### Exodus 6:25
Eleazar, Aaron's son, married one of Putiel's daughters, and she gave birth to Phinehas. These were the leaders of the Levite families.

### Exodus 6:26
This was the same Aaron and Moses to whom the Lord said, "Bring the children of Israel out of Egypt by their groups."

### Exodus 6:27
They were the ones who spoke to Pharaoh king of Egypt to bring the children of Israel out of Egypt. This was Moses and Aaron.

### Exodus 6:28
On the day when the Lord spoke to Moses in the land of Egypt,

### Exodus 6:29
the Lord said to Moses, "I am the Lord. Tell Pharaoh king of Egypt everything I say to you."

### Exodus 6:30
But Moses said to the Lord, "I do not speak well. How will Pharaoh listen to me?"
