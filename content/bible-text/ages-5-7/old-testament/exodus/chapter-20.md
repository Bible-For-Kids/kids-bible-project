# Exodus 20

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
20

## Verses

### Exodus 20:1
God spoke all these words.

### Exodus 20:2
"I am the Lord your God, who brought you out of Egypt, out of the house of slavery."

### Exodus 20:3
"You must have no other gods before me."

### Exodus 20:4
"You must not make for yourself a carved image, or anything shaped like what is in heaven above, on the earth below, or in the water under the earth."

### Exodus 20:5
"You must not bow down to them or serve them. I, the Lord your God, am a jealous God. I bring the guilt of fathers on children to the third and fourth generation of those who hate me."

### Exodus 20:6
"But I show faithful love to thousands of generations of those who love me and keep my commandments."

### Exodus 20:7
"You must not misuse the name of the Lord your God, because the Lord will not hold guiltless anyone who misuses his name."

### Exodus 20:8
"Remember the Sabbath day, and keep it holy."

### Exodus 20:9
"Six days you must work and do all your work."

### Exodus 20:10
"But the seventh day is a Sabbath to the Lord your God. On it you must not do any work: not you, your son, your daughter, your male servant, your female servant, your animals, or the visitor inside your gates."

### Exodus 20:11
"In six days the Lord made heaven, earth, the sea, and everything in them, and he rested on the seventh day. So the Lord blessed the Sabbath day and made it holy."

### Exodus 20:12
"Honor your father and your mother, so your days may be long in the land the Lord your God is giving you."

### Exodus 20:13
"You must not murder."

### Exodus 20:14
"You must not break marriage faithfulness."

### Exodus 20:15
"You must not steal."

### Exodus 20:16
"You must not tell false testimony against your neighbor."

### Exodus 20:17
"You must not want to take your neighbor's house. You must not want to take your neighbor's wife, his male servant, his female servant, his ox, his donkey, or anything that belongs to your neighbor."

### Exodus 20:18
All the people saw the thunder, lightning, trumpet sound, and smoking mountain. They were afraid, trembled, and stood far away.

### Exodus 20:19
They said to Moses, "You speak to us, and we will listen. But do not let God speak to us, or we may die."

### Exodus 20:20
Moses said to the people, "Do not be afraid. God has come to test you, so respect for him will be before you and you will not sin."

### Exodus 20:21
The people stood far away, while Moses came near the thick darkness where God was.

### Exodus 20:22
The Lord said to Moses, "Say this to the people of Israel: You have seen that I spoke with you from heaven."

### Exodus 20:23
"You must not make gods of silver or gods of gold to be with me. Do not make them for yourselves."

### Exodus 20:24
"Make an altar of earth for me, and sacrifice your burned offerings and peace offerings on it, your sheep and your oxen. In every place where I cause my name to be remembered, I will come to you and bless you."

### Exodus 20:25
"If you make me an altar of stone, do not build it with cut stones. If you use your tool on it, you have made it unfit."

### Exodus 20:26
"Do not go up to my altar by steps, so your body stays covered there."
