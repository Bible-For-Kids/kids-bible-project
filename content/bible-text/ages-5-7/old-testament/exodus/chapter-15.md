# Exodus 15

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
15

## Verses

### Exodus 15:1
Then Moses and the Israelites sang this song to the Lord: "I will sing to the Lord, because he has won a wonderful victory. He has thrown the horse and rider into the sea.

### Exodus 15:2
The Lord is my strength and my song, and he has saved me. He is my God, and I will praise him. He is my father's God, and I will lift him high.

### Exodus 15:3
The Lord is a warrior. The Lord is his name.

### Exodus 15:4
He threw Pharaoh's chariots and army into the sea. Pharaoh's best army leaders sank in the Red Sea.

### Exodus 15:5
The deep waters covered them. They sank down like a stone.

### Exodus 15:6
Your right hand, Lord, is glorious in power. Your right hand, Lord, crushed the enemy.

### Exodus 15:7
In your great majesty, you threw down those who rose against you. You sent out your anger, and it burned them up like dry straw.

### Exodus 15:8
At the blast of your breath, the waters piled up. The waves stood like a heap, and the deep waters became firm in the heart of the sea.

### Exodus 15:9
The enemy said, 'I will chase them. I will catch them. I will divide what they have. I will take out my sword, and my hand will destroy them.'

### Exodus 15:10
But you blew with your wind, and the sea covered them. They sank like lead in the mighty waters.

### Exodus 15:11
Who is like you among the gods, Lord? Who is like you, glorious in holiness, awesome in praise, and doing wonders?

### Exodus 15:12
You stretched out your right hand, and the earth swallowed them.

### Exodus 15:13
In your mercy, you led the people you rescued. In your strength, you guided them toward your holy home.

### Exodus 15:14
The peoples will hear and tremble. Fear will come over the people of Philistia.

### Exodus 15:15
The leaders of Edom will be amazed. The strong men of Moab will tremble. The people of Canaan will melt with fear.

### Exodus 15:16
Terror and dread will fall on them. Because of your great arm, they will be still as stone until your people pass by, Lord, the people you have bought.

### Exodus 15:17
You will bring them in and plant them on the mountain that belongs to you, the place you made for your home, Lord, the holy place your hands established.

### Exodus 15:18
The Lord will reign forever and ever."

### Exodus 15:19
Pharaoh's horses, chariots, and riders went into the sea, and the Lord brought the waters of the sea back over them. But the Israelites walked on dry ground through the middle of the sea.

### Exodus 15:20
Miriam the prophetess, Aaron's sister, took a tambourine in her hand. All the women followed her with tambourines and dancing.

### Exodus 15:21
Miriam sang back to them, "Sing to the Lord, because he has won a wonderful victory. He has thrown the horse and rider into the sea."

### Exodus 15:22
Then Moses led Israel away from the Red Sea. They went into the wilderness of Shur. For three days they traveled in the wilderness and found no water.

### Exodus 15:23
When they came to Marah, they could not drink the water there because it was bitter. That is why the place was called Marah.

### Exodus 15:24
The people grumbled against Moses and asked, "What will we drink?"

### Exodus 15:25
Moses cried out to the Lord. The Lord showed him a tree, and Moses threw it into the water. Then the water became sweet. There the Lord gave them a rule and tested them.

### Exodus 15:26
The Lord said, "If you carefully listen to the voice of the Lord your God, do what is right in his sight, pay attention to his commands, and keep all his instructions, I will not put on you the diseases I put on the Egyptians. I am the Lord who heals you."

### Exodus 15:27
Then they came to Elim, where there were twelve springs of water and seventy palm trees. They camped there by the water.
