# Exodus 36

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
36

## Verses

### Exodus 36:1
Bezalel, Aholiab, and every skilled worker began the holy work. The Lord had given them wisdom and understanding, so they knew how to make everything for the sanctuary just as the Lord commanded.

### Exodus 36:2
Moses called Bezalel, Aholiab, and all the skilled people whose hearts the Lord had filled with wisdom. Everyone who wanted to help came close to do the work.

### Exodus 36:3
They received from Moses all the gifts the Israelites had brought for building the sanctuary. Every morning, the people still brought more freewill gifts.

### Exodus 36:4
The skilled workers left the work they were doing on the sanctuary and came to speak with Moses.

### Exodus 36:5
They told Moses, "The people are bringing much more than enough for the work the Lord commanded us to make."

### Exodus 36:6
So Moses sent a command through the camp: no man or woman should make anything else as an offering for the sanctuary. Then the people stopped bringing more.

### Exodus 36:7
They already had enough materials for all the work, and even more than they needed.

### Exodus 36:8
The skilled workers made the tabernacle from ten curtains of finely twisted linen, with blue, purple, and scarlet thread. They carefully worked cherubim into the curtains.

### Exodus 36:9
Each curtain was twenty-eight cubits long and four cubits wide. All the curtains were the same size.

### Exodus 36:10
They joined five curtains together as one set, and the other five curtains together as another set.

### Exodus 36:11
They made blue loops along the edge of the last curtain in one set, and they made matching loops along the edge of the last curtain in the second set.

### Exodus 36:12
They made fifty loops on one curtain and fifty loops on the matching curtain. The loops lined up with each other.

### Exodus 36:13
They made fifty gold clasps and used them to join the curtains together, so the tabernacle became one whole tent.

### Exodus 36:14
They made eleven curtains of goats' hair to form a tent over the tabernacle.

### Exodus 36:15
Each goats' hair curtain was thirty cubits long and four cubits wide. All eleven curtains were the same size.

### Exodus 36:16
They joined five curtains together in one set and six curtains together in another set.

### Exodus 36:17
They made fifty loops on the outer edge of one curtain set and fifty loops on the edge of the other curtain set.

### Exodus 36:18
They made fifty bronze clasps to join the tent together, so it became one piece.

### Exodus 36:19
They made a covering for the tent from ram skins dyed red, and another covering above it from durable hides.

### Exodus 36:20
They made upright boards of acacia wood for the tabernacle.

### Exodus 36:21
Each board was ten cubits long and one and a half cubits wide.

### Exodus 36:22
Each board had two pegs set apart from each other. They made every board for the tabernacle this way.

### Exodus 36:23
They made twenty boards for the south side of the tabernacle.

### Exodus 36:24
They made forty silver bases for the twenty boards, with two bases under each board for its two pegs.

### Exodus 36:25
For the north side of the tabernacle, they made twenty boards.

### Exodus 36:26
They also made forty silver bases for them, with two bases under each board.

### Exodus 36:27
For the back of the tabernacle, on the west side, they made six boards.

### Exodus 36:28
They made two extra boards for the two back corners of the tabernacle.

### Exodus 36:29
The corner boards were joined at the bottom and joined at the top with one ring. They did this for both corners.

### Exodus 36:30
There were eight boards at the back, with sixteen silver bases, two bases under each board.

### Exodus 36:31
They made bars of acacia wood, five for the boards on one side of the tabernacle.

### Exodus 36:32
They made five bars for the boards on the other side, and five bars for the boards at the back, on the west side.

### Exodus 36:33
They made the middle bar pass through the boards from one end to the other.

### Exodus 36:34
They covered the boards with gold. They made gold rings to hold the bars, and they covered the bars with gold too.

### Exodus 36:35
They made the veil from blue, purple, and scarlet thread and finely twisted linen, with cherubim carefully worked into it.

### Exodus 36:36
They made four acacia-wood pillars for the veil and covered them with gold. Their hooks were gold, and their four bases were silver.

### Exodus 36:37
They made a curtain for the entrance of the tabernacle from blue, purple, and scarlet thread and finely twisted linen, all done with needlework.

### Exodus 36:38
They made five pillars for the entrance curtain, with hooks. They covered the tops and bands of the pillars with gold, but the five bases were bronze.
