# Exodus 26

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
26

## Verses

### Exodus 26:1
"Make the holy tent with ten beautiful curtains," God said. "Use fine twisted linen and blue, purple, and red thread yarn. Let skilled workers weave mighty heavenly guardians into the cloth."

### Exodus 26:2
"Each curtain must be twenty-eight arm-lengths long and four arm-lengths wide. Every curtain must be the same size."

### Exodus 26:3
"Join five curtains together in one group, and join the other five curtains together in another group."

### Exodus 26:4
"Make blue loops along the edge of the last curtain in one group. Make blue loops along the matching edge of the last curtain in the other group too."

### Exodus 26:5
"Make fifty loops on one curtain and fifty loops on the other curtain. The loops must match each other."

### Exodus 26:6
"Make fifty gold clasps. Use the clasps to join the curtains together, so the holy tent becomes one whole tent."

### Exodus 26:7
"Make eleven curtains from goat hair to cover the holy tent like a tent over it."

### Exodus 26:8
"Each goat-hair curtain must be thirty arm-lengths long and four arm-lengths wide. All eleven curtains must be the same size."

### Exodus 26:9
"Join five curtains together, and join six curtains together. Fold the sixth curtain double at the front of the tent."

### Exodus 26:10
"Make fifty loops on the edge of the last curtain in one group, and fifty loops on the edge of the curtain in the other group."

### Exodus 26:11
"Make fifty bronze clasps. Put the clasps through the loops and join the tent together, so it becomes one."

### Exodus 26:12
"The extra part of the tent curtains, the half curtain left over, must hang down over the back of the holy tent."

### Exodus 26:13
"The extra arm-length on one side and the extra arm-length on the other side must hang down over the sides of the holy tent to cover it."

### Exodus 26:14
"Make a covering for the tent from male sheep skins dyed red. Then make another covering above it from badger skins."

### Exodus 26:15
"Make upright frames for the holy tent from acacia wood."

### Exodus 26:16
"Each frame must be ten arm-lengths long and one and a half arm-lengths wide."

### Exodus 26:17
"Each frame must have two pegs beside each other. Make every frame for the holy tent this way."

### Exodus 26:18
"Make twenty frames for the south side of the holy tent."

### Exodus 26:19
"Make forty silver bases under the twenty frames, with two bases under each frame for its two pegs."

### Exodus 26:20
"For the other side, the north side of the holy tent, make twenty frames."

### Exodus 26:21
"Make forty silver bases for them, with two bases under each frame."

### Exodus 26:22
"For the back of the holy tent, on the west side, make six frames."

### Exodus 26:23
"Make two more frames for the two back corners of the holy tent."

### Exodus 26:24
"At the corners, join the frames together at the bottom and at the top with one ring. Do this for both corners."

### Exodus 26:25
"There will be eight frames at the back, with sixteen silver bases, two bases under each frame."

### Exodus 26:26
"Make crossbars from acacia wood. Make five for the frames on one side of the holy tent,"

### Exodus 26:27
"five for the frames on the other side, and five for the frames at the back, on the west side."

### Exodus 26:28
"The middle crossbar must go through the center of the frames from one end to the other."

### Exodus 26:29
"Cover the frames with gold. Make gold rings to hold the crossbars, and cover the crossbars with gold too."

### Exodus 26:30
"Set up the holy tent just like the pattern shown to you on the mountain."

### Exodus 26:31
"Make a curtain from blue, purple, and red thread yarn and fine twisted linen. Let skilled workers weave mighty heavenly guardians into it."

### Exodus 26:32
"Hang the curtain on four acacia-wood posts covered with gold. The hooks must be gold, and the posts must stand on four silver bases."

### Exodus 26:33
"Hang the curtain under the clasps. Bring the ark of the testimony inside the curtain. The curtain will separate the Holy Place from the Most Holy Place."

### Exodus 26:34
"Put the cover of the ark on the ark of the testimony in the Most Holy Place."

### Exodus 26:35
"Put the table outside the curtain. Put the lampstand across from the table on the south side of the holy tent, and put the table on the north side."

### Exodus 26:36
"Make a screen for the doorway of the tent from blue, purple, and red thread yarn and fine twisted linen, with needlework on it."

### Exodus 26:37
"Make five acacia-wood posts for the screen and cover them with gold. Their hooks must be gold, and you must make five bronze bases for them."
