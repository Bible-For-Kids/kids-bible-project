# Exodus 26

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
26

## Verses

### Exodus 26:1
"Make the tabernacle with ten beautiful curtains," God said. "Use fine twisted linen and blue, purple, and scarlet yarn. Let skilled workers weave cherubim into the cloth."

### Exodus 26:2
"Each curtain must be twenty-eight cubits long and four cubits wide. Every curtain must be the same size."

### Exodus 26:3
"Join five curtains together in one group, and join the other five curtains together in another group."

### Exodus 26:4
"Make blue loops along the edge of the last curtain in one group. Make blue loops along the matching edge of the last curtain in the other group too."

### Exodus 26:5
"Make fifty loops on one curtain and fifty loops on the other curtain. The loops must match each other."

### Exodus 26:6
"Make fifty gold clasps. Use the clasps to join the curtains together, so the tabernacle becomes one whole tent."

### Exodus 26:7
"Make eleven curtains from goat hair to cover the tabernacle like a tent over it."

### Exodus 26:8
"Each goat-hair curtain must be thirty cubits long and four cubits wide. All eleven curtains must be the same size."

### Exodus 26:9
"Join five curtains together, and join six curtains together. Fold the sixth curtain double at the front of the tent."

### Exodus 26:10
"Make fifty loops on the edge of the last curtain in one group, and fifty loops on the edge of the curtain in the other group."

### Exodus 26:11
"Make fifty bronze clasps. Put the clasps through the loops and join the tent together, so it becomes one."

### Exodus 26:12
"The extra part of the tent curtains, the half curtain left over, must hang down over the back of the tabernacle."

### Exodus 26:13
"The extra cubit on one side and the extra cubit on the other side must hang down over the sides of the tabernacle to cover it."

### Exodus 26:14
"Make a covering for the tent from ram skins dyed red. Then make another covering above it from badger skins."

### Exodus 26:15
"Make upright frames for the tabernacle from acacia wood."

### Exodus 26:16
"Each frame must be ten cubits long and one and a half cubits wide."

### Exodus 26:17
"Each frame must have two pegs beside each other. Make every frame for the tabernacle this way."

### Exodus 26:18
"Make twenty frames for the south side of the tabernacle."

### Exodus 26:19
"Make forty silver bases under the twenty frames, with two bases under each frame for its two pegs."

### Exodus 26:20
"For the other side, the north side of the tabernacle, make twenty frames."

### Exodus 26:21
"Make forty silver bases for them, with two bases under each frame."

### Exodus 26:22
"For the back of the tabernacle, on the west side, make six frames."

### Exodus 26:23
"Make two more frames for the two back corners of the tabernacle."

### Exodus 26:24
"At the corners, join the frames together at the bottom and at the top with one ring. Do this for both corners."

### Exodus 26:25
"There will be eight frames at the back, with sixteen silver bases, two bases under each frame."

### Exodus 26:26
"Make crossbars from acacia wood. Make five for the frames on one side of the tabernacle,"

### Exodus 26:27
"five for the frames on the other side, and five for the frames at the back, on the west side."

### Exodus 26:28
"The middle crossbar must go through the center of the frames from one end to the other."

### Exodus 26:29
"Cover the frames with gold. Make gold rings to hold the crossbars, and cover the crossbars with gold too."

### Exodus 26:30
"Set up the tabernacle just like the pattern shown to you on the mountain."

### Exodus 26:31
"Make a veil from blue, purple, and scarlet yarn and fine twisted linen. Let skilled workers weave cherubim into it."

### Exodus 26:32
"Hang the veil on four acacia-wood posts covered with gold. The hooks must be gold, and the posts must stand on four silver bases."

### Exodus 26:33
"Hang the veil under the clasps. Bring the ark of the testimony inside the veil. The veil will separate the Holy Place from the Most Holy Place."

### Exodus 26:34
"Put the mercy seat on the ark of the testimony in the Most Holy Place."

### Exodus 26:35
"Put the table outside the veil. Put the lampstand across from the table on the south side of the tabernacle, and put the table on the north side."

### Exodus 26:36
"Make a screen for the doorway of the tent from blue, purple, and scarlet yarn and fine twisted linen, with needlework on it."

### Exodus 26:37
"Make five acacia-wood posts for the screen and cover them with gold. Their hooks must be gold, and you must make five bronze bases for them."
