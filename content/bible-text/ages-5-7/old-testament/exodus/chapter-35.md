# Exodus 35

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
35

## Verses

### Exodus 35:1
Moses gathered all the people of Israel together. He said to them, "These are the words the Lord has commanded you to do."

### Exodus 35:2
"Work may be done for six days, but the seventh day must be holy for you. It is a Sabbath of rest to the Lord. Whoever works on it must be put to death."

### Exodus 35:3
"Do not light a fire in any of your homes on the Sabbath day."

### Exodus 35:4
Moses spoke to all the people of Israel and said, "This is what the Lord commanded."

### Exodus 35:5
"Take an offering for the Lord from among you. Everyone with a willing heart may bring the Lord's offering: gold, silver, and bronze."

### Exodus 35:6
"Bring blue, purple, and scarlet thread, fine linen, and goats' hair."

### Exodus 35:7
"Bring ram skins dyed red, fine leather, and acacia wood."

### Exodus 35:8
"Bring oil for the light, and spices for the anointing oil and the sweet-smelling incense."

### Exodus 35:9
"Bring onyx stones and other stones to set in the ephod and the breastpiece."

### Exodus 35:10
"Let every skilled worker among you come and make everything the Lord has commanded."

### Exodus 35:11
"Make the tabernacle, its tent, its covering, its hooks, boards, bars, posts, and bases."

### Exodus 35:12
"Make the ark, its poles, the mercy seat, and the veil that covers it."

### Exodus 35:13
"Make the table, its poles, all its dishes, and the bread of the presence."

### Exodus 35:14
"Make the lampstand for light, its tools, its lamps, and the oil for the light."

### Exodus 35:15
"Make the incense altar and its poles, the anointing oil, the sweet incense, and the curtain for the entrance of the tabernacle."

### Exodus 35:16
"Make the altar for burnt offerings, its bronze grate, its poles, all its tools, and the washbasin with its stand."

### Exodus 35:17
"Make the curtains for the courtyard, its posts and bases, and the curtain for the courtyard gate."

### Exodus 35:18
"Make the tent pegs for the tabernacle and the courtyard, and make their ropes."

### Exodus 35:19
"Make the woven clothes for serving in the holy place, the holy clothes for Aaron the priest, and the clothes for his sons when they serve as priests."

### Exodus 35:20
Then all the people of Israel left Moses.

### Exodus 35:21
Everyone whose heart was stirred and whose spirit was willing came and brought the Lord's offering. They brought gifts for the work on the meeting tent, for all its service, and for the holy clothes.

### Exodus 35:22
Men and women came, everyone with a willing heart. They brought brooches, earrings, rings, necklaces, and all kinds of gold jewelry. Everyone who gave gold offered it to the Lord.

### Exodus 35:23
Everyone who had blue, purple, or scarlet thread, fine linen, goats' hair, ram skins dyed red, or fine leather brought them.

### Exodus 35:24
Everyone who could give silver or bronze brought the Lord's offering. Everyone who had acacia wood for any part of the work brought it.

### Exodus 35:25
All the skilled women spun thread with their hands. They brought what they had spun: blue, purple, and scarlet thread, and fine linen.

### Exodus 35:26
All the women whose hearts stirred them with skill spun the goats' hair.

### Exodus 35:27
The leaders brought onyx stones and other stones to set in the ephod and the breastpiece.

### Exodus 35:28
They also brought spices and oil for the light, for the anointing oil, and for the sweet-smelling incense.

### Exodus 35:29
The people of Israel brought a willing offering to the Lord. Every man and woman whose heart made them willing brought what was needed for all the work the Lord had commanded through Moses.

### Exodus 35:30
Moses said to the people of Israel, "See, the Lord has chosen Bezalel by name. He is the son of Uri, the son of Hur, from the tribe of Judah."

### Exodus 35:31
"The Lord has filled him with the Spirit of God, with wisdom, understanding, knowledge, and every kind of skill."

### Exodus 35:32
"He can plan beautiful designs and work with gold, silver, and bronze."

### Exodus 35:33
"He can cut stones and set them in place. He can carve wood and do every kind of careful work."

### Exodus 35:34
"The Lord has placed in his heart the ability to teach, both him and Aholiab son of Ahisamach, from the tribe of Dan."

### Exodus 35:35
"He has filled them with skill to do every kind of work: engraving, designing, embroidering with blue, purple, and scarlet thread and fine linen, weaving, and planning beautiful work."
