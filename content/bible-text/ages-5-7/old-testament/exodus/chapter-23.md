# Exodus 23

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
23

## Verses

### Exodus 23:1
"You must not spread a false report. Do not join with a wicked person to be a dishonest witness."

### Exodus 23:2
"Do not follow a crowd to do evil. In a court case, do not speak with the crowd if it twists what is right."

### Exodus 23:3
"Do not show unfair favor to a poor person in his case."

### Exodus 23:4
"If you meet your enemy's ox or donkey wandering away, you must bring it back to him."

### Exodus 23:5
"If you see the donkey of someone who hates you lying down under its load, do not leave it there. You must help him with it."

### Exodus 23:6
"Do not twist justice against a poor person in his case."

### Exodus 23:7
"Stay far away from false charges. Do not put an innocent or righteous person to death, because I will not excuse the wicked."

### Exodus 23:8
"Do not take a bribe, because a bribe blinds people who see clearly and twists the words of righteous people."

### Exodus 23:9
"Do not crush a foreigner. You know the heart of a foreigner, because you were foreigners in the land of Egypt."

### Exodus 23:10
"For six years you must plant your land and gather its crops."

### Exodus 23:11
"But in the seventh year, let the land rest and lie still. Then the poor among your people may eat, and wild animals may eat what they leave. Do the same with your vineyard and olive trees."

### Exodus 23:12
"For six days you must do your work, but on the seventh day you must rest, so your ox and donkey may rest, and the son of your female servant and the foreigner may be refreshed."

### Exodus 23:13
"Pay careful attention to everything I have said to you. Do not speak the names of other gods, and do not let them be heard from your mouth."

### Exodus 23:14
"Three times each year you must keep a feast to me."

### Exodus 23:15
"Keep the Feast of Unleavened Bread. For seven days you must eat bread without yeast, as I commanded you, at the set time in the month of Abib, because in that month you came out of Egypt. No one must come before me empty-handed."

### Exodus 23:16
"Keep the Feast of Harvest with the first crops from what you planted in the field. Also keep the Feast of Gathering at the end of the year, when you gather in the fruit of your work from the field."

### Exodus 23:17
"Three times each year all your males must appear before the Lord God."

### Exodus 23:18
"Do not offer the blood of my sacrifice with bread that has yeast. Do not let the fat of my feast stay until morning."

### Exodus 23:19
"Bring the first of the firstfruits of your land into the house of the Lord your God. Do not boil a young goat in its mother's milk."

### Exodus 23:20
"See, I am sending an Angel before you to guard you on the way and bring you to the place I have prepared."

### Exodus 23:21
"Pay attention to him and obey his voice. Do not rebel against him, because he will not forgive your sins, for my name is in him."

### Exodus 23:22
"But if you truly obey his voice and do everything I say, then I will be an enemy to your enemies and stand against those who stand against you."

### Exodus 23:23
"My Angel will go before you and bring you to the Amorites, Hittites, Perizzites, Canaanites, Hivites, and Jebusites, and I will remove them."

### Exodus 23:24
"You must not bow down to their gods, serve them, or copy their ways. You must tear down their idols and break their sacred pillars."

### Exodus 23:25
"You must serve the Lord your God. He will bless your bread and water, and I will take sickness away from among you."

### Exodus 23:26
"No one in your land will lose a baby before birth or be unable to have children, and I will give you a full length of days."

### Exodus 23:27
"I will send fear of me ahead of you. I will throw into confusion all the people you meet, and I will make your enemies turn away from you."

### Exodus 23:28
"I will send hornets ahead of you, and they will drive out the Hivites, Canaanites, and Hittites before you."

### Exodus 23:29
"I will not drive them out before you in one year, or the land would become empty and wild animals would grow too many for you."

### Exodus 23:30
"Little by little I will drive them out before you, until your people grow many enough to receive the land."

### Exodus 23:31
"I will set your borders from the Red Sea to the sea of the Philistines, and from the desert to the River. I will give the people of the land into your hand, and you will drive them out before you."

### Exodus 23:32
"You must not make a covenant with them or with their gods."

### Exodus 23:33
"They must not live in your land, or they may make you sin against me. If you serve their gods, that will surely become a trap for you."
