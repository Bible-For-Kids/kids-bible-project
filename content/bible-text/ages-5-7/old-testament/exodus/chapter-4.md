# Exodus 4

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
4

## Verses

### Exodus 4:1
Moses answered, "But they will not believe me or listen to me. They will say, 'The Lord did not appear to you.'"

### Exodus 4:2
The Lord said to him, "What is that in your hand?" Moses said, "A staff."

### Exodus 4:3
The Lord said, "Throw it on the ground." Moses threw it on the ground, and it became a snake. Moses ran away from it.

### Exodus 4:4
The Lord said to Moses, "Reach out your hand and take it by the tail." Moses reached out and caught it, and it became a staff in his hand again.

### Exodus 4:5
The Lord said, "This is so they may believe that the Lord God of their fathers, the God of Abraham, the God of Isaac, and the God of Jacob, has appeared to you."

### Exodus 4:6
The Lord also said to him, "Put your hand inside your robe." Moses put his hand inside his robe. When he pulled it out, his hand had a disease and was white as snow.

### Exodus 4:7
Then the Lord said, "Put your hand back inside your robe." Moses put his hand back inside his robe. When he pulled it out again, it was healthy like the rest of his skin.

### Exodus 4:8
The Lord said, "If they do not believe you or listen to the first sign, they may believe the second sign.

### Exodus 4:9
"If they do not believe these two signs or listen to your voice, take water from the river and pour it on dry ground. The water you take from the river will become blood on the dry ground."

### Exodus 4:10
Moses said to the Lord, "My Lord, I am not good at speaking. I was not before, and I am still not, even after you have spoken to your servant. My speech is slow, and my words come slowly."

### Exodus 4:11
The Lord said to him, "Who made a person's mouth? Who makes a person unable to speak or hear, able to see or unable to see? Is it not I, the Lord?

### Exodus 4:12
"Now go. I will be with your mouth and teach you what to say."

### Exodus 4:13
Moses said, "My Lord, please send someone else."

### Exodus 4:14
Then the Lord became angry with Moses and said, "Is Aaron the Levite not your brother? I know he speaks well. He is already coming to meet you, and when he sees you, his heart will be glad.

### Exodus 4:15
"You will speak to him and put the words in his mouth. I will be with your mouth and with his mouth, and I will teach you what to do.

### Exodus 4:16
"Aaron will speak to the people for you. He will speak for you, and you will give him the words God gives you."

### Exodus 4:17
"Take this staff in your hand. With it you will do the signs."

### Exodus 4:18
Moses went back to Jethro, his father-in-law, and said, "Please let me go back to my relatives in Egypt and see if they are still alive." Jethro said to Moses, "Go in peace."

### Exodus 4:19
The Lord said to Moses in Midian, "Go back to Egypt, because all the men who wanted to kill you are dead."

### Exodus 4:20
Moses took his wife and his sons, put them on a donkey, and went back toward Egypt. Moses carried the staff of God in his hand.

### Exodus 4:21
The Lord said to Moses, "When you return to Egypt, do all the wonders before Pharaoh that I have put in your hand. But I will harden his heart, and he will not let the people go.

### Exodus 4:22
"You must say to Pharaoh, 'This is what the Lord says: Israel is my son, my firstborn.

### Exodus 4:23
"I say to you, let my son go, so he may serve me. If you refuse to let him go, I will kill your son, your firstborn.'"

### Exodus 4:24
On the way, at a place where they stopped for the night, the Lord met Moses and sought to kill him.

### Exodus 4:25
Then Zipporah took a sharp stone and circumcised her son. She touched Moses' feet with it and said, "Surely you are a husband of blood to me."

### Exodus 4:26
So the Lord let Moses go. Then Zipporah said, "A husband of blood," because of the circumcision.

### Exodus 4:27
The Lord said to Aaron, "Go into the wilderness to meet Moses." Aaron went, met Moses at the mountain of God, and kissed him.

### Exodus 4:28
Moses told Aaron all the words of the Lord, who had sent him. He also told Aaron about all the signs the Lord had commanded him to do.

### Exodus 4:29
Moses and Aaron went and gathered all the elders of the children of Israel.

### Exodus 4:30
Aaron spoke all the words that the Lord had spoken to Moses, and he did the signs where the people could see them.

### Exodus 4:31
The people believed. When they heard that the Lord had visited the children of Israel and had seen their suffering, they bowed their heads and worshiped.
