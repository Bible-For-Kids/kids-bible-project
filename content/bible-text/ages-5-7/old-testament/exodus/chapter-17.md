# Exodus 17

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
17

## Verses

### Exodus 17:1
All the people of Israel traveled from the wilderness of Sin, moving as the Lord commanded. They camped at Rephidim, but there was no water for the people to drink.

### Exodus 17:2
The people argued with Moses and said, "Give us water to drink." Moses said, "Why are you arguing with me? Why are you testing the Lord?"

### Exodus 17:3
The people were very thirsty there, and they grumbled against Moses. They said, "Why did you bring us out of Egypt? Was it to kill us, our children, and our animals with thirst?"

### Exodus 17:4
Moses cried out to the Lord, "What should I do with these people? They are almost ready to stone me."

### Exodus 17:5
The Lord said to Moses, "Go ahead of the people. Take some of Israel's elders with you, and take in your hand the staff you used to strike the river."

### Exodus 17:6
"I will stand before you there on the rock at Horeb. Strike the rock, and water will come out of it so the people may drink." Moses did this while the elders of Israel watched.

### Exodus 17:7
Moses named the place Massah and Meribah, because the people of Israel argued and tested the Lord by saying, "Is the Lord among us or not?"

### Exodus 17:8
Then Amalek came and fought against Israel at Rephidim.

### Exodus 17:9
Moses said to Joshua, "Choose men for us and go fight Amalek. Tomorrow I will stand on top of the hill with God's staff in my hand."

### Exodus 17:10
Joshua did what Moses said and fought Amalek. Moses, Aaron, and Hur went up to the top of the hill.

### Exodus 17:11
As Moses held his hand up high, Israel was winning. But whenever his hand came down, Amalek started winning.

### Exodus 17:12
But Moses' hands got heavy, and he grew tired. So Aaron and Hur brought a stone for him to sit on. Then they stood beside him, one on each side, and held up his hands until the sun went down.

### Exodus 17:13
So Joshua defeated Amalek and his people with the sword.

### Exodus 17:14
The Lord said to Moses, "Write this in a book so it will be remembered, and tell Joshua: I will completely wipe out the memory of Amalek from under heaven."

### Exodus 17:15
Moses built an altar and named it, "The Lord Is My Banner."

### Exodus 17:16
He said, "Because a hand was lifted against the Lord's throne, the Lord will be at war with Amalek from generation to generation."
