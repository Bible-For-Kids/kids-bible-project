# Exodus 40

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
40

## Verses

### Exodus 40:1
The Lord spoke to Moses.

### Exodus 40:2
He said, "On the first day of the first month, set up the holy tent, the tent of meeting."

### Exodus 40:3
"Put the ark of the testimony inside it, and cover the ark with the curtain."

### Exodus 40:4
"Bring in the table and arrange everything that belongs on it. Bring in the lampstand and light its lamps."

### Exodus 40:5
"Set the gold altar for sweet-smelling incense in front of the ark of the testimony, and hang the curtain at the entrance of the holy tent."

### Exodus 40:6
"Place the altar for burned offerings in front of the entrance to the holy tent, the tent of meeting."

### Exodus 40:7
"Place the basin between the tent of meeting and the altar, and put water in it."

### Exodus 40:8
"Set up the courtyard all around, and hang the curtain at the courtyard gate."

### Exodus 40:9
"Take the special setting-apart oil and set the holy tent and everything inside it apart with oil. Set it apart with all its furnishings, and it will be holy."

### Exodus 40:10
"Set the altar for burned offerings and all its tools apart with oil. Set the altar apart, and it will be most holy."

### Exodus 40:11
"Set the basin and its stand apart with oil."

### Exodus 40:12
"Bring Aaron and his sons to the entrance of the tent of meeting, and wash them with water."

### Exodus 40:13
"Dress Aaron in the holy clothes. Set him apart with oil, so he may serve me as priest."

### Exodus 40:14
"Bring his sons and dress them in long shirts."

### Exodus 40:15
"Set them apart with oil as you set their father apart with oil, so they may serve me as priests. Their setting apart will mark a lasting priesthood through their generations."

### Exodus 40:16
Moses did everything just as the Lord commanded him.

### Exodus 40:17
In the first month of the second year, on the first day of the month, the holy tent was set up.

### Exodus 40:18
Moses set up the holy tent. He put its bases in place, set up its boards, put in its bars, and raised its pillars.

### Exodus 40:19
He spread the tent over the holy tent and put the covering on top of the tent, just as the Lord commanded Moses.

### Exodus 40:20
He took the testimony and put it inside the ark. He set the poles on the ark and placed the cover of the ark on top of it.

### Exodus 40:21
He brought the ark into the holy tent, hung the curtain, and covered the ark of the testimony, just as the Lord commanded Moses.

### Exodus 40:22
He put the table in the tent of meeting on the north side of the holy tent, outside the curtain.

### Exodus 40:23
He arranged the bread on it before the Lord, just as the Lord commanded Moses.

### Exodus 40:24
He put the lampstand in the tent of meeting, across from the table, on the south side of the holy tent.

### Exodus 40:25
He lit the lamps before the Lord, just as the Lord commanded Moses.

### Exodus 40:26
He put the gold altar in the tent of meeting in front of the curtain.

### Exodus 40:27
He burned sweet-smelling incense on it, just as the Lord commanded Moses.

### Exodus 40:28
He hung the curtain at the entrance of the holy tent.

### Exodus 40:29
He placed the altar for burned offerings by the entrance of the holy tent, the tent of meeting. He offered the burned offering and the grain gift on it, just as the Lord commanded Moses.

### Exodus 40:30
He placed the basin between the tent of meeting and the altar and put water in it for washing.

### Exodus 40:31
Moses, Aaron, and Aaron's sons washed their hands and feet there.

### Exodus 40:32
Whenever they went into the tent of meeting or came near the altar, they washed, just as the Lord commanded Moses.

### Exodus 40:33
Moses set up the courtyard around the holy tent and the altar, and he hung the curtain at the courtyard gate. So Moses finished the work.

### Exodus 40:34
Then a cloud covered the tent of meeting, and the glory of the Lord filled the holy tent.

### Exodus 40:35
Moses could not enter the tent of meeting because the cloud stayed on it, and the glory of the Lord filled the holy tent.

### Exodus 40:36
Whenever the cloud lifted from above the holy tent, the Israelites traveled on through their journeys.

### Exodus 40:37
But if the cloud did not lift, they did not travel until the day it lifted.

### Exodus 40:38
The cloud of the Lord was over the holy tent by day, and fire was in the cloud by night. All the house of Israel could see it throughout all their journeys.
