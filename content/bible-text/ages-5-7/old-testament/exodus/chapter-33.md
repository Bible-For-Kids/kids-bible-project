# Exodus 33

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
33

## Verses

### Exodus 33:1
The Lord said to Moses, "Leave this place. You and the people you brought out of Egypt must go up to the land I promised to Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob. I said, 'I will give it to your children.'"

### Exodus 33:2
"I will send an angel ahead of you. I will drive out the Canaanites, Amorites, Hittites, Perizzites, Hivites, and Jebusites."

### Exodus 33:3
"Go to a land flowing with milk and honey. But I will not go up among you, because you are stubborn people, and I might destroy you on the way."

### Exodus 33:4
When the people heard this sad news, they mourned. No one put on their jewelry.

### Exodus 33:5
The Lord had said to Moses, "Tell the people of Israel, 'You are stubborn people. If I went among you for even a moment, I might destroy you. Now take off your jewelry, and I will decide what to do with you.'"

### Exodus 33:6
So the people of Israel took off their jewelry by Mount Horeb.

### Exodus 33:7
Moses used to take the tent and set it up outside the camp, far away from the camp. He called it the meeting tent. Everyone who wanted to seek the Lord went out to the meeting tent outside the camp.

### Exodus 33:8
Whenever Moses went out to the tent, all the people would rise and stand at the doors of their own tents. They watched Moses until he went inside the meeting tent.

### Exodus 33:9
When Moses entered the tent, the pillar of cloud came down and stood at the door of the tent, and the Lord spoke with Moses.

### Exodus 33:10
All the people saw the pillar of cloud standing at the tent door. Then each person stood and worshiped at the door of their own tent.

### Exodus 33:11
The Lord spoke to Moses face to face, as a man speaks with his friend. Then Moses returned to the camp. But his helper Joshua, son of Nun, a young man, did not leave the tent.

### Exodus 33:12
Moses said to the Lord, "You tell me, 'Bring up this people,' but you have not told me whom you will send with me. You have said, 'I know you by name, and you have found favor with me.'"

### Exodus 33:13
"Now if I have found favor with you, please show me your way. Then I may know you and keep finding favor with you. Remember that this nation is your people."

### Exodus 33:14
The Lord said, "My presence will go with you, and I will give you rest."

### Exodus 33:15
Moses said to him, "If your presence does not go with us, do not take us up from here."

### Exodus 33:16
"How will anyone know that I and your people have found favor with you? It will be because you go with us. That is what makes me and your people different from all the other people on the earth."

### Exodus 33:17
The Lord said to Moses, "I will also do this thing you have asked, because you have found favor with me, and I know you by name."

### Exodus 33:18
Moses said, "Please show me your glory."

### Exodus 33:19
The Lord said, "I will make all my goodness pass before you, and I will speak the name of the Lord before you. I will be gracious to whom I will be gracious, and I will show mercy to whom I will show mercy."

### Exodus 33:20
He said, "You cannot see my face, because no person can see me and live."

### Exodus 33:21
The Lord said, "There is a place beside me where you will stand on a rock."

### Exodus 33:22
"While my glory passes by, I will put you in a cleft of the rock and cover you with my hand until I have passed by."

### Exodus 33:23
"Then I will take away my hand, and you will see my back. But my face must not be seen."
