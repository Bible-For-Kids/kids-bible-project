# Numbers 5

## Book
Numbers

## Chapter
5

## Verses

### Numbers 5:1
The Lord spoke to Moses, saying,

### Numbers 5:2
"Command the people of Israel that they put out of the camp every person with a serious disease, everyone who has a discharge, and whoever is unclean because of a dead body.

### Numbers 5:3
You must put both male and female outside of the camp so that they don't make their camp unclean, in the midst of which I live."

### Numbers 5:4
The people of Israel did so, and put them outside of the camp; as the Lord spoke to Moses, so the people of Israel did.

### Numbers 5:5
The Lord spoke to Moses, saying,

### Numbers 5:6
"Speak to the people of Israel: 'When a man or woman commits any sin that people commit, so as to act unfaithfully against the Lord, and that person is guilty,

### Numbers 5:7
then he must confess his sin which he has done; and he must make restitution for his guilt in full, add to it the fifth part of it, and give it to him against whom he has done wrong.

### Numbers 5:8
But if the man has no relative to whom restitution may be made for the guilt, the restitution for guilt which is made to the Lord must be the priest's, in addition to the male sheep of the atonement, by which atonement must be made for him.

### Numbers 5:9
Every special offering of all the holy things of the people of Israel, which they present to the priest, must be his.

### Numbers 5:10
Every man's holy things must be his; whatever any man gives the priest, it must be his.' "

### Numbers 5:11
The Lord spoke to Moses, saying,

### Numbers 5:12
"Speak to the people of Israel, and tell them: 'If any man's wife goes astray and is unfaithful to him,

### Numbers 5:13
and a man has sexual relations with her, and it is hidden from the eyes of her husband and this is kept concealed, and she is made unclean, there is no witness against her, and she isn't taken in the act;

### Numbers 5:14
and the spirit of jealousy comes on him, and he is jealous of his wife and she is made unclean; or if the spirit of jealousy comes on him, and he is jealous of his wife and she isn't made unclean;

### Numbers 5:15
then the man must bring his wife to the priest, and must bring her offering for her: one tenth-measure of barley meal. He must pour no oil on it, nor put frankincense on it, for it is a grain gift of jealousy, a grain gift of memorial, bringing sin to memory.

### Numbers 5:16
The priest must bring her near, and bring her before the Lord.

### Numbers 5:17
The priest must take holy water in a clay bowl; and the priest must take some of the dust that is on the floor of the tent and put it into the water.

### Numbers 5:18
The priest must bring the woman before the Lord, and let the hair of the woman's head go loose, and put the grain gift of memorial in her hands, which is the grain gift of jealousy. The priest must have in his hand the bitter water that brings a curse.

### Numbers 5:19
The priest must cause her to take an oath and must tell the woman, "If no man has had sexual relations with you, and if you haven't gone aside to being unclean, while you are married to your husband, be free from this bitter water that brings a curse.

### Numbers 5:20
But if you have gone astray, while you are married to your husband, and if you are made unclean, and another man has had sexual relations with you besides your husband-"

### Numbers 5:21
then the priest must cause the woman to swear with the oath of cursing, and the priest must tell the woman, "May the Lord make your case a warning and an oath among your people, when the Lord allows your thigh to waste away, and your body to swell;

### Numbers 5:22
and this water that brings a curse will go into your body, and make your body swell and make your thigh waste away." The woman must say, "Amen, Amen."

### Numbers 5:23
"The priest must write these curses in a book, and he must wipe them into the bitter water.

### Numbers 5:24
He must make the woman drink the bitter water connected with the curse; and the water connected with the curse must enter into her and become bitter.

### Numbers 5:25
The priest must take the grain gift of jealousy out of the woman's hand, and must lift the grain gift before the Lord, and bring it to the altar.

### Numbers 5:26
The priest must take a handful of the grain gift, as its memorial portion, and burn it on the altar, and afterward must make the woman drink the water.

### Numbers 5:27
When he has made her drink the water, if she is made unclean and has acted unfaithfully against her husband, the water connected with the curse will enter into her and become bitter, and her body will swell, and her thigh will waste away; and the woman will be a curse among her people.

### Numbers 5:28
If the woman isn't made unclean, but is clean; then she will be free, and will conceive children.

### Numbers 5:29
"This is the law of jealousy, when a wife, while she is married to her husband, goes astray, and is made unclean,

### Numbers 5:30
or when the spirit of jealousy comes on a man, and he is jealous of his wife; then he must bring the woman before the Lord, and the priest must carry out this whole law for her.

### Numbers 5:31
The man must be free from sin, and that woman must bear her sin.' "
