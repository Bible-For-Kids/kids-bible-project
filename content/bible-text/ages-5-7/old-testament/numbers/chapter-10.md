# Numbers 10

## Book
Numbers

## Chapter
10

## Verses

### Numbers 10:1
The Lord spoke to Moses, saying,

### Numbers 10:2
"Make two trumpets of silver. You must make them of beaten work. You must use them for the calling of the whole group, and for the journeying of the camps.

### Numbers 10:3
When they blow them, all the people must gather themselves to you at the door of the Tent of Meeting.

### Numbers 10:4
If they blow just one, then the leaders, the leaders of the thousands of Israel, must gather themselves to you.

### Numbers 10:5
When you blow an alarm, the camps that lie on the east side must go forward.

### Numbers 10:6
When you blow an alarm the second time, the camps that lie on the south side must go forward. They must blow an alarm for their travels.

### Numbers 10:7
But when the whole group is to be gathered together, you must blow, but you must not sound an alarm.

### Numbers 10:8
"The sons of Aaron, the priests, must blow the trumpets. This will be a rule for you forever for all your generations.

### Numbers 10:9
When you go to war in your land against an enemy who presses hard against you, sound the alarm with the trumpets. Then the Lord your God will remember you and save you from your enemies.

### Numbers 10:10
"On your glad days, at your set feasts, and at the beginning of each month, blow the trumpets over your burned offerings and peace offerings. They will be a reminder for you before your God. I am the Lord your God."

### Numbers 10:11
In the second year, in the second month, on the twentieth day of the month, the cloud was taken up from over the tent of the covenant.

### Numbers 10:12
The people of Israel went forward on their travels out of the wilderness of Sinai; and the cloud stayed in the wilderness of Paran.

### Numbers 10:13
They first went forward as the Lord commanded through Moses.

### Numbers 10:14
First, the banner of the camp of Judah went forward according to their armies. Nahshon the son of Amminadab was over his army.

### Numbers 10:15
Nethanel the son of Zuar was over the army of the tribe of Issachar.

### Numbers 10:16
Eliab the son of Helon was over the army of the tribe of Zebulun.

### Numbers 10:17
The tent was taken down; and the sons of Gershon and the sons of Merari, who bore the tent, went forward.

### Numbers 10:18
The banner of the camp of Reuben went forward according to their armies. Elizur the son of Shedeur was over his army.

### Numbers 10:19
Shelumiel the son of Zurishaddai was over the army of the tribe of Simeon.

### Numbers 10:20
Eliasaph the son of Deuel was over the army of the tribe of Gad.

### Numbers 10:21
The Kohathites set forward, bearing the holy place. The others set up the tent before they arrived.

### Numbers 10:22
The banner of the camp of Ephraim set forward according to their armies. Elishama the son of Ammihud was over his army.

### Numbers 10:23
Gamaliel the son of Pedahzur was over the army of the tribe of Manasseh.

### Numbers 10:24
Abidan the son of Gideoni was over the army of the tribe of Benjamin.

### Numbers 10:25
The banner of the camp of Dan, which was the rear guard of all the camps, set forward according to their armies. Ahiezer the son of Ammishaddai was over his army.

### Numbers 10:26
Pagiel the son of Ochran was over the army of the tribe of Asher.

### Numbers 10:27
Ahira the son of Enan was over the army of the tribe of Naphtali.

### Numbers 10:28
Thus were the travels of the people of Israel according to their armies; and they went forward.

### Numbers 10:29
Moses said to Hobab, the son of Reuel the Midianite, Moses' father-in-law, "We are journeying to the place of which the Lord said, 'I will give it to you.' Come with us, and we will treat you well; for the Lord has spoken good concerning Israel."

### Numbers 10:30
He said to him, "I will not go; but I will depart to my own land, and to my relatives."

### Numbers 10:31
Moses said, "Don't leave us, please; because you know how we are to camp in the wilderness, and you can be our eyes.

### Numbers 10:32
If you go with us, whatever good the Lord does to us, we will do the same to you."

### Numbers 10:33
They set forward from the Mount of the Lord three days' travel. The ark of the Lord's covenant went before them three days' travel, to seek out a resting place for them.

### Numbers 10:34
The cloud of the Lord was over them by day, when they set forward from the camp.

### Numbers 10:35
When the ark went forward, Moses said, "Rise up, the Lord, and let your enemies be scattered! Let those who hate you flee before you!"

### Numbers 10:36
When it rested, he said, "Return, the Lord, to the ten thousands of the thousands of Israel."
