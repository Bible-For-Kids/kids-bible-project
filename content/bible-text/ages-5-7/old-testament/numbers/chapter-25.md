# Numbers 25

## Book
Numbers

## Chapter
25

## Verses

### Numbers 25:1
Israel stayed at Shittim, and the people began to act as only husbands and wives should with the daughters of Moab.

### Numbers 25:2
The Moabite women invited the people to the sacrifices for their gods. The people ate and bowed down to those gods.

### Numbers 25:3
Israel joined himself to Baal Peor, and the Lord's anger burned against Israel.

### Numbers 25:4
The Lord said to Moses, "Take all the chiefs of the people who joined this sin, and put them to death before the Lord in the open sunlight, so the fierce anger of the Lord may turn away from Israel."

### Numbers 25:5
Moses said to the judges of Israel, "Each of you must put to death the men under you who joined themselves to Baal Peor."

### Numbers 25:6
One of the people of Israel came and brought to his brothers a Midianite woman in the sight of Moses, and in the sight of all the people of Israel, while they were weeping at the door of the Tent of Meeting.

### Numbers 25:7
When Phinehas, the son of Eleazar, the son of Aaron the priest, saw it, he rose up from the middle of the whole group, and took a spear in his hand.

### Numbers 25:8
He followed the Israelite man into the tent and put both the man and the woman to death there. Then the terrible sickness stopped among the people of Israel.

### Numbers 25:9
Those who died by the terrible sickness were twenty-four thousand.

### Numbers 25:10
The Lord spoke to Moses, saying,

### Numbers 25:11
"Phinehas, son of Eleazar and grandson of Aaron the priest, has turned my burning anger away from the people of Israel. He was jealous for my honor among them, so I did not destroy the people of Israel in my jealousy.

### Numbers 25:12
Therefore say, 'I give to him my covenant of peace.

### Numbers 25:13
It will be for him, and for his children after him, the covenant of an everlasting priesthood, because he was jealous for his God, and made atonement for the people of Israel.' "

### Numbers 25:14
Now the name of the man of Israel who was killed with the Midianite woman, was Zimri, the son of Salu, a leader of a family group among the Simeonites.

### Numbers 25:15
The name of the Midianite woman who was killed was Cozbi, the daughter of Zur. He was head of the people of a family group in Midian.

### Numbers 25:16
The Lord spoke to Moses, saying,

### Numbers 25:17
"Treat the Midianites as enemies and strike them.

### Numbers 25:18
They treated you as enemies with their tricks. They deceived you in the matter of Peor and in the matter of Cozbi, the daughter of a Midianite leader, their sister, who was killed on the day of the terrible sickness at Peor."
