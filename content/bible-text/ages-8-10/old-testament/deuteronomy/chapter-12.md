# Deuteronomy 12

## Book
Deuteronomy

## Chapter
12

## Verses

### Deuteronomy 12:1
These are the rules and the commands which you will be careful to do in the land that the Lord, the God of your ancestors, has given you to possess all the days that you live on the earth.

### Deuteronomy 12:2
You must surely destroy all the places in which the nations that you must dispossess served their gods: on the high mountains, and on the hills, and under every green tree.

### Deuteronomy 12:3
You must break down their altars, dash their pillars in pieces, and burn their Asherah pole poles with fire. You must cut down the engraved images of their gods. You must destroy their name out of that place.

### Deuteronomy 12:4
You must not do so to the Lord your God.

### Deuteronomy 12:5
But to the place that the Lord your God must choose out of all your tribes, to put his name there, you must seek his habitation, and you will come there.

### Deuteronomy 12:6
You must bring your burned offerings, your sacrifices, your tithes, the offering lifted before the Lord of your hand, your vows, your free will offerings, and the firstborn of your herd and of your flock there.

### Deuteronomy 12:7
There you must eat before the Lord your God, and you must rejoice in all that you put your hand to, you and your households, in that the Lord your God has blessed you.

### Deuteronomy 12:8
You must not do all the things that we do here today, every person whatever is right in his own eyes;

### Deuteronomy 12:9
for you haven't yet come to the rest and to the inheritance that the Lord your God gives you.

### Deuteronomy 12:10
But when you go over the Jordan and live in the land that the Lord your God causes you to receive as an inheritance, and he gives you rest from all your enemies around you, so that you live in safety,

### Deuteronomy 12:11
then it must happen that to the place that the Lord your God must choose, to cause his name to live there, there you must bring all that I command you: your burned offerings, your sacrifices, your tithes, the offering lifted before the Lord of your hand, and all your choice vows which you vow to the Lord.

### Deuteronomy 12:12
You must rejoice before the Lord your God-you, and your sons, your daughters, your male servants, your female servants, and the Levite who is in your towns, because he has no portion nor inheritance with you.

### Deuteronomy 12:13
Be careful that you don't offer your burned offerings in every place that you see;

### Deuteronomy 12:14
but in the place that the Lord chooses in one of your tribes, there you must offer your burned offerings, and there you must do all that I command you.

### Deuteronomy 12:15
Yet you may kill and eat meat within all your gates, as much as you desire, according to the Lord your God's blessing which he has given you. The unclean and the clean may eat of it, as of the gazelle and the deer.

### Deuteronomy 12:16
Only you must not eat the blood. You must pour it out on the earth like water.

### Deuteronomy 12:17
You may not eat in your towns the tithe of your grain, or of your new wine, or of your oil, or the firstborn of your herd or of your flock, nor any of your vows which you vow, nor your free will offerings, nor the offering lifted before the Lord of your hand;

### Deuteronomy 12:18
but you must eat them before the Lord your God in the place that the Lord your God must choose: you, your son, your daughter, your male servant, your female servant, and the Levite who is in your towns. You must rejoice before the Lord your God in all that you put your hand to.

### Deuteronomy 12:19
Be careful that you don't forsake the Levite as long as you live in your land.

### Deuteronomy 12:20
When the Lord your God enlarges your border, as he has promised you, and you say, "I want to eat meat," because you want to eat meat, you may eat meat, as much as you desire.

### Deuteronomy 12:21
If the place that the Lord your God must choose to put his name is too far from you, then you must kill of your herd and of your flock, that the Lord has given you, as I have commanded you; and you may eat in your towns, as much as you desire.

### Deuteronomy 12:22
Even as the gazelle and as the deer is eaten, so you must eat of it. The unclean and the clean may eat of it alike.

### Deuteronomy 12:23
Only be sure that you don't eat the blood; for the blood is the life. You must not eat the life with the meat.

### Deuteronomy 12:24
You must not eat it. You must pour it out on the earth like water.

### Deuteronomy 12:25
You must not eat it, so things may go well with you and with your children after you, when you do what is right in the Lord's eyes.

### Deuteronomy 12:26
Only your holy things which you have, and your vows, you must take and go to the place that the Lord must choose.

### Deuteronomy 12:27
You must offer your burned offerings, the meat and the blood, on the Lord your God's altar. The blood of your sacrifices will be poured out on the Lord your God's altar, and you must eat the meat.

### Deuteronomy 12:28
Keep and hear all these words which I command you, so things may go well with you and with your children after you forever, when you do what is good and right in the Lord your God's eyes.

### Deuteronomy 12:29
When the Lord your God cuts off the nations from before you where you go in to dispossess them, and you dispossess them and live in their land,

### Deuteronomy 12:30
be careful that you are not ensnared to follow them after they are destroyed from before you, and that you not inquire after their gods, saying, "How do these nations serve their gods? I will do likewise."

### Deuteronomy 12:31
You must not do so to the Lord your God; for every detestable thing to the Lord, which he hates, they have done to their gods; for they even burn their sons and their daughters in the fire to their gods.

### Deuteronomy 12:32
Whatever thing I command you, that you will be careful to do. You must not add to it, nor take away from it.
