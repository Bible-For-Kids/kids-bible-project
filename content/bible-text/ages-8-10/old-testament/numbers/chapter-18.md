# Numbers 18

## Book
Numbers

## Chapter
18

## Verses

### Numbers 18:1
The Lord said to Aaron, "You and your sons and your family house with you must bear the sin of the holy place; and you and your sons with you must bear the sin of your priesthood.

### Numbers 18:2
Bring your brothers also, the tribe of Levi, the tribe of your father, near with you, that they may be joined to you, and minister to you; but you and your sons with you must be before the Tent of the Testimony.

### Numbers 18:3
They must keep your commands and the duty of the whole Tent; only they must not come near to the holy tent tools and to the altar, that they not die, neither they nor you.

### Numbers 18:4
They must be joined to you and keep the responsibility of the Tent of Meeting, for all the service of the Tent. A foreigner must not come near to you.

### Numbers 18:5
"You must perform the duty of the holy place and the duty of the altar, that there be no more wrath on the people of Israel.

### Numbers 18:6
I myself have taken your brothers the Levites from among the people of Israel. They are a gift to you, dedicated to the Lord, to do the service of the Tent of Meeting.

### Numbers 18:7
You and your sons with you must keep your priesthood for everything of the altar, and for that within the curtain. You must serve. I give you the service of the priesthood as a gift. The foreigner who comes near must be put to death."

### Numbers 18:8
The Lord spoke to Aaron, "I myself have given you the command of my offerings lifted before the Lord, even all the holy things of the people of Israel. I have given them to you by reason of the anointing, and to your sons, as a portion forever.

### Numbers 18:9
This must be yours of the most holy things from the fire: every offering of theirs, even every grain offering of theirs, and every sin offering of theirs, and every guilt offering of theirs, which they must render to me, must be most holy for you and for your sons.

### Numbers 18:10
You must eat of it like the most holy things. Every male must eat of it. It will be holy to you.

### Numbers 18:11
"This is yours, too: the offering lifted before the Lord of their gift, even all the offerings lifted before the Lord of the people of Israel. I have given them to you, and to your sons and to your daughters with you, as a portion forever. Everyone who is clean in your house must eat of it.

### Numbers 18:12
"I have given to you all the best of the oil, all the best of the vintage, and of the grain, the first fruits of them which they give to the Lord.

### Numbers 18:13
The first-ripe fruits of all that is in their land, which they bring to the Lord, must be yours. Everyone who is clean in your house must eat of it.

### Numbers 18:14
"Everything devoted in Israel must be yours.

### Numbers 18:15
Every firstborn that opens the womb, which they offer to the Lord, whether human or animal, must be yours. Nevertheless, you must surely redeem the firstborn of man, and you must redeem the firstborn of unclean animals.

### Numbers 18:16
You must redeem those who are to be redeemed of them from a month old, according to your estimation, for five silver pieces, by the holy place weight.

### Numbers 18:17
"But you must not redeem the firstborn of a cow, or the firstborn of a sheep, or the firstborn of a goat. They are holy. You must sprinkle their blood on the altar, and must burn their fat for an offering burned on the altar, for a pleasing smell to the Lord.

### Numbers 18:18
Their meat must be yours, as the offering lifted before the Lord breast and as the right thigh, it must be yours.

### Numbers 18:19
All the offerings lifted before the Lord of the holy things which the people of Israel offer to the Lord, I have given you and your sons and your daughters with you, as a portion forever. It is a covenant of salt forever before the Lord to you and to your offspring with you."

### Numbers 18:20
The Lord said to Aaron, "You must have no inheritance in their land, neither must you have any portion among them. I am your portion and your inheritance among the people of Israel.

### Numbers 18:21
"To the descendants of Levi, I have given all the tithe in Israel for an inheritance, in return for their service which they serve, even the service of the Tent of Meeting.

### Numbers 18:22
Henceforth the people of Israel must not come near the Tent of Meeting, or else they bear sin, and die.

### Numbers 18:23
But the Levites must do the service of the Tent of Meeting, and they must bear their sin. It will be a lasting rule for all your generations. Among the people of Israel, they must have no inheritance.

### Numbers 18:24
For the tithe of the people of Israel, which they offer as an offering lifted before the Lord to the Lord, I have given to the Levites for an inheritance. Therefore I have said to them, 'Among the people of Israel they must have no inheritance.' "

### Numbers 18:25
The Lord spoke to Moses, saying,

### Numbers 18:26
"Moreover you must speak to the Levites, and tell them, 'When you take of the people of Israel the tithe which I have given you from them for your inheritance, then you must offer up an offering lifted before the Lord of it for the Lord, a tithe of the tithe.

### Numbers 18:27
Your offering lifted before the Lord must be credited to you, as though it were the grain of the threshing floor, and as the fullness of the wine press.

### Numbers 18:28
Thus you also must offer an offering lifted before the Lord to the Lord of all your tithes, which you receive of the people of Israel; and of it you must give the Lord's offering lifted before the Lord to Aaron the priest.

### Numbers 18:29
Out of all your gifts, you must offer every offering lifted before the Lord to the Lord, of all its best parts, even the holy part of it.

### Numbers 18:30
"Therefore you must tell them, 'When you heave its best from it, then it must be credited to the Levites as the increase of the threshing floor, and as the increase of the wine press.

### Numbers 18:31
You may eat it anywhere, you and your households, for it is your reward in return for your service in the Tent of Meeting.

### Numbers 18:32
You must bear no sin by reason of it, when you have heaved from it its best. You must not profane the holy things of the people of Israel, that you not die.' "
