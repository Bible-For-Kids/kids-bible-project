# 2 Kings 2

## Book
2 Kings

## Chapter
2

## Verses

### 2 Kings 2:1
When the Lord was about to take Elijah up into heaven by a whirlwind, Elijah and Elisha were walking together from Gilgal.

### 2 Kings 2:2
Elijah said to Elisha, "Please stay here, for the Lord has sent me as far as Bethel." Elisha said, "As the Lord lives, and as you live, I will not leave you." So they went down to Bethel.

### 2 Kings 2:3
The company of prophets at Bethel came out to Elisha and said, "Do you know that the Lord will take your master away from you today?" Elisha said, "Yes, I know. Be quiet."

### 2 Kings 2:4
Elijah said to him, "Elisha, please wait here, for the Lord has sent me to Jericho." He said, "As the Lord lives, and as your soul lives, I will not leave you." So they came to Jericho.

### 2 Kings 2:5
The company of prophets who were at Jericho came near to Elisha, and said to him, "Do you know that the Lord will take away your master away from you today?" He answered, "Yes, I know it. Be quiet."

### 2 Kings 2:6
Elijah said to him, "Please wait here, for the Lord has sent me to the Jordan." He said, "As the Lord lives, and as your soul lives, I will not leave you." Then they both went on.

### 2 Kings 2:7
Fifty men of the company of prophets went, and stood opposite them at a distance; and they both stood by the Jordan.

### 2 Kings 2:8
Elijah took his cloak, rolled it up, and struck the water. The water divided to the right and to the left, and the two men crossed on dry ground.

### 2 Kings 2:9
When they had crossed over, Elijah said to Elisha, "Ask what I should do for you before I am taken from you." Elisha said, "Please let me receive a double portion of your spirit."

### 2 Kings 2:10
He said, "You have asked a hard thing. If you see me when I am taken from you, it will be so for you; but if not, it will not be so."

### 2 Kings 2:11
As they walked and talked, look, a chariot of fire and horses of fire came between them. Elijah went up by a whirlwind into heaven.

### 2 Kings 2:12
Elisha saw it and cried, "My father, my father! The chariots of Israel and its horsemen!" Then he saw Elijah no more. Elisha took hold of his own clothes and tore them in two in grief.

### 2 Kings 2:13
He also took up Elijah's cloak that fell from him, and went back, and stood by the bank of the Jordan.

### 2 Kings 2:14
Elisha took Elijah's cloak that had fallen from him and struck the water. He said, "Where is the Lord, the God of Elijah?" When he struck the water, it divided to the right and to the left, and Elisha crossed over.

### 2 Kings 2:15
When the company of prophets who were at Jericho facing him saw him, they said, "The spirit of Elijah rests on Elisha." They came to meet him, and bowed themselves to the ground before him.

### 2 Kings 2:16
They said to him, "See now, there are with your servants fifty strong men. Please let them go and seek your master. Perhaps the Lord's Spirit has taken him up, and put him on some mountain, or into some valley." He said, "Don't send them."

### 2 Kings 2:17
When they urged him until he was ashamed, he said, "Send them." Therefore they sent fifty men; and they searched for three days, but didn't find him.

### 2 Kings 2:18
They came back to him, while he stayed at Jericho; and he said to them, "Didn't I tell you, 'Don't go?' "

### 2 Kings 2:19
The men of the city said to Elisha, "Look, please, the situation of this city is pleasant, as my lord sees; but the water is bad, and the land is barren."

### 2 Kings 2:20
He said, "Bring me a new jar, and put salt in it." Then they brought it to him.

### 2 Kings 2:21
Elisha went out to the spring and threw salt into it. He said, "The Lord says, 'I have healed this water. From now on it will not bring death or make the land unable to grow food.'"

### 2 Kings 2:22
So the waters were healed to this day, according to Elisha's word.

### 2 Kings 2:23
Elisha went up from there to Bethel. As he went along the road, some boys came out of the city and mocked him, saying, "Go up, bald head! Go up, bald head!"

### 2 Kings 2:24
Elisha turned around, looked at them, and pronounced the Lord's curse on them. Then two female bears came out of the woods and attacked forty-two of the boys.

### 2 Kings 2:25
He went from there to Mount Carmel, and from there he returned to Samaria.
