# Joshua 19

## Book
Joshua

## Chapter
19

## Verses

### Joshua 19:1
The second lot came out for Simeon, even for the tribe of the descendants of Simeon by their family groups. Their inheritance was in the middle of the inheritance of the descendants of Judah.

### Joshua 19:2
They had for their inheritance Beersheba (or Sheba), Moladah,

### Joshua 19:3
Hazar Shual, Balah, Ezem,

### Joshua 19:4
Eltolad, Bethul, Hormah,

### Joshua 19:5
Ziklag, Beth Marcaboth, Hazar Susah,

### Joshua 19:6
Beth Lebaoth, and Sharuhen; thirteen cities with their villages;

### Joshua 19:7
Ain, Rimmon, Ether, and Ashan; four cities with their villages;

### Joshua 19:8
and all the villages that were around these cities to Baalath Beer, Ramah of the South. This is the inheritance of the tribe of the descendants of Simeon by their family groups.

### Joshua 19:9
Out of the part of the descendants of Judah was the inheritance of the descendants of Simeon; for the portion of the descendants of Judah was too much for them. Therefore the descendants of Simeon had inheritance in the middle of their inheritance.

### Joshua 19:10
The third lot came up for the children of Zebulun by their family groups. The boundary line of their inheritance was to Sarid.

### Joshua 19:11
Their boundary line went up westward, even to Maralah, and reached to Dabbesheth. It reached to the brook that is before Jokneam.

### Joshua 19:12
It turned from Sarid eastward toward the sunrise to the boundary line of Chisloth Tabor. It went out to Daberath, and went up to Japhia.

### Joshua 19:13
From there it passed along eastward to Gath Hepher, to Ethkazin, and it went out at Rimmon that stretches to Neah.

### Joshua 19:14
The boundary line turned around it on the north to Hannathon, and it ended at the valley of Iphtah El;

### Joshua 19:15
Kattath, Nahalal, Shimron, Idalah, and Bethlehem: twelve cities with their villages.

### Joshua 19:16
This is the inheritance of the children of Zebulun by their family groups, these cities with their villages.

### Joshua 19:17
The fourth lot came out for Issachar, even for the children of Issachar by their family groups.

### Joshua 19:18
Their boundary line was to Jezreel, Chesulloth, Shunem,

### Joshua 19:19
Hapharaim, Shion, Anaharath,

### Joshua 19:20
Rabbith, Kishion, Ebez,

### Joshua 19:21
Remeth, Engannim, En Haddah, and Beth Pazzez.

### Joshua 19:22
The boundary line reached to Tabor, Shahazumah, and Beth Shemesh. Their boundary line ended at the Jordan River: sixteen cities with their villages.

### Joshua 19:23
This is the inheritance of the tribe of the children of Issachar by their family groups, the cities with their villages.

### Joshua 19:24
The fifth lot came out for the tribe of the children of Asher by their family groups.

### Joshua 19:25
Their boundary line was Helkath, Hali, Beten, Achshaph,

### Joshua 19:26
Allammelech, Amad, Mishal. It reached to Carmel westward, and to Shihorlibnath.

### Joshua 19:27
It turned toward the sunrise to Beth Dagon, and reached to Zebulun, and to the valley of Iphtah El northward to Beth Emek and Neiel. It went out to Cabul on the left hand,

### Joshua 19:28
and Ebron, Rehob, Hammon, and Kanah, even to great Sidon.

### Joshua 19:29
The boundary line turned to Ramah, to the fortified city of Tyre, and the boundary line turned to Hosah. It ended at the sea by the region of Achzib;

### Joshua 19:30
Ummah also, and Aphek, and Rehob: twenty-two cities with their villages.

### Joshua 19:31
This is the inheritance of the tribe of the children of Asher by their family groups, these cities with their villages.

### Joshua 19:32
The sixth lot came out for the children of Naphtali, even for the children of Naphtali by their family groups.

### Joshua 19:33
Their boundary line was from Heleph, from the oak in Zaanannim, Adami-nekeb, and Jabneel, to Lakkum. It ended at the Jordan River.

### Joshua 19:34
The boundary line turned westward to Aznoth Tabor, and went out from there to Hukkok. It reached to Zebulun on the south, and reached to Asher on the west, and to Judah at the Jordan River toward the sunrise.

### Joshua 19:35
The fortified cities were Ziddim, Zer, Hammath, Rakkath, Chinnereth,

### Joshua 19:36
Adamah, Ramah, Hazor,

### Joshua 19:37
Kedesh, Edrei, En Hazor,

### Joshua 19:38
Iron, Migdal El, Horem, Beth Anath, and Beth Shemesh; nineteen cities with their villages.

### Joshua 19:39
This is the inheritance of the tribe of the children of Naphtali by their family groups, the cities with their villages.

### Joshua 19:40
The seventh lot came out for the tribe of the children of Dan by their family groups.

### Joshua 19:41
The boundary line of their inheritance was Zorah, Eshtaol, Irshemesh,

### Joshua 19:42
Shaalabbin, Aijalon, Ithlah,

### Joshua 19:43
Elon, Timnah, Ekron,

### Joshua 19:44
Eltekeh, Gibbethon, Baalath,

### Joshua 19:45
Jehud, Bene Berak, Gath Rimmon,

### Joshua 19:46
Me Jarkon, and Rakkon, with the boundary line opposite Joppa.

### Joshua 19:47
The boundary line of the children of Dan went out beyond them; for the children of Dan went up and fought against Leshem, and took it, and struck the city, and took it, and lived therein, and called Leshem, Dan, after the name of Dan their forefather.

### Joshua 19:48
This is the inheritance of the tribe of the children of Dan by their family groups, these cities with their villages.

### Joshua 19:49
When they finished dividing the land by its boundaries, the people of Israel gave Joshua son of Nun a share of land among them.

### Joshua 19:50
According to the Lord's commandment, they gave him the city that he asked, even Timnathserah in the hill country of Ephraim, and he built the city, and lived there.

### Joshua 19:51
These were the land shares that Eleazar the priest, Joshua son of Nun, and the heads of the family houses of Israel divided by lot at Shiloh before the Lord, at the entrance of the Tent of Meeting. So they finished dividing the land.
