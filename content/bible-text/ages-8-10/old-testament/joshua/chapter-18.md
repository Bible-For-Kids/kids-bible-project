# Joshua 18

## Book
Joshua

## Chapter
18

## Verses

### Joshua 18:1
The whole whole group of the people of Israel assembled themselves together at Shiloh, and set up the Tent of Meeting there. The land was subdued before them.

### Joshua 18:2
Seven tribes remained among the people of Israel, that had not yet divided their inheritance.

### Joshua 18:3
Joshua said to the people of Israel, "How long will you wait before going in to receive the land the Lord, the God of your ancestors, has given you?

### Joshua 18:4
Appoint for yourselves three men from each tribe. I will send them, and they must arise, walk through the land, and describe it according to their inheritance; then they must come to me.

### Joshua 18:5
They must divide it into seven portions. Judah must live in his boundary lines on the south, and the house of Joseph must live in their boundary lines on the north.

### Joshua 18:6
You must survey the land into seven parts, and bring the description here to me, and I will cast lots for you here before the Lord our God.

### Joshua 18:7
The Levites have no land share among you, because serving as priests of the Lord is their share. Gad, Reuben, and the half-tribe of Manasseh have already received their share east of the Jordan River, the land Moses, the servant of the Lord, gave them."

### Joshua 18:8
The men arose and went. Joshua commanded those who went to survey the land, saying, "Go walk through the land, survey it, and come again to me. I will cast lots for you here before the Lord in Shiloh."

### Joshua 18:9
The men went and passed through the land, and surveyed it by cities into seven portions in a book. They came to Joshua to the camp at Shiloh.

### Joshua 18:10
Joshua cast lots for them in Shiloh before the Lord. There Joshua divided the land to the people of Israel according to their divisions.

### Joshua 18:11
The lot of the tribe of the children of Benjamin came up by their family groups. The boundary line of their lot went out between the descendants of Judah and the descendants of Joseph.

### Joshua 18:12
Their boundary line on the north quarter was from the Jordan River. The boundary line went up to the side of Jericho on the north, and went up through the hill country westward. It ended at the wilderness of Beth Aven.

### Joshua 18:13
The boundary line passed along from there to Luz, to the side of Luz (also called Bethel), southward. The boundary line went down to Ataroth Addar, by the mountain that lies on the south of Beth Horon the lower.

### Joshua 18:14
The boundary line extended, and turned around on the west quarter southward, from the mountain that lies before Beth Horon southward, and ended at Kiriath Baal (also called Kiriath Jearim), a city of the descendants of Judah. This was the west quarter.

### Joshua 18:15
The south quarter was from the farthest part of Kiriath Jearim. The boundary line went out westward, and went out to the spring of the waters of Nephtoah.

### Joshua 18:16
The boundary line went down to the farthest part of the mountain that lies before the valley of the son of Hinnom, that is in the valley of Rephaim northward. It went down to the valley of Hinnom, to the side of the Jebusite southward, and went down to En Rogel.

### Joshua 18:17
It extended northward, went out at En Shemesh, and went out to Geliloth, that is opposite the ascent of Adummim. It went down to the stone of Bohan the son of Reuben.

### Joshua 18:18
It passed along to the side opposite the Arabah northward, and went down to the Arabah.

### Joshua 18:19
The boundary line passed along to the side of Beth Hoglah northward, and the boundary line ended at the north bay of the Salt Sea, at the south end of the Jordan River. This was the south boundary line.

### Joshua 18:20
The Jordan was its boundary line on the east quarter. This was the inheritance of the children of Benjamin, by the boundary lines around it, by their family groups.

### Joshua 18:21
Now the cities of the tribe of the children of Benjamin by their family groups were Jericho, Beth Hoglah, Emek Keziz,

### Joshua 18:22
Beth Arabah, Zemaraim, Bethel,

### Joshua 18:23
Avvim, Parah, Ophrah,

### Joshua 18:24
Chephar Ammoni, Ophni, and Geba; twelve cities with their villages.

### Joshua 18:25
Gibeon, Ramah, Beeroth,

### Joshua 18:26
Mizpeh, Chephirah, Mozah,

### Joshua 18:27
Rekem, Irpeel, Taralah,

### Joshua 18:28
Zelah, Eleph, the Jebusite (also called Jerusalem), Gibeath, and Kiriath; fourteen cities with their villages. This is the inheritance of the children of Benjamin by their family groups.
