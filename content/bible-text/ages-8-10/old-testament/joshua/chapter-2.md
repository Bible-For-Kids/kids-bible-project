# Joshua 2

## Book
Joshua

## Chapter
2

## Verses

### Joshua 2:1
From Shittim, Joshua son of Nun secretly sent out two men as spies. He said, "Go look over the land, especially Jericho." So they went to Jericho and came to the house of Rahab, and they stayed there.

### Joshua 2:2
Someone told the king of Jericho, "Men from the people of Israel came here tonight to spy out the land."

### Joshua 2:3
Jericho's king sent to Rahab, saying, "Bring out the men who have come to you, who have entered into your house; for they have come to spy out all the land."

### Joshua 2:4
But Rahab had taken the two men and hidden them. She said, "Yes, the men came to me, but I did not know where they were from.

### Joshua 2:5
When it was getting dark and the city gate was about to close, the men went out. I do not know where they went. Chase after them quickly. You may still catch them."

### Joshua 2:6
But Rahab had taken the spies up to the flat roof of her house. She hid them under bundles of flax stalks that were spread out there to dry.

### Joshua 2:7
The king's men chased after them on the road toward the places where people crossed the Jordan River. As soon as the pursuers went out, the city gate was shut.

### Joshua 2:8
Before the spies lay down for the night, Rahab went up to them on the roof.

### Joshua 2:9
She said, "I know the Lord has given you this land. Fear of you has fallen on us, and everyone living in the land is trembling because of you.

### Joshua 2:10
We heard how the Lord dried up the Red Sea before you when you came out of Egypt. We also heard what you did to Sihon and Og, the two Amorite kings east of the Jordan River, when you completely destroyed them.

### Joshua 2:11
When we heard it, our hearts melted with fear, and no courage was left in anyone because of you. The Lord your God is God in heaven above and on earth below.

### Joshua 2:12
Now please promise me by the Lord: I have shown kindness to you, so show kindness to my father's family. Give me a sure sign.

### Joshua 2:13
Promise that you will spare my father, my mother, my brothers, my sisters, and everyone who belongs to them. Save our lives from death."

### Joshua 2:14
The men said to her, "Our lives for yours, if you do not tell anyone what we are doing. When the Lord gives us the land, we will show you kindness and keep our promise."

### Joshua 2:15
Then Rahab let them down by a cord through the window, because her house was built into the city wall and she lived in the wall.

### Joshua 2:16
She told them, "Go to the hills, or the men chasing you will find you. Hide there for three days, until they come back. After that, you can go on your way."

### Joshua 2:17
The men said to her, "We will be free from the promise you made us swear unless you do this:

### Joshua 2:18
When we come into the land, tie this red cord in the window you used to let us down. Bring your father, your mother, your brothers, and all your father's family into your house.

### Joshua 2:19
If anyone goes outside your house into the street, his death will be his own fault. But if anyone inside the house is harmed, the blame will be on us.

### Joshua 2:20
If you tell anyone what we are doing, then we will be free from the promise you made us swear."

### Joshua 2:21
Rahab said, "Let it be just as you have said." She sent them away, and they left. Then she tied the red cord in the window.

### Joshua 2:22
They went and came to the mountain, and stayed there three days, until the pursuers had returned. The pursuers sought them all along the way, but didn't find them.

### Joshua 2:23
Then the two men returned, descended from the mountain, crossed the river, and came to Joshua the son of Nun. They told him all that had happened to them.

### Joshua 2:24
They told Joshua, "The Lord has truly given the whole land into our hands. Everyone living there is trembling because of us."
