# Joshua 1

## Book
Joshua

## Chapter
1

## Verses

### Joshua 1:1
After Moses, the servant of the Lord, died, the Lord spoke to Joshua son of Nun. Joshua had been Moses' helper.

### Joshua 1:2
The Lord said, "Moses my servant is dead. Now get up and lead all these people across the Jordan River into the land I am giving to the people of Israel.

### Joshua 1:3
Every place where your feet step will be land I have given you, just as I promised Moses.

### Joshua 1:4
Your land will stretch from the wilderness and Lebanon to the great Euphrates River, across the land of the Hittites, and all the way to the Great Sea where the sun goes down.

### Joshua 1:5
No one will be able to stand against you all the days of your life. As I was with Moses, I will be with you. I will not leave you or give you up.

### Joshua 1:6
Be strong and courageous, because you will lead this people to receive the land I swore to give their ancestors.

### Joshua 1:7
Only be strong and very courageous. Carefully do everything in the law that Moses my servant commanded you. Do not turn away from it to the right or to the left, so you may do wisely wherever you go.

### Joshua 1:8
Keep this Book of the Law on your lips. Think about it day and night, so you will carefully do everything written in it. Then your way will be blessed, and you will do wisely.

### Joshua 1:9
Have I not commanded you? Be strong and courageous. Do not be afraid or discouraged, because the Lord your God is with you wherever you go.

### Joshua 1:10
Then Joshua commanded the officers of the people, saying,

### Joshua 1:11
He told them, "Go through the camp and tell the people, 'Get your food ready. In three days you will cross the Jordan River and go in to receive the land the Lord your God is giving you.'"

### Joshua 1:12
Joshua spoke to the Reubenites, and to the Gadites, and to the half-tribe of Manasseh, saying,

### Joshua 1:13
Joshua said, "Remember what Moses, the servant of the Lord, commanded you: 'The Lord your God is giving you rest, and he has given you this land.'

### Joshua 1:14
Your wives, your little ones, and your animals may stay in the land Moses gave you east of the Jordan River. But your brave fighting men must cross over in front of your brothers and help them.

### Joshua 1:15
Keep helping them until the Lord gives your brothers rest as he has given you rest, and until they too receive the land the Lord your God is giving them. Then you may return to the land Moses gave you east of the Jordan River, toward the sunrise.

### Joshua 1:16
They answered Joshua, saying, "All that you have commanded us we will do, and wherever you send us we will go.

### Joshua 1:17
Just as we listened to Moses in all things, so will we listen to you. Only may the Lord your God be with you, as he was with Moses.

### Joshua 1:18
Anyone who rebels against your command and refuses to listen to your words must be put to death. Only be strong and courageous.
