# Exodus 29

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
29

## Verses

### Exodus 29:1
"This is what you must do to set Aaron and his sons apart to serve me as priests: take one young bull and two male sheep with nothing wrong with them."

### Exodus 29:2
"Use fine wheat flour to make bread made without yeast, cakes made without yeast mixed with oil, and thin wafers made without yeast spread with oil."

### Exodus 29:3
"Put them in one basket, and bring them in the basket along with the bull and the two male sheep."

### Exodus 29:4
"Bring Aaron and his sons to the entrance of the tent of meeting, and wash them with water."

### Exodus 29:5
"Take the clothes and dress Aaron in the long shirt, the robe of the priestly vest, the priestly vest, and the breastplate. Fasten the priestly vest on him with its woven waistband."

### Exodus 29:6
"Put the head wrap on his head, and put the holy crown on the head wrap."

### Exodus 29:7
"Then take the special anointing oil, pour it on his head, and set him apart with oil."

### Exodus 29:8
"Bring his sons and put long shirts on them."

### Exodus 29:9
"Fasten waistbands on Aaron and his sons, and put caps on them. The priesthood will belong to them by a lasting rule. In this way you must set apart Aaron and his sons."

### Exodus 29:10
"Bring the bull to the front of the tent of meeting, and Aaron and his sons must lay their hands on the bull's head."

### Exodus 29:11
"Kill the bull before the Lord at the entrance of the tent of meeting."

### Exodus 29:12
"Take some of the bull's blood and put it on the horns of the altar with your finger. Pour out the rest of the blood at the base of the altar."

### Exodus 29:13
"Take all the fat that covers the inner parts, the lobe above the liver, the two kidneys, and the fat on them, and burn them on the altar."

### Exodus 29:14
"But burn the bull's flesh, skin, and waste outside the camp. It is a sin offering."

### Exodus 29:15
"Take one male sheep, and Aaron and his sons must lay their hands on the male sheep's head."

### Exodus 29:16
"Kill the male sheep, take its blood, and splash it against all sides of the altar."

### Exodus 29:17
"Cut the male sheep into pieces, wash its inner parts and legs, and put them with its pieces and its head."

### Exodus 29:18
"Burn the whole male sheep on the altar. It is a burned offering to the Lord, a pleasing aroma, an offering made by fire to the Lord."

### Exodus 29:19
"Take the other male sheep, and Aaron and his sons must lay their hands on the male sheep's head."

### Exodus 29:20
"Kill the male sheep. Put some of its blood on Aaron's right ear tip, on the right ear tips of his sons, on the thumbs of their right hands, and on the big toes of their right feet. Splash the rest of the blood against all sides of the altar."

### Exodus 29:21
"Take some of the blood from the altar and some of the special anointing oil, and sprinkle them on Aaron and his clothes, and on his sons and their clothes. Then he, his clothes, his sons, and their clothes will be holy."

### Exodus 29:22
"From the male sheep, take the fat, the fat tail, the fat covering the inner parts, the lobe above the liver, the two kidneys and the fat on them, and the right shoulder, because this is the male sheep of setting apart."

### Exodus 29:23
"Also take one loaf of bread, one cake of bread made with oil, and one wafer from the basket of bread made without yeast before the Lord."

### Exodus 29:24
"Put all these in the hands of Aaron and his sons, and lift them before the Lord as an offering."

### Exodus 29:25
"Then take them from their hands and burn them on the altar with the burned offering. It is a pleasing aroma before the Lord, an offering made by fire to the Lord."

### Exodus 29:26
"Take the breast of Aaron's male sheep used to set them apart and lift it as an offering lifted before the Lord. It will be your portion."

### Exodus 29:27
"Set apart the breast of the offering lifted before the Lord and the shoulder of the contribution from the male sheep used to set them apart, the parts lifted before the Lord from what belongs to Aaron and his sons."

### Exodus 29:28
"This will belong to Aaron and his sons as a lasting rule from the Israelites. It is a contribution from the Israelites, taken from their peace offerings as their contribution to the Lord."

### Exodus 29:29
"Aaron's holy clothes will belong to his sons after him, so they may be set apart with oil and set apart in them."

### Exodus 29:30
"The son who becomes priest in Aaron's place must wear them for seven days when he enters the tent of meeting to serve in the Holy Place."

### Exodus 29:31
"Take the male sheep used to set them apart and boil its meat in a holy place."

### Exodus 29:32
"Aaron and his sons must eat the male sheep's meat and the bread in the basket at the entrance of the tent of meeting."

### Exodus 29:33
"They must eat the things used to make atonement for them, to set them apart. No outsider may eat them, because they are holy."

### Exodus 29:34
"If any of the meat from the setting-apart ceremony or bread is left until morning, burn what remains with fire. It must not be eaten, because it is holy."

### Exodus 29:35
"Do all these things for Aaron and his sons exactly as I have commanded you. Set them apart for seven days."

### Exodus 29:36
"Each day offer a bull as a sin offering for atonement. Purify the altar by making atonement for it, and set it apart with oil to set it apart."

### Exodus 29:37
"For seven days make atonement for the altar and set it apart. Then the altar will be most holy, and whatever touches the altar will be holy."

### Exodus 29:38
"This is what you must offer on the altar regularly: two year-old lambs each day, every day."

### Exodus 29:39
"Offer one lamb in the morning and the other lamb at twilight."

### Exodus 29:40
"With the first lamb, offer one-tenth of a dry measure of fine flour mixed with one-fourth measure of beaten olive oil, and one-fourth measure of wine as a drink offering."

### Exodus 29:41
"Offer the other lamb at twilight with the same grain offering and drink offering as in the morning. It will be a pleasing aroma, an offering made by fire to the Lord."

### Exodus 29:42
"This must be a regular burned offering throughout your generations at the entrance of the tent of meeting before the Lord. There I will meet with you and speak to you."

### Exodus 29:43
"There I will meet with the Israelites, and the place will be made holy by my glory."

### Exodus 29:44
"I will set apart the tent of meeting and the altar. I will also set apart Aaron and his sons to serve me as priests."

### Exodus 29:45
"I will live among the Israelites and be their God."

### Exodus 29:46
"They will know that I am the Lord their God, who brought them out of the land of Egypt so I could live among them. I am the Lord their God."
