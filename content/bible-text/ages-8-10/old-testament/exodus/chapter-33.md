# Exodus 33

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
33

## Verses

### Exodus 33:1
The Lord said to Moses, "Leave this place. You and the people you brought up from Egypt must go up to the land I promised to Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob, saying, 'I will give it to your descendants.'"

### Exodus 33:2
"I will send an angel before you, and I will drive out the Canaanites, Amorites, Hittites, Perizzites, Hivites, and Jebusites."

### Exodus 33:3
"Go to a land flowing with milk and honey. But I will not go up among you, because you are stubborn people, and I might destroy you on the way."

### Exodus 33:4
When the people heard this bad news, they mourned, and no one put on his jewelry.

### Exodus 33:5
The Lord had said to Moses, "Tell the people of Israel, 'You are stubborn people. If I went up among you for one moment, I might destroy you. Now take off your jewelry, and I will decide what to do with you.'"

### Exodus 33:6
So the people of Israel took off their jewelry at Mount Horeb.

### Exodus 33:7
Moses used to take the tent and set it up outside the camp, far from the camp. He called it the meeting tent. Everyone who sought the Lord went out to the meeting tent outside the camp.

### Exodus 33:8
Whenever Moses went out to the tent, all the people would rise and stand at the doors of their tents, watching Moses until he entered the tent.

### Exodus 33:9
When Moses entered the tent, the pillar of cloud would come down and stand at the door of the tent, and the Lord would speak with Moses.

### Exodus 33:10
When all the people saw the pillar of cloud standing at the tent door, each person would rise and worship at the door of his tent.

### Exodus 33:11
The Lord spoke to Moses face to face, as a man speaks with his friend. Then Moses returned to the camp, but his servant Joshua son of Nun, a young man, did not leave the tent.

### Exodus 33:12
Moses said to the Lord, "You have told me, 'Bring up this people,' but you have not told me whom you will send with me. Yet you have said, 'I know you by name, and you have found favor with me.'"

### Exodus 33:13
"Now if I have found favor with you, please show me your way, so I may know you and continue to find favor with you. Remember that this nation is your people."

### Exodus 33:14
The Lord said, "My presence will go with you, and I will give you rest."

### Exodus 33:15
Moses said to him, "If your presence does not go with us, do not take us up from here."

### Exodus 33:16
"How will it be known that I and your people have found favor with you unless you go with us? That is how I and your people will be set apart from every other people on the earth."

### Exodus 33:17
The Lord said to Moses, "I will do this thing you have spoken, because you have found favor with me, and I know you by name."

### Exodus 33:18
Moses said, "Please show me your glory."

### Exodus 33:19
The Lord said, "I will make all my goodness pass before you, and I will proclaim the name of the Lord before you. I will be gracious to whom I will be gracious, and I will show mercy to whom I will show mercy."

### Exodus 33:20
He said, "You cannot see my face, because no one can see me and live."

### Exodus 33:21
The Lord said, "There is a place beside me, and you will stand on the rock."

### Exodus 33:22
"While my glory passes by, I will put you in a cleft of the rock and cover you with my hand until I have passed by."

### Exodus 33:23
"Then I will take away my hand, and you will see my back, but my face must not be seen."
