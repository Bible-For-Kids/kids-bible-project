# Exodus 36

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
36

## Verses

### Exodus 36:1
Bezalel, Aholiab, and every skilled worker did the work for the holy place. The Lord had given them wisdom and understanding to know how to make everything He commanded.

### Exodus 36:2
Moses called Bezalel, Aholiab, and all the skilled people whose hearts the Lord had filled with wisdom, everyone who was willing to come and do the work.

### Exodus 36:3
They received from Moses all the offerings the Israelites had brought to make the holy place. The people continued bringing freewill offerings every morning.

### Exodus 36:4
All the skilled workers who were doing the work on the holy place left their work and came to Moses.

### Exodus 36:5
They said to Moses, "The people are bringing much more than enough for the work the Lord commanded us to do."

### Exodus 36:6
Moses gave an order, and it was announced through the camp: no man or woman should make any more offerings for the holy place. So the people were stopped from bringing more.

### Exodus 36:7
The materials they already had were enough for all the work, and more than enough.

### Exodus 36:8
Every skilled worker among them made the tabernacle with ten curtains of finely twisted linen, blue, purple, and red thread, with cherubim skillfully worked into them.

### Exodus 36:9
Each curtain was twenty-eight arm-lengths long and four arm-lengths wide. All the curtains had the same measurements.

### Exodus 36:10
They joined five curtains together, and they joined the other five curtains together.

### Exodus 36:11
They made blue loops on the edge of the end curtain in one set, and matching loops on the edge of the end curtain in the second set.

### Exodus 36:12
They made fifty loops on one curtain and fifty loops on the matching curtain, so the loops lined up with each other.

### Exodus 36:13
They made fifty gold clasps and joined the curtains together with them, so the tabernacle became one whole structure.

### Exodus 36:14
They made eleven curtains of goats' hair for the tent over the tabernacle.

### Exodus 36:15
Each curtain was thirty arm-lengths long and four arm-lengths wide. All eleven curtains had the same measurements.

### Exodus 36:16
They joined five curtains into one set and six curtains into another set.

### Exodus 36:17
They made fifty loops on the outer edge of one curtain set and fifty loops on the edge of the other set.

### Exodus 36:18
They made fifty bronze clasps to join the tent together as one.

### Exodus 36:19
They made a covering for the tent from male sheep skins dyed red, and an outer covering of durable hides.

### Exodus 36:20
They made upright boards of acacia wood for the tabernacle.

### Exodus 36:21
Each board was ten arm-lengths long and one and a half arm-lengths wide.

### Exodus 36:22
Each board had two pegs set parallel to each other. They made all the tabernacle boards this way.

### Exodus 36:23
They made twenty boards for the south side of the tabernacle.

### Exodus 36:24
They made forty silver bases under the twenty boards, two bases under each board for its two pegs.

### Exodus 36:25
For the north side of the tabernacle, they made twenty boards.

### Exodus 36:26
They made forty silver bases for them, two bases under each board.

### Exodus 36:27
For the back of the tabernacle, toward the west, they made six boards.

### Exodus 36:28
They made two boards for the two back corners of the tabernacle.

### Exodus 36:29
The corner boards were joined at the bottom and joined at the top with one ring. They made both corners this way.

### Exodus 36:30
There were eight boards at the back with sixteen silver bases, two bases under each board.

### Exodus 36:31
They made bars of acacia wood: five for the boards on one side of the tabernacle.

### Exodus 36:32
They made five bars for the boards on the other side, and five bars for the boards on the west side at the back.

### Exodus 36:33
They made the middle bar run through the boards from one end to the other.

### Exodus 36:34
They covered the boards with gold, made gold rings to hold the bars, and covered the bars with gold.

### Exodus 36:35
They made the curtain from blue, purple, and red thread and finely twisted linen, with cherubim skillfully worked into it.

### Exodus 36:36
They made four acacia-wood pillars for the curtain and covered them with gold. Their hooks were gold, and they make four silver bases for them.

### Exodus 36:37
They made the entrance curtain for the tabernacle from blue, purple, and red thread and finely twisted linen, using needlework.

### Exodus 36:38
They made its five pillars and hooks. They covered the tops and bands of the pillars with gold, but their five bases were bronze.
