# Exodus 40

## Book
Exodus

## Chapter
40

## Verses

### Exodus 40:1
The Lord spoke to Moses.

### Exodus 40:2
He said, "On the first day of the first month, set up the tabernacle, the tent of meeting."

### Exodus 40:3
"Place the ark of the testimony inside it, and cover the ark with the veil."

### Exodus 40:4
"Bring in the table and arrange what belongs on it. Bring in the lampstand and light its lamps."

### Exodus 40:5
"Put the gold incense altar in front of the ark of the testimony, and hang the curtain at the entrance of the tabernacle."

### Exodus 40:6
"Place the altar for burnt offerings in front of the entrance to the tabernacle, the tent of meeting."

### Exodus 40:7
"Place the basin between the tent of meeting and the altar, and put water in it."

### Exodus 40:8
"Set up the courtyard around it, and hang the curtain at the courtyard gate."

### Exodus 40:9
"Take the anointing oil and anoint the tabernacle and everything in it. Set it apart with all its furnishings, and it will be holy."

### Exodus 40:10
"Anoint the altar for burnt offerings and all its utensils. Set the altar apart, and it will be most holy."

### Exodus 40:11
"Anoint the basin and its stand, and set it apart."

### Exodus 40:12
"Bring Aaron and his sons to the entrance of the tent of meeting, and wash them with water."

### Exodus 40:13
"Dress Aaron in the holy garments. Anoint him and set him apart so he may serve Me as priest."

### Exodus 40:14
"Bring his sons and dress them in tunics."

### Exodus 40:15
"Anoint them as you anointed their father, so they may serve Me as priests. Their anointing will mark a lasting priesthood through their generations."

### Exodus 40:16
Moses did everything according to all that the Lord commanded him.

### Exodus 40:17
In the first month of the second year, on the first day of the month, the tabernacle was set up.

### Exodus 40:18
Moses set up the tabernacle. He put its bases in place, set up its boards, inserted its bars, and raised its pillars.

### Exodus 40:19
He spread the tent over the tabernacle and put the covering of the tent above it, as the Lord commanded Moses.

### Exodus 40:20
He took the testimony and placed it in the ark. He put the poles on the ark and set the mercy seat on top of it.

### Exodus 40:21
He brought the ark into the tabernacle, set up the veil, and covered the ark of the testimony, as the Lord commanded Moses.

### Exodus 40:22
He put the table in the tent of meeting on the north side of the tabernacle, outside the veil.

### Exodus 40:23
He arranged the bread on it before the Lord, as the Lord commanded Moses.

### Exodus 40:24
He put the lampstand in the tent of meeting, opposite the table, on the south side of the tabernacle.

### Exodus 40:25
He lit the lamps before the Lord, as the Lord commanded Moses.

### Exodus 40:26
He placed the gold altar in the tent of meeting in front of the veil.

### Exodus 40:27
He burned fragrant incense on it, as the Lord commanded Moses.

### Exodus 40:28
He hung the curtain at the entrance of the tabernacle.

### Exodus 40:29
He placed the altar for burnt offerings by the entrance of the tabernacle, the tent of meeting, and offered the burnt offering and grain offering on it, as the Lord commanded Moses.

### Exodus 40:30
He placed the basin between the tent of meeting and the altar and put water there for washing.

### Exodus 40:31
Moses, Aaron, and Aaron's sons washed their hands and feet there.

### Exodus 40:32
Whenever they entered the tent of meeting or came near the altar, they washed, as the Lord commanded Moses.

### Exodus 40:33
Moses set up the courtyard around the tabernacle and the altar, and hung the curtain at the courtyard gate. So Moses finished the work.

### Exodus 40:34
Then a cloud covered the tent of meeting, and the glory of the Lord filled the tabernacle.

### Exodus 40:35
Moses could not enter the tent of meeting because the cloud rested on it, and the glory of the Lord filled the tabernacle.

### Exodus 40:36
Whenever the cloud lifted from above the tabernacle, the Israelites continued their journeys.

### Exodus 40:37
If the cloud did not lift, they did not travel until the day it lifted.

### Exodus 40:38
The cloud of the Lord was over the tabernacle by day, and fire was in it by night, in the sight of all the house of Israel throughout all their journeys.
