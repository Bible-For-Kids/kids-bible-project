# Joshua Chapter 1

## Book Overview
Joshua tells how the Lord brought Israel into the promised land, kept his promises, judged wicked cities, gave Israel their land, and called his people to serve him faithfully. This chapter focuses on after Moses dies, the Lord speaks to Joshua and commands him to lead Israel across the Jordan into the land he promised.

## Important Keywords
- command: Something God tells someone to do
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Jordan: The river Israel crossed when the Lord brought them into the promised land
- Joshua: The servant God appointed to lead Israel after Moses

## Chapter Summary
After Moses dies, the Lord speaks to Joshua and commands him to lead Israel across the Jordan into the land he promised. The Lord tells Joshua to be strong and courageous, to keep the Book of the Law close, and promises to be with him as he was with Moses.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God keeps leading his people**: Moses has died, but the Lord continues his promise through Joshua.
2. **Courage comes from God being near**: Joshua can be strong because the Lord promises to be with him.
