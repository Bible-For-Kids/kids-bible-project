# Joshua Chapter 10

## Book Overview
Joshua tells how the Lord brought Israel into the promised land, kept his promises, judged wicked cities, gave Israel their land, and called his people to serve him faithfully. This chapter focuses on five Amorite kings attack Gibeon, and the Lord gives Israel victory as Joshua comes to help.

## Important Keywords
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Jericho: The first city Israel faced after crossing the Jordan
- Joshua: The servant God appointed to lead Israel after Moses
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles

## Chapter Summary
Five Amorite kings attack Gibeon, and the Lord gives Israel victory as Joshua comes to help. The Lord sends hailstones, Joshua asks for the sun and moon to stand still, and Israel defeats many southern cities.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **The Lord fights for his people**: The victory is described as the Lord giving enemies into Israel's hand.
2. **God rules creation and battle**: Even the sun and moon serve the Lord's purpose.
