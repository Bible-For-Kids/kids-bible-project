# Joshua Chapter 14

## Book Overview
Joshua tells how the Lord brought Israel into the promised land, kept his promises, judged wicked cities, gave Israel their land, and called his people to serve him faithfully. This chapter focuses on the land west of the Jordan begins to be divided, and Caleb asks Joshua for Hebron.

## Important Keywords
- blessed: Spoke good over someone or something
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Jordan: The river Israel crossed when the Lord brought them into the promised land
- Joshua: The servant God appointed to lead Israel after Moses

## Chapter Summary
The land west of the Jordan begins to be divided, and Caleb asks Joshua for Hebron. Caleb remembers the Lord's promise through Moses and trusts the Lord for strength even in old age.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Faith can stay strong for many years**: Caleb still trusts the Lord after forty-five years.
2. **God's promises are worth waiting for**: Caleb asks for what the Lord promised him.
