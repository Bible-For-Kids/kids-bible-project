# Joshua Chapter 19

## Book Overview
Joshua tells how the Lord brought Israel into the promised land, kept his promises, judged wicked cities, gave Israel their land, and called his people to serve him faithfully. This chapter focuses on the remaining tribes receive their inheritances: Simeon, Zebulun, Issachar, Asher, Naphtali, and Dan.

## Important Keywords
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Jordan: The river Israel crossed when the Lord brought them into the promised land
- Joshua: The servant God appointed to lead Israel after Moses
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles
- priest: A worship leader who served at the holy place

## Chapter Summary
The remaining tribes receive their inheritances: Simeon, Zebulun, Issachar, Asher, Naphtali, and Dan. When the land is divided, Israel gives Joshua his own city, Timnath Serah, as the Lord commanded.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Every tribe has a place**: The Lord provides land for tribe after tribe.
2. **Faithful leaders receive care too**: Joshua receives a city after serving the people.
