# Joshua Chapter 15

## Book Overview
Joshua tells how the Lord brought Israel into the promised land, kept his promises, judged wicked cities, gave Israel their land, and called his people to serve him faithfully. This chapter focuses on judah receives its land, including boundaries, cities, and villages.

## Important Keywords
- blessing: Good that comes from God
- Egypt: The land where Israel had been enslaved
- Jordan: The river Israel crossed when the Lord brought them into the promised land
- Joshua: The servant God appointed to lead Israel after Moses
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles

## Chapter Summary
Judah receives its land, including boundaries, cities, and villages. Caleb takes Hebron, Othniel captures Kiriath Sepher, and Achsah asks her father Caleb for springs of water.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God gives particular places**: The land is named in detail because the promise is concrete.
2. **Families live inside God's promise**: Caleb, Othniel, and Achsah appear within the story of Judah's inheritance.
