# Joshua Chapter 17

## Book Overview
Joshua tells how the Lord brought Israel into the promised land, kept his promises, judged wicked cities, gave Israel their land, and called his people to serve him faithfully. This chapter focuses on manasseh receives land, including an inheritance for Zelophehad's daughters because the Lord had commanded it through Moses.

## Important Keywords
- blessed: Spoke good over someone or something
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Jordan: The river Israel crossed when the Lord brought them into the promised land
- Joshua: The servant God appointed to lead Israel after Moses
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles

## Chapter Summary
Manasseh receives land, including an inheritance for Zelophehad's daughters because the Lord had commanded it through Moses. Joseph's descendants ask for more space, and Joshua tells them to clear the forested hill country.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God keeps just commands**: Zelophehad's daughters receive land as the Lord had said.
2. **God's gifts call for faithful work**: Joshua tells Joseph's descendants to use the land before them.
