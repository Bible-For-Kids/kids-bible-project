# Joshua Chapter 12

## Book Overview
Joshua tells how the Lord brought Israel into the promised land, kept his promises, judged wicked cities, gave Israel their land, and called his people to serve him faithfully. This chapter focuses on joshua 12 lists the kings defeated east and west of the Jordan.

## Important Keywords
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Jericho: The first city Israel faced after crossing the Jordan
- Jordan: The river Israel crossed when the Lord brought them into the promised land
- Joshua: The servant God appointed to lead Israel after Moses
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles

## Chapter Summary
Joshua 12 lists the kings defeated east and west of the Jordan. The list remembers the victories the Lord gave through Moses and Joshua as Israel received the promised land.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God remembers every victory**: The list of kings shows that the Lord gave real victories in real places.
2. **God keeps promises over time**: The victories under Moses and Joshua belong to one covenant story.
