# Joshua Chapter 9

## Book Overview
Joshua tells how the Lord brought Israel into the promised land, kept his promises, judged wicked cities, gave Israel their land, and called his people to serve him faithfully. This chapter focuses on the Gibeonites deceive Israel by pretending to come from a faraway land.

## Important Keywords
- altar: A special place where offerings were given to the Lord
- covenant: A serious promise relationship God makes
- cursed: Placed under judgment instead of blessing
- Egypt: The land where Israel had been enslaved
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all

## Chapter Summary
The Gibeonites deceive Israel by pretending to come from a faraway land. Israel makes a covenant without asking counsel from the Lord, and when the truth is found out, the Gibeonites are spared but assigned servant work for the altar.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God's counsel must be sought**: Israel makes a serious decision without asking the Lord.
2. **Promises must be kept**: Even after deception, Israel keeps the covenant they made.
