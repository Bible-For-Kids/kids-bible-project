# Joshua Chapter 18

## Book Overview
Joshua tells how the Lord brought Israel into the promised land, kept his promises, judged wicked cities, gave Israel their land, and called his people to serve him faithfully. This chapter focuses on the tabernacle is set up at Shiloh, and Joshua sends men to describe the land that remains.

## Important Keywords
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Jericho: The first city Israel faced after crossing the Jordan
- Jordan: The river Israel crossed when the Lord brought them into the promised land
- Joshua: The servant God appointed to lead Israel after Moses

## Chapter Summary
The tabernacle is set up at Shiloh, and Joshua sends men to describe the land that remains. Lots are cast before the Lord, and Benjamin receives its inheritance with named boundaries and cities.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Worship stands at the center**: The tent of meeting is set up as the land is divided.
2. **God orders his people's place**: The remaining land is divided before the Lord.
