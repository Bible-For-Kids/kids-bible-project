# Joshua Chapter 6

## Book Overview
Joshua tells how the Lord brought Israel into the promised land, kept his promises, judged wicked cities, gave Israel their land, and called his people to serve him faithfully. This chapter focuses on the Lord gives Joshua instructions for Jericho: Israel must march around the city, the priests must blow trumpets, and the people must shout.

## Important Keywords
- covenant: A serious promise relationship God makes
- cursed: Placed under judgment instead of blessing
- holy: Set apart for God
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- judgment: God's right decision about sin

## Chapter Summary
The Lord gives Joshua instructions for Jericho: Israel must march around the city, the priests must blow trumpets, and the people must shout. The walls fall, the city comes under judgment, and Rahab and her family are rescued as promised.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Victory belongs to the Lord**: Jericho falls because the Lord gives the city into Israel's hand.
2. **God remembers mercy in judgment**: Rahab and her family are brought safely out.
