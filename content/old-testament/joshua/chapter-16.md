# Joshua Chapter 16

## Book Overview
Joshua tells how the Lord brought Israel into the promised land, kept his promises, judged wicked cities, gave Israel their land, and called his people to serve him faithfully. This chapter focuses on the descendants of Joseph receive their inheritance, beginning with the land for Ephraim.

## Important Keywords
- Jericho: The first city Israel faced after crossing the Jordan
- Jordan: The river Israel crossed when the Lord brought them into the promised land
- wilderness: A wild, dry place away from towns and farms

## Chapter Summary
The descendants of Joseph receive their inheritance, beginning with the land for Ephraim. The chapter names boundary lines and notes that the Canaanites in Gezer were not driven out but lived among Ephraim as forced labor.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God gives land by family**: Joseph's descendants receive a real share in the promise.
2. **Incomplete obedience matters**: The note about Gezer shows a problem left unresolved.
