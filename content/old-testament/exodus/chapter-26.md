# Exodus Chapter 26

## Book Overview
Exodus tells how the Lord rescued Israel from Egypt, brought them through the wilderness, and taught them to live as his covenant people. This resource chapter supports Exodus 26 and should be reviewed before approval.

## Important Keywords
- cherubim: Mighty heavenly servants of God
- covenant: A serious promise relationship God makes
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- holy: Set apart for God
- tabernacle: The holy tent where the Lord met with Israel
