# Exodus Chapter 36

## Book Overview
Exodus tells how the Lord rescued Israel from Egypt, brought them through the wilderness, and taught them to live as his covenant people. This resource chapter supports Exodus 36 and should be reviewed before approval.

## Important Keywords
- cherubim: Mighty heavenly servants of God
- command: Something God tells someone to do
- holy: Set apart for God
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles
