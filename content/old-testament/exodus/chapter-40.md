# Exodus Chapter 40

## Book Overview
Exodus tells how the Lord rescued Israel from Egypt, brought them through the wilderness, and taught them to live as his covenant people. This resource chapter supports Exodus 40 and should be reviewed before approval.

## Important Keywords
- Aaron: Moses' brother and Israel's first high priest
- altar: A special place where offerings were given to the Lord
- covenant: A serious promise relationship God makes
- holy: Set apart for God
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
