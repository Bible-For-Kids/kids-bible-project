# Exodus Chapter 29

## Book Overview
Exodus tells how the Lord rescued Israel from Egypt, brought them through the wilderness, and taught them to live as his covenant people. This resource chapter supports Exodus 29 and should be reviewed before approval.

## Important Keywords
- Aaron: Moses' brother and Israel's first high priest
- altar: A special place where offerings were given to the Lord
- atonement: God making a way for sin to be covered and forgiven
- Egypt: The land where Israel had been enslaved
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
