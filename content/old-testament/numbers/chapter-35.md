# Numbers Chapter 35

## Book Overview
Numbers follows Israel through the wilderness, showing the people counted, tested, corrected, protected, and guided by the Lord. This resource chapter supports Numbers 35 and should be reviewed before approval.

## Important Keywords
- atonement: God making a way for sin to be covered and forgiven
- command: Something God tells someone to do
- holy: Set apart for God
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- judgment: God's right decision about sin
