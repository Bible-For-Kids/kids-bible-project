# 1 Samuel Chapter 25

## Book Overview
1 Samuel tells how the Lord answered Hannah, called Samuel, judged corrupt leadership, gave Israel a king, rejected Saul for disobedience, and began raising up David. In 1 Samuel 25, After Samuel dies, Nabal insults David, but Abigail wisely hurries to stop bloodshed. David blesses the Lord for sending Abigail, and the Lord judges Nabal.

## Important Keywords
- blessed: Spoke good over someone or something
- evil: What is wrong and against God
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul

## Chapter Summary
After Samuel dies, Nabal insults David, but Abigail wisely hurries to stop bloodshed. David blesses the Lord for sending Abigail, and the Lord judges Nabal.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Wisdom can stop harm**: Abigail acts quickly with humble words and gifts.
2. **The Lord can keep us from sin**: David sees that God used Abigail to keep him from bloodshed.
