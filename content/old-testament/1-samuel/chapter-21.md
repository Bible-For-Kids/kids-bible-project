# 1 Samuel Chapter 21

## Book Overview
1 Samuel tells how the Lord answered Hannah, called Samuel, judged corrupt leadership, gave Israel a king, rejected Saul for disobedience, and began raising up David. In 1 Samuel 21, David comes to Nob hungry and receives holy bread from Ahimelech, along with Goliath's sword. Then David flees to Gath and acts strangely because he is afraid of Achish.

## Important Keywords
- holy: Set apart for God
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul
- Saul: Israel's first king, who later disobeyed the Lord
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles
- priest: A worship leader who served at the holy place

## Chapter Summary
David comes to Nob hungry and receives holy bread from Ahimelech, along with Goliath's sword. Then David flees to Gath and acts strangely because he is afraid of Achish.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God provides in need**: David receives food while he is fleeing.
2. **Fear can make even brave people weak**: David is afraid in Gath and acts to survive.
