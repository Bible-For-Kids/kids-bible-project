# 1 Samuel Chapter 27

## Book Overview
1 Samuel tells how the Lord answered Hannah, called Samuel, judged corrupt leadership, gave Israel a king, rejected Saul for disobedience, and began raising up David. In 1 Samuel 27, David fears Saul will one day catch him, so he goes to live among the Philistines under Achish. Achish gives David Ziklag, and David stays there while fighting surrounding peoples.

## Important Keywords
- Egypt: The land where Israel had been enslaved
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul
- Saul: Israel's first king, who later disobeyed the Lord

## Chapter Summary
David fears Saul will one day catch him, so he goes to live among the Philistines under Achish. Achish gives David Ziklag, and David stays there while fighting surrounding peoples.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Fear can push complicated choices**: David moves to Philistine land because he feels unsafe.
2. **God's story continues in hard places**: Even in Ziklag, David remains part of the Lord's larger plan.
