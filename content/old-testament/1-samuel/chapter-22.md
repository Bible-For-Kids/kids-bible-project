# 1 Samuel Chapter 22

## Book Overview
1 Samuel tells how the Lord answered Hannah, called Samuel, judged corrupt leadership, gave Israel a king, rejected Saul for disobedience, and began raising up David. In 1 Samuel 22, David gathers distressed people at Adullam, but Saul grows cruel and suspicious. Doeg tells Saul about Nob, Saul orders the priests killed, and Abiathar escapes to David.

## Important Keywords
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul
- Saul: Israel's first king, who later disobeyed the Lord
- law: God's instruction for his people
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles

## Chapter Summary
David gathers distressed people at Adullam, but Saul grows cruel and suspicious. Doeg tells Saul about Nob, Saul orders the priests killed, and Abiathar escapes to David.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Unjust anger destroys**: Saul's fear and suspicion lead to terrible violence.
2. **God preserves a witness**: Abiathar escapes and comes to David.
