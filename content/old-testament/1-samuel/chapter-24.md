# 1 Samuel Chapter 24

## Book Overview
1 Samuel tells how the Lord answered Hannah, called Samuel, judged corrupt leadership, gave Israel a king, rejected Saul for disobedience, and began raising up David. In 1 Samuel 24, David finds Saul in the cave but refuses to kill the Lord's anointed king. David shows Saul the corner of the robe and asks the Lord to judge between them.

## Important Keywords
- evil: What is wrong and against God
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- judge: A leader God raised up to rescue and guide Israel
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul
- Saul: Israel's first king, who later disobeyed the Lord

## Chapter Summary
David finds Saul in the cave but refuses to kill the Lord's anointed king. David shows Saul the corner of the robe and asks the Lord to judge between them.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Do not take revenge into your own hands**: David refuses to kill Saul when he has the chance.
2. **Respect for God shapes choices**: David spares Saul because Saul is the Lord's anointed king.
