# 1 Samuel Chapter 6

## Book Overview
1 Samuel tells how the Lord answered Hannah, called Samuel, judged corrupt leadership, gave Israel a king, rejected Saul for disobedience, and began raising up David. In 1 Samuel 6, The Philistines send the ark back to Israel with guilt offerings, and the cows pull it straight toward Beth Shemesh. The people rejoice, but some treat the ark wrongly and suffer judgment.

## Important Keywords
- evil: What is wrong and against God
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- holy: Set apart for God
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Joshua: The servant God appointed to lead Israel after Moses

## Chapter Summary
The Philistines send the ark back to Israel with guilt offerings, and the cows pull it straight toward Beth Shemesh. The people rejoice, but some treat the ark wrongly and suffer judgment.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God guides even unusual events**: The cows go straight toward Israel, showing the Lord's hand.
2. **Joy still needs reverence**: The ark returns, but holy things must be honored rightly.
