# 1 Samuel Chapter 3

## Book Overview
1 Samuel tells how the Lord answered Hannah, called Samuel, judged corrupt leadership, gave Israel a king, rejected Saul for disobedience, and began raising up David. In 1 Samuel 3, The Lord calls Samuel during the night and gives him a hard message about Eli's house. Samuel tells Eli the truth, and all Israel knows that Samuel is established as a prophet of the Lord.

## Important Keywords
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- judge: A leader God raised up to rescue and guide Israel
- Eli: The priest who served at Shiloh when Samuel was a child
- Samuel: The prophet and judge whom the Lord called as a boy

## Chapter Summary
The Lord calls Samuel during the night and gives him a hard message about Eli's house. Samuel tells Eli the truth, and all Israel knows that Samuel is established as a prophet of the Lord.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God speaks truly**: Samuel learns to listen when the Lord calls.
2. **Faithful servants tell the truth**: Samuel must share a difficult message without changing it.
