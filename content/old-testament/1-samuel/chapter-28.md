# 1 Samuel Chapter 28

## Book Overview
1 Samuel tells how the Lord answered Hannah, called Samuel, judged corrupt leadership, gave Israel a king, rejected Saul for disobedience, and began raising up David. In 1 Samuel 28, The Philistines gather for war, and Saul is terrified because the Lord does not answer him. Saul seeks a forbidden woman who tries to speak with the dead, and Samuel announces that Saul and his sons will fall.

## Important Keywords
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul
- Samuel: The prophet and judge whom the Lord called as a boy
- Saul: Israel's first king, who later disobeyed the Lord

## Chapter Summary
The Philistines gather for war, and Saul is terrified because the Lord does not answer him. Saul seeks a forbidden woman who tries to speak with the dead, and Samuel announces that Saul and his sons will fall.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Forbidden guidance is not faith**: Saul turns to what the Lord had forbidden instead of repenting.
2. **The Lord's word stands**: Samuel's message confirms the judgment already spoken against Saul.
