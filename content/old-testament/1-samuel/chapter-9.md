# 1 Samuel Chapter 9

## Book Overview
1 Samuel tells how the Lord answered Hannah, called Samuel, judged corrupt leadership, gave Israel a king, rejected Saul for disobedience, and began raising up David. In 1 Samuel 9, Saul goes looking for his father's lost donkeys, but the Lord is leading him to Samuel. Samuel honors Saul at a meal and prepares to reveal the Lord's plan for him.

## Important Keywords
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Samuel: The prophet and judge whom the Lord called as a boy
- Saul: Israel's first king, who later disobeyed the Lord
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles

## Chapter Summary
Saul goes looking for his father's lost donkeys, but the Lord is leading him to Samuel. Samuel honors Saul at a meal and prepares to reveal the Lord's plan for him.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God can guide ordinary errands**: Saul is looking for donkeys, but the Lord is directing the meeting.
2. **God sees future needs**: The Lord has already told Samuel about Saul before Saul arrives.
