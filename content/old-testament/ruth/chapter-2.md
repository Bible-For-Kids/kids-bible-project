# Ruth Chapter 2

## Book Overview
Ruth tells how the Lord cared for Naomi and Ruth through loyal love, ordinary work, Boaz's kindness, and the family line that would lead to King David. In Ruth 2, Ruth goes to gather leftover grain so she and Naomi can eat. The Lord leads her to Boaz's field, where Boaz protects her, provides for her, and blesses her for taking refuge under the Lord's wings.

## Important Keywords
- blessed: Spoke good over someone or something
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Boaz: The man from Bethlehem who showed covenant kindness to Ruth and Naomi
- Naomi: Ruth's mother-in-law, who returned to Bethlehem after great loss

## Chapter Summary
Ruth goes to gather leftover grain so she and Naomi can eat. The Lord leads her to Boaz's field, where Boaz protects her, provides for her, and blesses her for taking refuge under the Lord's wings.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God can care through ordinary work**: Ruth gathers grain one handful at a time, and the Lord provides.
2. **Kindness shows God's care**: Boaz sees Ruth, protects her, and gives generously.
