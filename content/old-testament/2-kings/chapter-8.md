# 2 Kings Chapter 8

## Book Overview
2 Kings continues the story of Israel and Judah, showing Elisha's ministry, the fall of Israel, Hezekiah and Josiah's reforms, and Jerusalem's exile after the people would not listen to the Lord. In 2 Kings 8, The Shunammite woman receives her land back after famine. Elisha weeps over the harm Hazael will do, and the chapter records kings of Judah who walk in sinful ways.

## Important Keywords
- evil: What is wrong and against God
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul
- Ahab: A wicked king of Israel who worshiped Baal

## Chapter Summary
The Shunammite woman receives her land back after famine. Elisha weeps over the harm Hazael will do, and the chapter records kings of Judah who walk in sinful ways.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **The Lord can restore what was lost**: The woman receives her house and land again.
2. **God knows future sorrow**: Elisha grieves because he knows the damage Hazael will bring.
