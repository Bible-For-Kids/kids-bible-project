# 2 Kings Chapter 2

## Book Overview
2 Kings continues the story of Israel and Judah, showing Elisha's ministry, the fall of Israel, Hezekiah and Josiah's reforms, and Jerusalem's exile after the people would not listen to the Lord. In 2 Kings 2, The Lord takes Elijah up by a whirlwind, and Elisha receives Elijah's cloak and prophetic work. The Lord confirms Elisha's ministry through signs of judgment and mercy.

## Important Keywords
- cursed: Placed under judgment instead of blessing
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Jericho: The first city Israel faced after crossing the Jordan
- Jordan: The river Israel crossed when the Lord brought them into the promised land

## Chapter Summary
The Lord takes Elijah up by a whirlwind, and Elisha receives Elijah's cloak and prophetic work. The Lord confirms Elisha's ministry through signs of judgment and mercy.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God continues his work**: Elijah is taken up, but the Lord continues speaking through Elisha.
2. **The Lord gives power for his calling**: Elisha crosses the Jordan as Elijah had done.
