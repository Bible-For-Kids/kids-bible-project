# 2 Kings Chapter 4

## Book Overview
2 Kings continues the story of Israel and Judah, showing Elisha's ministry, the fall of Israel, Hezekiah and Josiah's reforms, and Jerusalem's exile after the people would not listen to the Lord. In 2 Kings 4, The Lord works through Elisha to provide oil for a widow, give a son to the Shunammite woman, restore that son to life, heal a pot of food, and feed many people.

## Important Keywords
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- holy: Set apart for God
- Baal: A false god worshiped by many Canaanites
- Elisha: The prophet who followed Elijah and spoke the Lord's word
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles

## Chapter Summary
The Lord works through Elisha to provide oil for a widow, give a son to the Shunammite woman, restore that son to life, heal a pot of food, and feed many people.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God sees needy families**: The widow and her sons receive help through the oil.
2. **The Lord gives life and provision**: The chapter shows God caring in homes, kitchens, and hungry crowds.
