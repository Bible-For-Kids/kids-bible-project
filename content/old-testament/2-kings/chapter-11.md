# 2 Kings Chapter 11

## Book Overview
2 Kings continues the story of Israel and Judah, showing Elisha's ministry, the fall of Israel, Hezekiah and Josiah's reforms, and Jerusalem's exile after the people would not listen to the Lord. In 2 Kings 11, Athaliah tries to destroy the royal family, but Joash is hidden and protected. Jehoiada brings Joash out, makes covenant before the Lord, and the people remove Baal worship.

## Important Keywords
- altar: A special place where offerings were given to the Lord
- covenant: A serious promise relationship God makes
- Baal: A false god worshiped by many Canaanites
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles

## Chapter Summary
Athaliah tries to destroy the royal family, but Joash is hidden and protected. Jehoiada brings Joash out, makes covenant before the Lord, and the people remove Baal worship.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God preserves David's line**: Joash is kept alive when the royal family is attacked.
2. **Covenant renewal changes worship**: The people promise to belong to the Lord and remove Baal worship.
