# Genesis Chapter 38

## Book Overview
Genesis 38 continues the covenant family story. Judah's family story with Tamar exposes sin, injustice, and confession while preserving the line through Perez.

## Important Keywords
- Judah: Jacob's son whose family line receives an important ruler promise
- Tamar: The woman in Judah's family story through whom Perez was born
- sin: Disobeying God and going against his good way
- confession: Telling the truth about sin or wrongdoing
- offspring: Children and later family who come after someone

## Chapter Summary
Judah's family story with Tamar exposes sin, injustice, and confession while preserving the line through Perez.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God keeps the story moving**: Even family conflict, journeys, and danger cannot stop the promises God has spoken.
2. **People need mercy and truth**: The chapter shows real human choices while keeping God as the faithful one.
3. **Details matter**: Names, places, blessings, and promises should be kept clear because they carry the story forward.
