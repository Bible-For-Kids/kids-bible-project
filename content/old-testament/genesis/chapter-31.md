# Genesis Chapter 31

## Book Overview
Genesis 31 continues the covenant family story. Jacob leaves Laban, Rachel hides household idols, Laban pursues, and the families make a covenant boundary before God.

## Important Keywords
- idols: A key word that helps explain this chapter.
- covenant: A serious promise relationship God makes
- protection: God keeping someone from harm or guarding his promise
- oath: A serious promise made before God
- journey: Travel from one place to another

## Chapter Summary
Jacob leaves Laban, Rachel hides household idols, Laban pursues, and the families make a covenant boundary before God.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God keeps the story moving**: Even family conflict, journeys, and danger cannot stop the promises God has spoken.
2. **People need mercy and truth**: The chapter shows real human choices while keeping God as the faithful one.
3. **Details matter**: Names, places, blessings, and promises should be kept clear because they carry the story forward.
