# Leviticus Chapter 9

## Book Overview
Leviticus gives Israel worship laws, holiness laws, and priestly instructions for living near the holy Lord. This resource chapter supports Leviticus 9 and should be reviewed before approval.

## Important Keywords
- Aaron: Moses' brother and Israel's first high priest
- altar: A special place where offerings were given to the Lord
- atonement: God making a way for sin to be covered and forgiven
- blessed: Spoke good over someone or something
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
