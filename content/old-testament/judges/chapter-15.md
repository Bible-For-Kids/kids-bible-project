# Judges Chapter 15

## Book Overview
Judges shows Israel turning from the Lord again and again, and the Lord raising rescuers while exposing how badly his people needed faithful covenant leadership. This chapter focuses on samson fights the Philistines after they wrong him and his wife.

## Important Keywords
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Samson: A judge given great strength, whom God used against the Philistines
- law: God's instruction for his people
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles

## Chapter Summary
Samson fights the Philistines after they wrong him and his wife. The Spirit of the Lord rushes on him, he strikes many Philistines with a donkey's jawbone, and the Lord gives him water when he cries out.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **The Lord can strengthen the weak and thirsty**: After victory, Samson still needs God to keep him alive.
2. **Sinful conflict grows destructive**: Personal revenge and Philistine cruelty lead to more violence.
