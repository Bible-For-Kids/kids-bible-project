# Judges Chapter 7

## Book Overview
Judges shows Israel turning from the Lord again and again, and the Lord raising rescuers while exposing how badly his people needed faithful covenant leadership. This chapter focuses on the Lord reduces Gideon's army so Israel cannot boast that they saved themselves.

## Important Keywords
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Jordan: The river Israel crossed when the Lord brought them into the promised land
- Gideon: A judge the Lord used to rescue Israel from Midian
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles

## Chapter Summary
The Lord reduces Gideon's army so Israel cannot boast that they saved themselves. With only three hundred men, trumpets, jars, and torches, Gideon obeys the Lord, and the Midianite camp falls into panic.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God receives the glory**: The small army shows that victory comes from the Lord.
2. **Faith obeys while afraid**: Gideon is strengthened and then acts on the Lord's word.
