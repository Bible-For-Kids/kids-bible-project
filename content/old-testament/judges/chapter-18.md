# Judges Chapter 18

## Book Overview
Judges shows Israel turning from the Lord again and again, and the Lord raising rescuers while exposing how badly his people needed faithful covenant leadership. This chapter focuses on the tribe of Dan searches for land, steals Micah's carved image, and takes his Levite priest.

## Important Keywords
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles
- Moses: The servant God used to lead Israel out of Egypt
- priest: A worship leader who served at the holy place

## Chapter Summary
The tribe of Dan searches for land, steals Micah's carved image, and takes his Levite priest. They capture Laish and set up idol worship, showing Israel's deep spiritual disorder.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Idols spread trouble**: Micah's idol becomes a snare for a whole tribe.
2. **Success is not the same as faithfulness**: Dan takes a city but sets up false worship.
