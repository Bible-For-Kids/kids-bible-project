# Judges Chapter 19

## Book Overview
Judges shows Israel turning from the Lord again and again, and the Lord raising rescuers while exposing how badly his people needed faithful covenant leadership. This chapter focuses on a Levite travels with his secondary wife, and terrible wickedness happens in Gibeah.

## Important Keywords
- Egypt: The land where Israel had been enslaved
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- law: God's instruction for his people
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles
- Yahweh: The Lord, God's covenant name in the Hebrew Scriptures

## Chapter Summary
A Levite travels with his secondary wife, and terrible wickedness happens in Gibeah. Her death shocks Israel, and the chapter shows how dark life becomes when people reject God's ways.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God's word names evil as evil**: The chapter does not pretend wickedness is normal or good.
2. **People need righteous leadership**: The horror prepares for the book's repeated warning that Israel had no king.
