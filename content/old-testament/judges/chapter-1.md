# Judges Chapter 1

## Book Overview
Judges shows Israel turning from the Lord again and again, and the Lord raising rescuers while exposing how badly his people needed faithful covenant leadership. This chapter focuses on after Joshua dies, Israel asks the Lord who should go first against the Canaanites.

## Important Keywords
- blessing: Good that comes from God
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Joshua: The servant God appointed to lead Israel after Moses
- law: God's instruction for his people

## Chapter Summary
After Joshua dies, Israel asks the Lord who should go first against the Canaanites. Judah leads, several tribes receive victories, but many tribes leave Canaanite people in the land instead of fully obeying the Lord.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God leads after leaders die**: The Lord still answers Israel after Joshua is gone.
2. **Incomplete obedience matters**: The chapter shows victories, but also warns that leaving sin in place brings trouble later.
