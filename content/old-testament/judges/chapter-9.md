# Judges Chapter 9

## Book Overview
Judges shows Israel turning from the Lord again and again, and the Lord raising rescuers while exposing how badly his people needed faithful covenant leadership. This chapter focuses on abimelech violently makes himself king after killing his brothers, but Jotham warns that treachery will bring judgment.

## Important Keywords
- cursed: Placed under judgment instead of blessing
- evil: What is wrong and against God
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Baal: A false god worshiped by many Canaanites

## Chapter Summary
Abimelech violently makes himself king after killing his brothers, but Jotham warns that treachery will bring judgment. Abimelech and Shechem destroy each other, and God repays their wickedness.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Power without righteousness destroys**: Abimelech grabs rule through violence.
2. **God sees wickedness**: The chapter says God repaid Abimelech and Shechem for evil.
