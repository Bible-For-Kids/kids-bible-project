# Judges Chapter 20

## Book Overview
Judges shows Israel turning from the Lord again and again, and the Lord raising rescuers while exposing how badly his people needed faithful covenant leadership. This chapter focuses on israel gathers to judge the evil done in Gibeah, but Benjamin refuses to hand over the guilty men.

## Chapter Summary
Israel gathers to judge the evil done in Gibeah, but Benjamin refuses to hand over the guilty men. A terrible civil war follows, and Israel seeks the Lord as many people die.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Sin can wound a whole people**: One city's wickedness becomes a national crisis.
2. **Judgment is serious and sorrowful**: Israel weeps before the Lord during the war.
