# Judges Chapter 12

## Book Overview
Judges shows Israel turning from the Lord again and again, and the Lord raising rescuers while exposing how badly his people needed faithful covenant leadership. This chapter focuses on jephthah fights with Ephraim after the Ammonite victory, and many people die in tribal conflict.

## Important Keywords
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Jordan: The river Israel crossed when the Lord brought them into the promised land
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles
- Yahweh: The Lord, God's covenant name in the Hebrew Scriptures

## Chapter Summary
Jephthah fights with Ephraim after the Ammonite victory, and many people die in tribal conflict. The chapter then briefly records Ibzan, Elon, and Abdon judging Israel.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Pride can turn brothers against brothers**: Ephraim and Gilead fight each other after a rescue.
2. **Judges were temporary leaders**: The short lists show leadership passing from one judge to another.
