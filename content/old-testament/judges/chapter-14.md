# Judges Chapter 14

## Book Overview
Judges shows Israel turning from the Lord again and again, and the Lord raising rescuers while exposing how badly his people needed faithful covenant leadership. This chapter focuses on samson goes to Timnah, kills a lion by the Spirit of the Lord, and gives a riddle at his wedding feast.

## Important Keywords
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Samson: A judge given great strength, whom God used against the Philistines
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles
- Yahweh: The Lord, God's covenant name in the Hebrew Scriptures

## Chapter Summary
Samson goes to Timnah, kills a lion by the Spirit of the Lord, and gives a riddle at his wedding feast. The Philistines pressure his wife, and Samson's anger brings conflict.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Strength is from the Lord**: Samson's power comes by the Spirit of the Lord.
2. **Desire without wisdom brings trouble**: Samson's choices lead into conflict.
