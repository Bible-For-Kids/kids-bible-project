# Judges Chapter 5

## Book Overview
Judges shows Israel turning from the Lord again and again, and the Lord raising rescuers while exposing how badly his people needed faithful covenant leadership. This chapter focuses on deborah and Barak sing a song praising the Lord for rescuing Israel.

## Important Keywords
- blessed: Spoke good over someone or something
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Jordan: The river Israel crossed when the Lord brought them into the promised land
- Deborah: A prophetess and judge who helped lead Israel

## Chapter Summary
Deborah and Barak sing a song praising the Lord for rescuing Israel. The song remembers the tribes that helped, the tribes that stayed back, the defeat of Sisera, and the blessing of those who love the Lord.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God's works should be remembered**: The song teaches Israel to remember the Lord's victory.
2. **Love for the Lord matters**: The song ends by blessing those who love him.
