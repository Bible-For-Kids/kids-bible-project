# Judges Chapter 16

## Book Overview
Judges shows Israel turning from the Lord again and again, and the Lord raising rescuers while exposing how badly his people needed faithful covenant leadership. This chapter focuses on samson is betrayed by Delilah after he tells the secret connected to his Nazirite hair.

## Important Keywords
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Samson: A judge given great strength, whom God used against the Philistines
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles
- sacrifice: An offering given in worship

## Chapter Summary
Samson is betrayed by Delilah after he tells the secret connected to his Nazirite hair. The Philistines blind him and imprison him, but the Lord hears Samson's final prayer and brings judgment on the Philistines.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Sin can make strong people weak**: Samson's choices lead to capture and suffering.
2. **The Lord still hears prayer**: Samson cries to the Lord at the end.
