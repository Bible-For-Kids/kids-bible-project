# 2 Samuel Chapter 9

## Book Overview
2 Samuel tells how the Lord established David as king, made covenant promises to his house, exposed David's sin and its sorrow, and showed both judgment and mercy. In 2 Samuel 9, David remembers his covenant with Jonathan and seeks someone from Saul's house to show kindness to. He brings Mephibosheth to his table and restores Saul's land to him.

## Important Keywords
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul
- Jonathan: Saul's son and David's faithful friend
- Saul: Israel's first king, who later disobeyed the Lord
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles

## Chapter Summary
David remembers his covenant with Jonathan and seeks someone from Saul's house to show kindness to. He brings Mephibosheth to his table and restores Saul's land to him.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Covenant love remembers promises**: David shows kindness for Jonathan's sake.
2. **Kindness lifts the weak**: Mephibosheth receives a place at the king's table.
