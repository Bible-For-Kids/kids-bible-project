# 2 Samuel Chapter 6

## Book Overview
2 Samuel tells how the Lord established David as king, made covenant promises to his house, exposed David's sin and its sorrow, and showed both judgment and mercy. In 2 Samuel 6, David brings the ark toward Jerusalem, but Uzzah dies when the ark is handled wrongly. Later David brings the ark with reverence and joy, while Michal despises David's worship.

## Important Keywords
- blessed: Spoke good over someone or something
- cherubim: Mighty heavenly servants of God
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul

## Chapter Summary
David brings the ark toward Jerusalem, but Uzzah dies when the ark is handled wrongly. Later David brings the ark with reverence and joy, while Michal despises David's worship.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Holy worship needs reverence**: The ark must be treated according to the Lord's holiness.
2. **Joy before the Lord can be humble**: David rejoices before the Lord with all his strength.
