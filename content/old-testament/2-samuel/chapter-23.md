# 2 Samuel Chapter 23

## Book Overview
2 Samuel tells how the Lord established David as king, made covenant promises to his house, exposed David's sin and its sorrow, and showed both judgment and mercy. In 2 Samuel 23, David's last words praise the God who made an everlasting covenant with him. The chapter also remembers David's mighty men and their brave deeds.

## Important Keywords
- covenant: A serious promise relationship God makes
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Jacob: The man God also named Israel
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul

## Chapter Summary
David's last words praise the God who made an everlasting covenant with him. The chapter also remembers David's mighty men and their brave deeds.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God's covenant is sure**: David rests in the everlasting covenant God made with him.
2. **Faithful courage should be remembered**: The mighty men used their strength in service of the king.
