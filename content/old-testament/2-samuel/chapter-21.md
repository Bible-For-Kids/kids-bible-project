# 2 Samuel Chapter 21

## Book Overview
2 Samuel tells how the Lord established David as king, made covenant promises to his house, exposed David's sin and its sorrow, and showed both judgment and mercy. In 2 Samuel 21, David seeks the Lord during a famine and deals with Saul's guilt against the Gibeonites. The chapter also records battles with Philistine giants and the bravery of David's servants.

## Important Keywords
- atonement: God making a way for sin to be covered and forgiven
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul
- Jonathan: Saul's son and David's faithful friend

## Chapter Summary
David seeks the Lord during a famine and deals with Saul's guilt against the Gibeonites. The chapter also records battles with Philistine giants and the bravery of David's servants.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Old wrongs still matter**: David must address Saul's sin against the Gibeonites.
2. **God can give strength through others**: David's servants help protect Israel in battle.
