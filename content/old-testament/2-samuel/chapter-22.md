# 2 Samuel Chapter 22

## Book Overview
2 Samuel tells how the Lord established David as king, made covenant promises to his house, exposed David's sin and its sorrow, and showed both judgment and mercy. In 2 Samuel 22, David sings a song of praise to the Lord, calling him rock, fortress, deliverer, refuge, and savior. David remembers the Lord's rescue, righteousness, strength, and covenant kindness.

## Important Keywords
- blessed: Spoke good over someone or something
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul
- Saul: Israel's first king, who later disobeyed the Lord
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles

## Chapter Summary
David sings a song of praise to the Lord, calling him rock, fortress, deliverer, refuge, and savior. David remembers the Lord's rescue, righteousness, strength, and covenant kindness.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **The Lord is a sure refuge**: David describes God as rock, fortress, shield, and savior.
2. **Rescue should become praise**: David answers the Lord's deliverance with worship.
