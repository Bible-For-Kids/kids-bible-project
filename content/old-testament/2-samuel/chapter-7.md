# 2 Samuel Chapter 7

## Book Overview
2 Samuel tells how the Lord established David as king, made covenant promises to his house, exposed David's sin and its sorrow, and showed both judgment and mercy. In 2 Samuel 7, David wants to build a house for the Lord, but the Lord promises to build David a house. God promises that David's offspring will have an everlasting throne.

## Important Keywords
- blessed: Spoke good over someone or something
- blessing: Good that comes from God
- Egypt: The land where Israel had been enslaved
- evil: What is wrong and against God
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all

## Chapter Summary
David wants to build a house for the Lord, but the Lord promises to build David a house. God promises that David's offspring will have an everlasting throne.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God's promises are greater than our plans**: David wants to build for God, but God promises to build David's house.
2. **The Lord keeps covenant**: God promises a kingdom and throne that point beyond David himself.
