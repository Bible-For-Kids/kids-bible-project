# 2 Samuel Chapter 17

## Book Overview
2 Samuel tells how the Lord established David as king, made covenant promises to his house, exposed David's sin and its sorrow, and showed both judgment and mercy. In 2 Samuel 17, Ahithophel and Hushai give different counsel to Absalom, and the Lord uses Hushai's counsel to defeat Ahithophel's plan. David receives warning and supplies in the wilderness.

## Important Keywords
- evil: What is wrong and against God
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Jordan: The river Israel crossed when the Lord brought them into the promised land
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul
- Absalom: David's son who rebelled against him

## Chapter Summary
Ahithophel and Hushai give different counsel to Absalom, and the Lord uses Hushai's counsel to defeat Ahithophel's plan. David receives warning and supplies in the wilderness.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **The Lord can overturn clever plans**: God defeats Ahithophel's counsel through Hushai.
2. **God provides in the wilderness**: David and his people receive food and help when they are weary.
