# 2 Samuel Chapter 8

## Book Overview
2 Samuel tells how the Lord established David as king, made covenant promises to his house, exposed David's sin and its sorrow, and showed both judgment and mercy. In 2 Samuel 8, The Lord gives David victories over surrounding enemies, and David dedicates treasures to the Lord. David rules over Israel with justice and righteousness.

## Important Keywords
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles
- Yahweh: The Lord, God's covenant name in the Hebrew Scriptures

## Chapter Summary
The Lord gives David victories over surrounding enemies, and David dedicates treasures to the Lord. David rules over Israel with justice and righteousness.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **The Lord gives victory**: David's success comes because the Lord preserves him.
2. **Good rule needs justice**: David administers justice and righteousness for his people.
