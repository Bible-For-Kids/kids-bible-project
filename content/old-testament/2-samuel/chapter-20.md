# 2 Samuel Chapter 20

## Book Overview
2 Samuel tells how the Lord established David as king, made covenant promises to his house, exposed David's sin and its sorrow, and showed both judgment and mercy. In 2 Samuel 20, Sheba leads another rebellion, and Joab kills Amasa. A wise woman of Abel helps end the crisis so the city is spared.

## Important Keywords
- evil: What is wrong and against God
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Jordan: The river Israel crossed when the Lord brought them into the promised land
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul
- Absalom: David's son who rebelled against him

## Chapter Summary
Sheba leads another rebellion, and Joab kills Amasa. A wise woman of Abel helps end the crisis so the city is spared.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **Rebellion keeps causing harm**: Sheba divides the people after Absalom's rebellion.
2. **Wisdom can protect a city**: A wise woman speaks and prevents wider destruction.
