# 1 Kings Chapter 22

## Book Overview
1 Kings tells how Solomon received wisdom and built the temple, how his heart turned away, how the kingdom divided, and how the Lord spoke through prophets like Elijah. In 1 Kings 22, Ahab wants to fight Ramoth Gilead, and Micaiah speaks the Lord's true word even when pressured to agree with false prophets. Ahab ignores the warning and dies in battle, as the Lord said.

## Important Keywords
- evil: What is wrong and against God
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- Israel: God's covenant people descended from Jacob
- Baal: A false god worshiped by many Canaanites
- David: The shepherd the Lord chose to become king after Saul

## Chapter Summary
Ahab wants to fight Ramoth Gilead, and Micaiah speaks the Lord's true word even when pressured to agree with false prophets. Ahab ignores the warning and dies in battle, as the Lord said.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God's word is true even when unpopular**: Micaiah says only what the Lord tells him.
2. **Disguises cannot hide someone from God**: Ahab goes into battle disguised, but the Lord's word still comes to pass.
