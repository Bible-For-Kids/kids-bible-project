# 1 Kings Chapter 6

## Book Overview
1 Kings tells how Solomon received wisdom and built the temple, how his heart turned away, how the kingdom divided, and how the Lord spoke through prophets like Elijah. In 1 Kings 6, Solomon builds the temple with carefully prepared rooms, woodwork, gold, and carvings. The Lord reminds Solomon that obedience matters more than the beauty of the building.

## Important Keywords
- altar: A special place where offerings were given to the Lord
- cherubim: Mighty heavenly servants of God
- covenant: A serious promise relationship God makes
- Egypt: The land where Israel had been enslaved
- holy: Set apart for God

## Chapter Summary
Solomon builds the temple with carefully prepared rooms, woodwork, gold, and carvings. The Lord reminds Solomon that obedience matters more than the beauty of the building.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God cares about obedience**: The Lord speaks about walking in his statutes while the temple is being built.
2. **Beautiful worship must honor God's word**: The temple is carefully made for the Lord, not for human pride.
