# Deuteronomy Chapter 4

## Book Overview
Deuteronomy records Moses speaking to Israel before they enter the land, calling them to remember the Lord and keep his covenant. This resource chapter supports Deuteronomy 4 and should be reviewed before approval.

## Important Keywords
- command: Something God tells someone to do
- covenant: A serious promise relationship God makes
- Egypt: The land where Israel had been enslaved
- evil: What is wrong and against God
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
