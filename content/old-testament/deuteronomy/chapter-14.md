# Deuteronomy Chapter 14

## Book Overview
Deuteronomy records Moses speaking to Israel before they enter the land, calling them to remember the Lord and keep his covenant. Chapter focus: Moses reminds Israel that they are the Lord's children and a holy people.

## Important Keywords
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- holy: Set apart for God
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles
- Yahweh: The Lord, God's covenant name in the Hebrew Scriptures

## Chapter Summary
Moses reminds Israel that they are the Lord's children and a holy people. He gives food laws that mark Israel as set apart and commands tithes that teach worship, joy, and care for Levites, foreigners, children without fathers, and widows.

## Key Lessons for Children
1. **God's people belong to him**: Israel is called the Lord's children and treasured people.
2. **Worship includes care for the weak**: The tithe provides for servants of the Lord and vulnerable neighbors.
