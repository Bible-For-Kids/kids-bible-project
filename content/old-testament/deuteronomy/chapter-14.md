# Deuteronomy Chapter 14

## Book Overview
Deuteronomy records Moses speaking to Israel before they enter the land, calling them to remember the Lord and keep his covenant. This resource chapter supports Deuteronomy 14 and should be reviewed before approval.

## Important Keywords
- God: The one true Creator and Lord over all
- holy: Set apart for God
- Lord: God's covenant name used in many English Bibles
- Yahweh: The Lord, God's covenant name in the Hebrew Scriptures
